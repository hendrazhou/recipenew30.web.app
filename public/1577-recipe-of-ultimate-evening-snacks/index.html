The ingredients needed to make Evening snacks:
  1. Get For Dough
  2. Prepare 2 cup Maida
  3. Take 2 tsp Yeasts
  4. Make ready 2 tsp Sugar
  5. Take 1/2 cup Milk
  6. Get As required Water
  7. Get For toppings
  8. Prepare 4 tbsp Pizza sauce
  9. Take 500 gm Mozzarella cheese
  10. Make ready 1/2 cup Capsicum cut into square
  11. Prepare 1/2 cup Onion cut into square
  12. Make ready 4 tsp Pizza seasoning
  13. Take 2 tsp Chilli flakes
  14. Prepare To taste Salt
  15. Make ready 250 gm Cheddar cheese

Instructions to make Evening snacks:
  1. Firstly take small ball. add milk,sugar and yeast. Keep it for 15 minutes to rise.
  2. After that take maida in bowl.add raised yeast. and mix it. now add leftover milk.mix and knead the Dough. add water too as required. knead soft dough. Keep it cover for 30 minutes.
  3. After that knead the dough again. And roll it like chapati.but it too thick compare then chapati.
  4. Now spread the pizza sauce on top. Than spread mozzarella cheese. toppings with all veggies. then spread cheddar cheese. sprinkle seasoning,salt, chilli flakes. and bake pizza for 15-20 min.
  5. Ready to eat yummy pizza at home.
