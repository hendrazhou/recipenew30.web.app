Instructions to make Salt and pepper baked crispy chicken wings:
  1. Turn oven on to highest temperature. Line your roasting tin/tray with foil for easy cleaning.
  2. Salt and pepper your wings and leave whilst oven is warming up.
  3. Cook wings 10/15 minutes on each side.
  4. When wings are ready, take out and leave whilst you make the salt and pepper seasoning.
  5. In a hot frying pan, stir fry the chilli, garlic, red pepper and add salt and pepper(a fair bit) to taste.
  6. Toss the wings in the seasoning. Finish off with the finely chopped spring onions.
  7. Pour glass of beer or preferred cold drink and enjoy.
