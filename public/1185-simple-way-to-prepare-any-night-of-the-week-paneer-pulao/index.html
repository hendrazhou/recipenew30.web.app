The ingredients needed to make Paneer Pulao:
  1. Take 2 cups basmati rice, soaked in water for 2 hrs,
  2. Prepare 1 cup paneer pcs, square cut,
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup aloo cut into very small pcs,
  4. Make ready 1 tsp oil & 3 tbsp butter,
  5. Make ready as required Whole garam masala— 2 Bari Elaichi, 1 inch dalchini pc, 5-6 laung, 8-10 kali mirch, 1 large tejpatta, 5-6 choti Elaichi,
  6. Get 1 med sized onion sliced,
  7. Make ready 1/2 tsp garlic paste,
  8. Take 1/4 tsp ginger paste,
  9. Take to taste, Salt
  10. Take 1/2 tsp black pepper powder,

Steps to make Paneer Pulao:
  1. First strain the rice, keep aside.
  2. Fry aloo pcs till golden brown, keep aside.
  3. Now take a heavy bottom wok, add oil & butter, heat it.
  4. Add whole garam masala, sauté it for a while. Add sliced onion, stir fry it till pink.
  5. Add paneer pcs, fry it till it becomes light brown.
  6. Now add salt & rice, lower the flame, stir fry it gently.
  7. Add ginger garlic paste, stir it nicely till rice grains aur separated.
  8. Sprinkle the pepper powder,
  9. Now add water accordingly, give a gentle stir, increase the flame, give a boil.
  10. Cover it with the lid, cook it at low flame. Do not stir.
  11. Let it be for 10 minutes. Rice will be cooked nicely. Open the lid after 10 minutes.
  12. Take out in a serving dish, garnish it with onion browns (optional).
  13. Serve it with any meal.
