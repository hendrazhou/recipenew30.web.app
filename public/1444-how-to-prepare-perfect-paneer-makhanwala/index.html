The ingredients needed to make Paneer Makhanwala:
  1. Prepare 250 gms Paneer (cubes)
  2. Take 2 tomato puréed
  3. Make ready 50 gms Suhana Paneer Makhanwala masala
  4. Make ready 100 ml milk
  5. Make ready 3 tbsp butter
  6. Prepare 200 ml water
  7. Get 2 tbsp oil

Steps to make Paneer Makhanwala:
  1. Mix the masala with milk and make a paste. Heat oil in a pan and add tomato purée in it. Sauté until oil oozes out (4-5 minutes)
  2. Add paste and cook for 1-2 minutes. Add water and mix well.
  3. Add Paneer cubes and butter. Cook for 5-6 minutes and gravy will be thicker. Serve hot with rice or paratha.
