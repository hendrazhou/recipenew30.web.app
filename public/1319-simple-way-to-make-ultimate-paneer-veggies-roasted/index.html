The ingredients needed to make Paneer Veggies Roasted:
  1. Make ready 50 gms low fat paneer
  2. Prepare 1 medium Capsicum
  3. Take 1 medium Tomato
  4. Take Handful Green peas
  5. Take 1 small Onion
  6. Get 1 tsp cooking oil (olive)
  7. Take as required Seasoning
  8. Prepare as per taste Rock Salt

Instructions to make Paneer Veggies Roasted:

  2. Take a fry pan and add cooking oil, once heated add onions and capsicum for a minute on low flame.
  3. Throw in veggies and stir fry another minute.
