The ingredients needed to make Stuffed Bell Peppers:
  1. Make ready 3 Large Bell Peppers
  2. Make ready 1 lb. lean ground beef
  3. Make ready 24 oz. jar spaghetti sauce
  4. Make ready 1/2 Cup grated Cheese
  5. Prepare 1 Tblsp. Parmesan Cheese
  6. Prepare 2 Cups Steamed Rice
  7. Prepare 1/3 Cup Minced Onion
  8. Get 1 tsp. salt
  9. Take 1 tsp. pepper
  10. Take 1/4 Cup Water

Steps to make Stuffed Bell Peppers:
  1. Mix meat in mixing bowl along with cheeses, rice, onion, seasonings, and 1/2 Cup sauce and set aside.
  2. Wash, then cut tops of peppers off and clean seeds and membranes from center of peppers.
  3. Stuff peppers evenly with meat mixture.
  4. Place peppers in crockpot and pour water into the bottom around peppers. Pour the remaing sauce over the top of peppers.
  5. Cook in crockpot for 3 hours on high, then 1 more hour on low.
  6. Let peppers cool a little before serving. otherwise, they fall apart getting them from the pot to the plate!
  7. These can be baked in a covered dish in a 350°oven for 1 and 1/2 hours.
