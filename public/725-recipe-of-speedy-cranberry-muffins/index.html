The ingredients needed to make Cranberry muffins:
  1. Make ready 2 cups all-purpose flour
  2. Get 2 teaspoons baking powder
  3. Prepare 1/4 teaspoon salt
  4. Take 1 cup chopped fresh cranberries
  5. Get 2/3 cup 2% reduced-fat milk
  6. Get 1/4 cup butter or stick margarine, melted
  7. Prepare 1 teaspoon grated orange rind
  8. Get 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  9. Get 1 large egg, lightly beaten
  10. Prepare as needed Cooking spray

Instructions to make Cranberry muffins:
  1. Preheat oven to 400°f.
  2. Lightly spoon flour into dry measuring cups; level with a knife. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl; stir well with a whisk. Stir in cranberries; make a well in center of mixture. Combine milk, butter, rind, vanilla, and egg; add to flour mixture, stirring just until moist.
  3. Spoon batter into 12 muffin cups coated with cooking spray. Bake at 400° for 18 minutes or until muffins spring back when touched lightly in center
  4. Remove muffins from pan immediately; place on a wire rack.
