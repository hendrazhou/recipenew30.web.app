Instructions to make Coffee cup scramble:
  1. Spray a 12 oz microwave-safe mug with cooking spray.
  2. Crack 2 eggs in the cup and microwave on high for 45 seconds then stir.
  3. Microwave until eggs are almost set. Usually another 30 to 45 seconds.
  4. Top with cheddar cheese. Salt and pepper to taste.
