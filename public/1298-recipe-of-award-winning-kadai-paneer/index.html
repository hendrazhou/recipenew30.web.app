The ingredients needed to make Kadai paneer:
  1. Get 1 tsp oil
  2. Make ready 100 gms paneer chopped
  3. Make ready 1 onion chopped
  4. Get 1 capsicum chopped
  5. Prepare 1/2 tomato chopped
  6. Make ready 1-2 red chilli whole
  7. Take 1/2 tsp kasoori methi
  8. Make ready Pinch chilli powder
  9. Get to taste Salt
  10. Prepare 1/2 tsp whole coriander or dhania
  11. Take tbsp Red gravy -2
  12. Take 1 tsp Garam masala
  13. Get 1 tsp cream
  14. Prepare 1 tsp butter
  15. Prepare 1 tsp mawa and 1/2 tsp ghee
  16. Prepare 2 tsp milk
  17. Make ready 2 tsp lemon juice
  18. Take Coriander leaves finely chopped
