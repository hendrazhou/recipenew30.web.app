The ingredients needed to make Majestic Boneless Baked Chicken Wings!:
  1. Get 2 chicken breasts
  2. Make ready 1 cup flour
  3. Get 1/3 cup milk
  4. Take Dash black pepper
  5. Take Dash salt
  6. Make ready Dash Cayanne pepper
  7. Prepare 1 egg
  8. Make ready Spray oil
Steps to make Majestic Boneless Baked Chicken Wings!:
  1. Chop the chicken breast into bite sized chunks
  2. Combine the wet ingredients in one bowl and dry ingredients in another bowl.
  3. Dip the chicken in the milk and egg then the flour mixture about 3 times to get a nice coating.
  4. Add to a greased baking sheet and then spray the cooking oil over the top of the chicken as well.
  5. Cook at 475 for about 10 minutes then broil for 2 minutes.
  6. Dip in Frank's buffalo hot sauce and serve with ranch.
