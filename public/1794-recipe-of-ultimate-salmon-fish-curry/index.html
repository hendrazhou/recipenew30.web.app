The ingredients needed to make Salmon fish curry:
  1. Prepare 500 gms salmon fish (cleaned)
  2. Take 2 chopped onions
  3. Make ready 2 tbsp ginger garlic coriander paste
  4. Take 1 tbsp coconut paste
  5. Make ready 1 tsp coriander powder
  6. Get 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  7. Make ready 2 tsp Kashmiri red chilli powder
  8. Make ready as per taste Salt
  9. Prepare as required Oil
  10. Make ready 1 tsp curry leaves
  11. Take 2-3 kokum(optional)

Instructions to make Salmon fish curry:
  1. In a bow take salmon pieces and add 1tsp ginger garlic paste, salt, turmeric powder red chilli powder and 1 tsp kokum juice. Mix well and keep for marinations.
  2. Heat 2 tbsp oil in a pan and add cumin seeds and curry leaves.After spluttering cumin seeds add chopped onions and sauté till it turns golden.
  3. Now add coconut paste along with ginger garlic coriander paste and sauté for a min on a medium flame. Now add turmeric powder and Kashmiri red chilli powder.
  4. Let the masala cook for few minutes on a medium flame now add salmon pieces like tale pieces and head pieces coat with the masala.
  5. Add little water and salt and let it cook for some time.
  6. Meanwhile heat 1 tbsp oil in a frying pan and place the marinated salmon pieces and shallow fry on a medium flame for a min. Don’t cook the fish only slightly shallow fry.
  7. Now add this shallow fried fish into the gravy and cook for 2 mins cover with the lid and cook on a medium flame.
  8. Off the gas burner and garnish with chopped coriander leaves.This salmon fish curry goes well with rice, chapati.
