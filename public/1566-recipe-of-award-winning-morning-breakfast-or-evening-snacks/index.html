The ingredients needed to make Morning breakfast or evening snacks:
  1. Prepare 2 cup beaten rice (poha)
  2. Take 1 medium chopped onion
  3. Take 1/2 cup peanut
  4. Make ready 1 tsp mustard seeds
  5. Prepare Salt as per taste
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp lemon juice
  7. Get 1 tsp sugar
  8. Make ready 1 tbsp oil
  9. Get 1 tbsp deshi ghee
  10. Make ready 6-7 curry leavs
  11. Make ready 2 green chillies chopped 1tsp turmeric powder
  12. Make ready Few coriander chopped for garnishing
  13. Prepare 1 tbsp broken kaju
  14. Take 1 medium diced potatoes

  2. Cook till potatoes cooked add drained poha. Mix properly. Turn off flame. Add lemon juice cover the lid. After 1minute open the lid garnish with chopped coriander leaves & broken Kaju.
