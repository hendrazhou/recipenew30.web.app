The ingredients needed to make Egg ham and cheese bake:
  1. Take 12 eggs
  2. Get 7 oz finely diced ham
  3. Take 1 cup chopped green bell pepper
  4. Get 1 1/2 cups finely diced white onion
  5. Get 1 tbsp cilantro shredded
  6. Make ready 1 tsp kosher salt
  7. Take 1 tsp crushed black pepper
  8. Take 1 garlic clove diced
  9. Prepare Pam cooking spray
  10. Prepare 2 cups mix blend shredded cheese
  11. Prepare 1/2 cup skin milk

Instructions to make Egg ham and cheese bake:
  1. Pre heat oven to 375
  2. Take the 12 eggs with 1/2 cup of skin milk. salt pepper cilantro peppers and onions and beat well in a mixing bowl
  3. Coat your 8 x 11 inch pan with pam cooking spray then layer the pan with the 7 oz of the diced ham cubes
  4. Pour egg mixture over the diced ham cubes inside your pan then add 2 cups of shredded cheese. Stir inside of pan once all ingredients are added together.
  5. Bake in oven for 50 mins (I used Reynolds foil to cover and cook mine so it would not over cook)
