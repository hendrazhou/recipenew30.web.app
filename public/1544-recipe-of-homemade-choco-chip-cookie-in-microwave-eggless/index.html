The ingredients needed to make Choco chip cookie in microwave eggless:
  1. Take 2 tbsp Melted butter
  2. Make ready 1 tbsp brown sugar
  3. Take 1/2 tbsp white sugar
  4. Take 1 1/2 tbsp milk
  5. Get 1/2 tsp baking soda
  6. Make ready 1/3 cup wheat / flour
  7. Prepare 1/4 cup Choco chip or chocolate
  8. Make ready Pinch salt

Steps to make Choco chip cookie in microwave eggless:
  1. Mix together the butter, white and brown sugar, salt, baking soda.
  2. Once it's well combined, add in the milk and mix till the sugar dissolves.
  3. Then add in the wheat/flour and form a cookie dough.
  4. Add in your chocolate chunks or choco chips and fold it together. Do not mix to much or the chocolate would melt
  5. Take a microwave safe dish and place a parchment paper over it and cook it for a minute
  6. Rest it for five minutes and then take a big bite and enjoy it.
