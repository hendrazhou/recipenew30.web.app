The ingredients needed to make Corndog bites:
  1. Make ready 1 pack Hotdogs
  2. Take 2 —8.5 ounces Jiffy Corn Muffin Mix
  3. Get 2 eggs
  4. Take 2/3 cup milk
  5. Take 3 Tbsp. butter, softened (I used nun stick spray)

Instructions to make Corndog bites:
  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees. - In a small mixing bowl, stir together corn muffin mix, eggs, and milk until combined.
  2. Cut each hotdog into 4 equal pieces.
  3. Next, coat a mini muffin pan well with the softened butter.
  4. Spoon about a tablespoon of corn muffin mix into each muffin tin and place a hotdog in the middle of each one.
  5. Bake in oven for approximately 7-8 minutes or until cooked through.
  6. Let cool, then dip into mustard, ketchup, or your favorite hotdog topping. - (makes approximately 30.)
