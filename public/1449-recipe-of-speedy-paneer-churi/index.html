The ingredients needed to make Paneer Churi:
  1. Prepare 1 cup Paneer
  2. Prepare 1 cup green chutney
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup Tomato sauce
  4. Prepare To taste Salt
  5. Make ready 1/2 cup corn flour power
  6. Take as needed crushed semaiya (Himalaya)
  7. Make ready as needed Oil for frying
  8. Prepare 1 tsp Chat masala

Steps to make Paneer Churi:
  1. Mix together Green chuteny & Corn flour powder,1/2 tbsp salt.
  2. Cut your paneer square shape One by One..
  3. Apply green chutney on one side of the square shape paneer and cover with another finger.
  4. Spared the crushed semaiya (himalaya) evenly on a plate.
  5. Heat sufficient oil in a kadai.deep the cottage paneer pieces in the green chuteny and roll in tha crushed semaiya.
  6. Gently slide into hot oil and deep-fry in medium hot oil till golden brown.
  7. Garnish with sprinkles remaining chat masala & Tomato sauce.
