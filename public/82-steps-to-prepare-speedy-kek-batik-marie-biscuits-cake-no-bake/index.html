The ingredients needed to make Kek Batik / Marie Biscuits Cake - NO BAKE:
  1. Get 1 row Marie Biscuits
  2. Take 1/2 can sweetened condensed milk
  3. Prepare 4 tbsp cocoa powder / milo powder
  4. Get 100 gm butter / margarine
Steps to make Kek Batik / Marie Biscuits Cake - NO BAKE:
  1. Melt butter on low heat. Add condensed milk and cocoa powder. Mix well.
  2. Add crushed marie biscuits and stir well. Turn off heat.
  3. Lay in preferred baking pan and set to cool in fridge overnight.
