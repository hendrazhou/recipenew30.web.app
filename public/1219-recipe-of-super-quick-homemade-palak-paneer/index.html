Instructions to make Palak paneer:
  1. First wash spinach and boiled them
  2. Then heat a pan take oil add onion paste, ginger & garlic paste to taste salt tomato puree,red chilli powder. Cook for 5 minutes
  3. Now grind the spinach
  4. Then take a pan add spinach puree add garam masala
  5. Then cut the paneer in small pieces and fry in oil
  6. Now add in paneer in the gravy cook for 5 minutes
  7. Palak paneer is ready. Serve wit rice or chapati.
