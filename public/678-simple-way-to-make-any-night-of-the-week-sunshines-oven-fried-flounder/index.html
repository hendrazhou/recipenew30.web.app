The ingredients needed to make sunshines oven fried flounder:
  1. Prepare 1/4 cup cornmeal (nit self-rising or cornmeal mix)
  2. Get 1/4 cup dry breadcrumbs
  3. Take 1/2 tsp dried dill
  4. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  5. Take 1/8 tsp pepper
  6. Take 1/2 tsp paprika
  7. Take 4 6.oz flounder
  8. Make ready 1/3 cup milk
  9. Prepare 1 cooking spray
  10. Take 3 tbsp butter melted
