The ingredients needed to make Cheesy Garlic Salmon:
  1. Make ready 500 g pink salmon
  2. Prepare 100 g cream cheese/softened
  3. Get 80 g mayonnise
  4. Get 1 head garlic
  5. Make ready 3 pcs calamansi
  6. Prepare Salt and pepper
  7. Get 40 g parmesan cheese
  8. Get slices cheddar cheese
  9. Prepare fine bread crumbs

Instructions to make Cheesy Garlic Salmon:
  1. Preheat oven 350°F.
  2. Season salmon with salt, pepper and calamansi juice. Let sit for 5mins.
  3. In a bowl, combine cream cheese, mayo, garlic and half of parmesan cheese. Mix well. Add salt and pepper if desired. (be mindful of the salt as parmesan can be salty already)
  4. Top the cream cheese mixture on the seasoned salmon. Spread evenly.
  5. Sprinkle remaining parmesan cheese and bread crumbs. Top it with cheddar cheese slices
  6. Cover with foil and cook for 20mins, plus another 10mins uncovered.
