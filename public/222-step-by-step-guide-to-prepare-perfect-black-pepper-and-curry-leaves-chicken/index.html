The ingredients needed to make Black pepper and curry leaves chicken:
  1. Get 4 chicken leg pieces
  2. Take 15 curry leaves
  3. Get 2 tbsp oil
  4. Take 3 tbsp curd
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp black pepper powder
  6. Prepare To taste salt
  7. Take 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  8. Prepare 1 onion (finely chopped)
  9. Get 1 tsp ginger paste
  10. Prepare 1 tsp garlic paste
  11. Get 2 cardamom
  12. Prepare 2 cloves
  13. Take 1/2 " cinnamon
  14. Get 1/2 tsp cumin seeds

Instructions to make Black pepper and curry leaves chicken:
  1. First add beaten curd, 1 tbsp black pepper,1/2 tbsp oil,5 curry leaves to chicken pieces and marinate for 2 hours. Add rest of oil into pan. Add cumin seeds, cardamom, cinnamon, clove. Add chopped onion. Add salt. After 2 minutes add ginger garlic paste. Saute until onion will be cooked well.
  2. Add marinated chicken.Add salt Cook in low flame for 10 minutes. Add cumin powder, rest of curry leaves. Add 1 tbsp black pepper powder.Cook for 5 minutes. Add water. Cook until the gravy will thick. Serve with roti or fried rice.
