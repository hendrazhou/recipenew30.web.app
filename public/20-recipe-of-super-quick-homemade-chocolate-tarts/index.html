The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Tarts:
  1. Make ready For Crust-
  2. Get 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  3. Prepare 2 cups cake flour
  4. Make ready 1 tbsp water
  5. Prepare 1 1/2 sticks unsalted butter
  6. Take For Chocolate sauce:
  7. Take 1 cup powdered sugar
  8. Get 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  9. Take 2 tsp vanilla essence
  10. Make ready 4 tbsp milk

Steps to make Chocolate Tarts:
  1. For the crust-
  2. Preheat the oven to 350°C.
  3. Combine in a food processor, the confectioners' sugar, flour, butter, water and process until the mixture forms a ball.
  4. Using your fingers, press the dough into a 12-inch tart pan with a removable bottom. Pat until the crust is even.
  5. Bake for 15 to 23 minutes, until very lightly browned. Set aside to cool.
  6. For the filling:
  7. Firstly, mix powdered sugar, cocoa powder, vanilla extract and milk.
  8. Stir well till the mixture forms smooth and creamy.
  9. Pour into the tart shell and refrigerate for 2 to 4 hours to set or overnight.
  10. Lightly sprinkle salt flakes, if desired. Serve.
