  2. Get 3 medium size onions
  3. Prepare 4 tomatoes
  4. Prepare 1 tsp ginger paste
  5. Take 1 tsp garlic paste
  6. Prepare 12 cashews
  7. Take 3 cardamom seeds
  8. Get 3 cloves
  9. Get 1 cinnamon stick
  10. Prepare 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  11. Take 2 bay leaves
  12. Take 3 tbsp butter
  13. Prepare 4 tsp oil
  14. Make ready 1/4 tsp roasted kasuri methi
  15. Make ready 1 tsp Kashmiri red chilli powder
  16. Prepare 1 &1/2 tsp coriander powder
  17. Prepare 1 tsp cumin powder
  18. Make ready 1 tsp turmeric powder
  19. Make ready 1/2 tsp garam masala powder
  20. Make ready 2 tsp fresh cream
  21. Make ready 1 tsp honey
  22. Take Salt as per taste
