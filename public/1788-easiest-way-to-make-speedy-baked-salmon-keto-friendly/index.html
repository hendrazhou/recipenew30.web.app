The ingredients needed to make Baked Salmon (keto friendly):
  1. Get 2 lbs salmon
  2. Get 1 lemon
  3. Take 2 Tbsp Sea Salt
  4. Get 2 Tbsp Pepper
  5. Make ready Drizzle Olive oil
  6. Take 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  7. Make ready 1 Tbsp Thyme/ basil/rosemary (fresh would be even better)

It's the perfect simple and healthy dinner recipe. Keto salmon recipe is a very tasty and healthy type of fish. Salmon is used in many diets, even in the ketogenic diet. This baked salmon recipe uses dry aromatic herbs because of their stronger scent.

Steps to make Baked Salmon (keto friendly):
  1. Heat oven to 375°. Coat salmon with rosemary/basil/thyme, salt and pepper. Add olive oil and rub into salmon. Add garlic. Cut lemon into slices and add slices on top of salmon (as many slices as you desire).
  2. Wrap salmon in foil and place in oven. Let cook for 20-30 minutes.
