  5. Get 1 1/2 tsp granulated garlic

Steps to make Ain't No Thang Pork Chops and Rice:
  1. Gather your spices.
  2. Add your spices and flour to a gallon storage bag. Mix well.
  3. In a large pan heat approximately 2 Tbsp of olive oil on medium high heat.
  4. Get out your pork chops.
  5. Dredge your pork chops one at a time in the flour and spice mixture.
  6. Brown pork chops well on each side two at a time.
  7. Have your soup available and open one can.
  8. While you are browning your first two chops; empty half of a can of soup into the slow cooker.
  9. Lay down your first set of two chops.
  10. Layer your chops with half a can of soup until you run out of both.
  11. At this point you cover and cook your chops on LOW in your slow cooker for 5 hrs or on HIGH for 2 1/2 hrs. (No Peeking)
  12. Serve it up!
