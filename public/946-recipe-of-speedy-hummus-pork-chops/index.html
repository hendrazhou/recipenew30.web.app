The ingredients needed to make Hummus Pork Chops:
  1. Take 3 pork chops (bone-in or boneless)
  2. Take 3 heaping tablespoons roasted red pepper hummus
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp toasted sesame seeds
  4. Prepare 2 tsp allspice
  5. Make ready 2 tsp onion powder
  6. Take 2 tsp garlic powder
  7. Take 1 tsp ground white pepper
  8. Take 1 sesame oil; as needed
  9. Prepare 1 kosher salt and black pepper; to taste
Instructions to make Hummus Pork Chops:
  1. Coat chops with enough oil to cover. Season. Bake at 400° for approximately 15 minutes or until pork reaches desired doneness. Top with hummus. Garnish with extra toasted sesame seeds.
  2. Variations; Maple syrup, honey, orher flavored hummus, cardamom, pumpkin pie spice, clove, cinnamon, nutmeg, sumac, crushed pepper flakes, parsely, lemon, turmeric, black pepper
