The ingredients needed to make A1 Baked Pork Chops:
  1. Get 3 pork chops
  2. Prepare 3/4 cup worcestershire sauce
  3. Take 1/3 cup rice vinegar
  4. Prepare 2 tsp lawrys seasoning salt
  5. Get 2 tsp garlic powder
  6. Take 1 tsp onion powder
  7. Get 1 tsp paprika
  8. Get 1/2 tsp ground celery seed
  9. Prepare 1/3 tsp ground white pepper
  10. Make ready 1 pinch salt
  11. Prepare 1 vegetable oil; as needed
  12. Take 1 A1 sauce; as needed
Steps to make A1 Baked Pork Chops:
  1. Marinate pork in Worcestershire and vinegar for one hour. Pat dry.
  2. Coat pork with enough oil to cover. Season with dried spices. Bake at 300° for approximately 20-25 minutes or until desired doneness. Brush with A1 during last five minutes of cooking
  3. Variations; H1 sauce, soy sauce, hoisin, scallions, ginger, horseradish, parsley, habanero, ground coriander seed, lemon, lime, wood chips, liquid smoke, malt vinegar, tamarind, bourbon
