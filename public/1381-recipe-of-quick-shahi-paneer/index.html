The ingredients needed to make Shahi Paneer:
  1. Take 200 gms paneer
  2. Get 5 to 6 roughly chopped tomatoes
  3. Make ready 2 to 3 chopped green chillies
  4. Make ready 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  5. Take 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp garam masala
  7. Prepare As per taste salt
  8. Prepare 3 to 4 tsp malai
  9. Make ready 1/2 cup milk
  10. Prepare 2 Cardamoms
  11. Prepare 2 cloves
  12. Make ready 1/2 tsp cumin seeds

Instructions to make Shahi Paneer:
  1. Take a kadai add ghee, cumin seeds, cloves, cardamom.
  2. Now add chopped tomatoes, green chili fry for 5 mins.
  3. Add all dry spices and cook for 2 to 3 mins.
  4. Cool, it then mix and then grind it.
  5. In a kadai add tomatoes puree and than add malai.
  6. Now add milk and paneer cook for 2 to 3 mins. Add coriander leaves.
  7. Now paneer gravy is ready for serve.
