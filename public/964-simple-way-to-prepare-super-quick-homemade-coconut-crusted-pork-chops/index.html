The ingredients needed to make Coconut Crusted Pork Chops:
  1. Make ready 2 boneless pork chops
  2. Make ready 2/3 cup shredded coconut
  3. Make ready 2 tbsp coconut oil
  4. Prepare 2 tsp onion powder
  5. Prepare 1 tsp garlic powder
  6. Get 1 tsp ground white pepper
  7. Prepare 1 kosher salt; to taste
Instructions to make Coconut Crusted Pork Chops:
  1. Gently heat 1 tablespoon coconut oil enough to melt. Coat pork chops. Season. Bake at 350° for approximately 25 minutes or until desired doneness.
  2. While chops are cooking, gently heat 1 tablespoon coconut oil until melted. Mix with shredded coconut. Press atop chops during last 10 minutes of cooking.
