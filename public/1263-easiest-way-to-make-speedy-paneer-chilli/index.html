The ingredients needed to make Paneer chilli:
  1. Prepare 250 gms paneer
  2. Make ready 1 onion
  3. Get 1 capsicum
  4. Get as required salt
  5. Take 1 cup paneer chilli masala
  6. Get 5 tsp corn flour
  7. Get 1/2 tsp red colour
  8. Make ready 4 tsp tomato sauce

Steps to make Paneer chilli:
  1. First cut paneer in pieces.
  2. Then take a bowl and put corn flour, salt, red colour, tomato sauce and mix it well.
  3. Then dip the paneer and fry it.
  4. Then take water and put paneer chilli masala and mix it well.
  5. Then take a pan put oil, onion, capsicum an keep for 2 mins.
  6. Then put paneer chilli masala water in it and keep for 2 mins.
  7. Then put paneer in it an keep for 2 mins and your yummy and tasty paneer chilli is ready to serve.
