The ingredients needed to make Paneer Bhurji:
  1. Make ready 250 g paneer
  2. Make ready 1 onion chopped
  3. Take 1 small tomato chopped
  4. Make ready 2 green chillies chopped
  5. Get 1 tbsp oil
  6. Make ready Spices (1/2 tsp chilly powder, 1/2 tsp coriander powder, 1/4 tsp turmeric powder, 1/4 tsp Garam Masala)
  7. Prepare Salt as required
  8. Take Mustard seeds and cumin seeds to temper

Steps to make Paneer Bhurji:
  1. Grate/scramble the paneer and keep aside.
  2. Heat a pan with oil and temper with some mustard seeds and cumin seeds.
  3. Add onion and green chillies. Saute until onion becomes glassy. Add tomato and sauté until it becomes soft. Add spice powders, salt and sauté until raw smell goes.
  4. Add paneer and cook it for just 1-2 mins in low to medium flame.
