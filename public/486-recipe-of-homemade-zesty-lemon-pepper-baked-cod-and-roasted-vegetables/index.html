Steps to make Zesty lemon pepper baked Cod and roasted vegetables:
  1. Preheat oven 425
  2. Quarter vegetables and 1 lemon, arrange on baking sheet.
  3. Sprinkle with olive oil, minced garlic, salt & pepper. Toss.
  4. Lay Cod filets on top of veg.
  5. Zest lemon. Mix with panko, dill, salt and pepper. Spoon mixture on top of fish.
  6. Bake for 10-15 minutes
