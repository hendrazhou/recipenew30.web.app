The ingredients needed to make Smothered Pork Chops:
  1. Get 1 packages pork chops
  2. Prepare 1 chopped onion
  3. Get 1 Bell pepper
  4. Get pinch season salt
  5. Get pinch black pepper
  6. Prepare 1 stick butter
  7. Prepare 2 cup milk
  8. Take 1 tsp flour

Instructions to make Smothered Pork Chops:
  1. Season your pork chops with season salt and black pepper then pan fry them for 45 minutes
  2. After their done cooking Sautee your onions and bell peppers in a separate pan with a little butter until they get soft
  3. To make your sauce take the pan that you fried your pork chops with and a little flour and make a rue then add your milk and stir till it thickens up and have no lumps then add a little seasoning salt and pepper for taste
