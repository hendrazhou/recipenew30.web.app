The ingredients needed to make Palak paneer:
  1. Make ready 2 bunch small of spinach chopped
  2. Get 1 tsp ginger chopped
  3. Take 1 tsp garlic chopped
  4. Make ready 1/2tsp Salt to taste
  5. Get 1-2 green chilli chopped
  6. Get 1 big tomato puree
  7. Get 1 onion chopped
  8. Take 1 tbs wheat flour or maize powder
  9. Get 1 tsp red chilli powder kashmiri
  10. Prepare 2 tbs amul fresh cream
  11. Make ready 1 tsp garam masala
  12. Take 2 tsp butter
  13. Take 1 TSP oil
  14. Prepare 1 packet of amul paneer

Instructions to make Palak paneer:
  1. First wash and clean spinach and cut it roughly! And assemble the all ingredients ina plate.
  2. Add spinach in to a pressure cooker add salt green chilli ginger. And garlicandclose the lid.Whistle it two and cool it.
  3. At grinding time add 1 the wheat flour and grind it nicely the extra water don't throw wewill use in the palak to adjust the consistency.
  4. Now. Take a wok add butter and little oil add onion saute till little brown add tomato puree a the saute till leaves the oil.
  5. Soak paneer in little hot water so that it becomes so soft add paneer into the palak and add pinch of sugar in it.
  6. Check the consistency as you want by adding excess water ofboiled spinach. Now off the gas add amul fresh cream. In It an enjoy this delicious palak paneer with roti pratha and nan.
