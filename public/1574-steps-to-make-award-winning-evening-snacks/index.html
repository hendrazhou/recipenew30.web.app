The ingredients needed to make Evening snacks:
  1. Take For Dough
  2. Take 1 cup Wheat flour
  3. Prepare 4 tsp Oil
  4. Prepare Water as required
  5. Prepare to taste Salt
  6. Take For filling
  7. Get 1 cup Chopped onion
  8. Make ready 1/2 cup Chopped capsicum
  9. Prepare 1/4 cup Chopped carrots
  10. Take 1/4 cup Chopped cabbage
  11. Make ready 2 tsp Green chilli chopped
  12. Take 1 tsp Chilli flakes
  13. Prepare 2 tsp Black pepper powder
  14. Get 2 tsp Pizza seasoning
  15. Make ready to taste Salt
  16. Prepare 1 cup Mozzarella cheese
  17. Get 1 tsp Oregano
  18. Get As needed Oil for frying or u can bake in otg

Instructions to make Evening snacks:
  1. Firstly take large bowl. put flour. add oil,salt and mix it well with hand. make Crumble. now add water little by little. and make a tight dough. keep it cover to rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile we will prepare filling. take a Pan. add oil. when oil is hot then add green chilli, onion. saute on high flame.now add carrot, cabbage, capsicum. mix and cook for 2 min. now add chilli flakes, salt, pizza seasoning, black pepper, oregano. mix it well. now add cheese. mix everything well. switch off flame. Filling is ready.
  3. Now take a portion of Dough. roll it into round shape. nor thin nor thick. then cut into square shape with help of knife. after that put the 2 tsp of filling on one side.and fold it other side.then press outer with finger. and seal gently. u can use fork also. now make all puff like this.
  4. Now heat oil in deep fry pan. and fry pizza puff on medium flame for 5-14 minutes. untill become crisp.u can bake in oven also. ready to serve crispy Mac pizza puff🤩😊
