The ingredients needed to make Kadhai Paneer:
  1. Take 2 onions diced
  2. Take 1 capsicum diced
  3. Take 1 tomato diced
  4. Make ready 250 gms Paneer cubes
  5. Take 3 medium onions sliced
  6. Make ready 4 tomatoes chopped
  7. Prepare 2 tbsp fresh cream
  8. Take 1 cup milk
  9. Get 2 tsp kashmiri red chilli powder
  10. Take 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  11. Make ready 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  12. Prepare 1 tsp garam masala
  13. Make ready 1 tsp kasoori methi
  14. Get As required Freshly grinned masala
  15. Prepare 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  16. Make ready As required Oil
  17. Prepare 2 tbsp butter
  18. Make ready For fresh masala:
  19. Make ready 1 tsp coriander seeds
  20. Get 1 tsp cumin seeds
  21. Take 8-10 pepper corns
  22. Make ready 2 bay leaves
  23. Prepare 1 piece cinnamon
Steps to make Kadhai Paneer:
  1. Dry roast all whole spices cool it and grind it.
  2. Heat oil in pan fry Paneer little light in pink color keep aside.
  3. Fry diced onion.
  4. Fry capsicum.
  5. Fry tomato diced.
  6. Heat oil in pan,. add sliced onions.
  7. Till light golden in colour add ginger garlic paste add chopped tomatoes add salt and cook till tomatoes mashy cool it.
  8. Grind it add little milk to make smooth paste.
  9. Add butter in pan add grinder mixture and cook for 10/15 minutes on low flame stir in between to avoid stick on bottom.
  10. Add milk to adjust consistency semi thick.
  11. Add dry spices red chilli powder turmeric powder coriander powder salt garam masala whole masala grinded kasoori methi mix it.
  12. Add fried Paneer and vegetables mix it properly and add fresh cream and serve with roti paratha or mein'am enjoy it.
