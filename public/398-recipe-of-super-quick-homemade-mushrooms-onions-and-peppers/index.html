The ingredients needed to make Mushrooms, Onions, and Peppers:
  1. Take 24 ounces white mushrooms
  2. Make ready 1 large red bell pepper
  3. Prepare 1 large green bell pepper
  4. Take 1 large onion
  5. Take 1/2 stick butter
  6. Make ready 2 cloves garlic sliced
  7. Get 1/2 teaspoon seasoned salt
  8. Prepare 1 cup beef broth

Instructions to make Mushrooms, Onions, and Peppers:
  1. Slice the mushrooms. Thinly slice the onion. Slice the bell peppers. Slice the garlic.
  2. Heat a skillet add the butter with the garlic. When the butter melts add the mushrooms, onions, and peppers.
  3. After the mixture has Sauteed for 5 minutes add the seasoned salt and beef broth.
  4. After the salt has been added reduce for 25 minutes. Serve I hope you enjoy!!
