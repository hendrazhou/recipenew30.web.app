The ingredients needed to make Zesty Pork Chops:
  1. Make ready 2 tsp salt
  2. Get 1 tsp black pepper
  3. Get 1 tsp Paprika
  4. Make ready 1 tsp Onion Powder
  5. Make ready Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  6. Get 4 boneless pork chops
Instructions to make Zesty Pork Chops:
  1. Preheat the oven at 400°. Mix all 4 ingredients for the rub in a small bowl
  2. Cover the porkchops with the olive oil and rub the seasoning over the top of it on both sides
  3. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until the internal temperature is 145°
