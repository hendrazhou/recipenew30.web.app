The ingredients needed to make Palak paneer:
  1. Make ready 100 gm paneer pieces
  2. Prepare 2 cup boil palak
  3. Get 2 onins chopped
  4. Make ready 3 tomato puree
  5. Get 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  6. Get 1 tsp jeera
  7. Get 1 tsp red chilli powder
  8. Prepare 1 tsp coriander powder
  9. Prepare 1/2 TSP turmeric
  10. Take 1 tsp garam masala
  11. Get to taste Salt
  12. Make ready 2 tbsp oil
  13. Get 2 tbsp cream
