The ingredients needed to make Palak Paneer:
  1. Make ready For the gravy
  2. Make ready 3 cups palak; chopped, blanched and pureed
  3. Make ready 1 medium sized onion, finely chopped
  4. Take 1 medium sized tomato, finely chopped or pureed
  5. Prepare 3/4 tsp salt (or as desired, spinach requires less salt)
  6. Take 1/4 tsp Cumin
  7. Get 1 tsp ginger, garlic paste (less ginger)
  8. Take 1 small green chilli, finely chopped
  9. Make ready 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  10. Take 1/4 tsp chat masala
  11. Make ready 1/4 tsp garam masala
  12. Get 1/4 tsp kasuri methi
  13. Take 2 tsp cream (or butter and milk)
  14. Make ready For the paneer
  15. Take 1/2 cup paneer
  16. Prepare 1 tsp oil

Instructions to make Palak Paneer:
  1. For the paneer, saute paneer cubes in oil and set aside once done.
  2. For the gravy, add 2 tsp olive oil; add 1/4 tsp cumin seeds when hot.
  3. Once they crackle, add onions and saute till translucent (not brown).
  4. Add the ginger, garlic and chilli; cook till the raw smell is gone.
  5. Add tomatoes and cook till mushy (if using chopped tomatoes) and till oil starts to separate.
  6. Add salt, red chilli powder, garam masala.
  7. Add 1-2 tsp water to prevent the spices from burning.
  8. Add spinach puree and cook for 3-5 min.
  9. Add paneer cubes.
  10. Add kasuri methi and chat masala; boil well.
  11. Add cream (or butter and milk) and mix well; don't cook for too long after adding the cream.
  12. Serve hot with paratha or rice!
