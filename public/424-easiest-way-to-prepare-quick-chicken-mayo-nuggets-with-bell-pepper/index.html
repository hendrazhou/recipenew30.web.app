The ingredients needed to make Chicken Mayo Nuggets with Bell Pepper:
  1. Make ready 250 gr ground chicken
  2. Get 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  3. Make ready 1 smallish egg
  4. Make ready 1 red bell pepper, grated or pulsed in food processor
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  7. Prepare 4 tbsp katakuriko (or you can substitute with cornflour)
  8. Take Breadcrumbs
  9. Get Oil for frying
Steps to make Chicken Mayo Nuggets with Bell Pepper:
  1. Combine ground chicken with mayo, egg, bell pepper, salt, garlic powder, and katakuriko. Put in the fridge for at least an hour (or overnight, in my case).
  2. The mixture is not too firm, so be careful when shaping the nuggets. Spoon the mixture to breadcrumbs. Use another spoon to coat it evenly.
  3. Fry in medium heat and flip after three minutes or so.
