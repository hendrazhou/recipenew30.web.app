The ingredients needed to make Paneer Tikka:
  1. Get 10-15 big square cut Paneer, capsicum and onion
  2. Take 3 spoon thick curd
  3. Prepare 2 tsp Kashmiri red chilli powder
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp garam masala
  5. Get 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  6. Make ready 1 tsp coriander powder
  7. Get 1 tsp jeera powder
  8. Make ready 3 spoon besan
  9. Prepare 1 spoon grated ginger garlic
  10. Get 2 spoon mustard oil
  11. Get Salt as per taste
  12. Get as needed Butter

Steps to make Paneer Tikka:
  1. In a bowl take 3spoon thick curd. Add 2tsp Kashmiri red chilli powder, 1/2tsp garam masala, 1/2tsp turmeric powder, 1tsp coriander powder, 1tsp jeera powder, 3 spoon besan, 1 spoon grated ginger garlic, 2 spoon mustard oil, salt as per taste and mix well.
  2. In the paste add 10-15 big square cut Paneer, capsicum and onion. Mix well and let it marinate for 15mins
  3. Now arrange marinated paneer, capsicum and onion in a barbecue stick.
  4. On a pan spread butter and place the prepared barbecue sticks to cook. Flip over in between to cook from all sides. Serve hot with mint chutney üòÄüëç
