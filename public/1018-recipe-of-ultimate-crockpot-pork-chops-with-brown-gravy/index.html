Steps to make Crockpot pork chops with brown gravy:
  1. Season pork chops on both sides.can use any kind of season you like.As you see in pic I used pork rub and garlic salt
  2. Lightly brown pork chops in skillet
  3. Meanwhile cook your brown gravy..Follow directions on the package
  4. When pork chops or lightly browned on both sides place them in the Crockpot.
  5. Pour your brown gravy on top of the pork chops.If needed can add water.Enough to cover the top of the pork chops.
  6. Let cook on low for 8hrs.
  7. Last but least serve over mashed potatoes or steamed rice.
