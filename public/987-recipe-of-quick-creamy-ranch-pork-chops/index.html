The ingredients needed to make Creamy Ranch Pork Chops:
  1. Get 2 lb boneless pork chops
  2. Take 2 tbsp olive oil
  3. Take 1 can family size cream of mushroom soup
  4. Make ready 1/2 can milk
  5. Get 1 can mushrooms
  6. Take 1 envelope ranch seasoning mix
  7. Make ready salt and pepper
  8. Get garlic powder, seasoning salt, pepper
  9. Take 1 medium onion, chopped

Instructions to make Creamy Ranch Pork Chops:
  1. Season pork chops with salt and pepper. Brown in oil in skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Transfer pork chops to crock pot. Add remaining ingredients to skillet and heat through, scraping pan. Pour over chops and cook on low for 6 hours.
  3. Serve with rice or pasta and enjoy!
