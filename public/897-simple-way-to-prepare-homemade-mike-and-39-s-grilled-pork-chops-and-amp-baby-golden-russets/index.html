The ingredients needed to make Mike's Grilled Pork Chops & Baby Golden Russets:
  1. Take ● For The Pork Chops & Marinade
  2. Get 4 LG EX Thick Pork Chops [fat free]
  3. Make ready as needed Worshestershire Sauce
  4. Take to taste Fresh Ground Black Pepper [a light dusting]
  5. Get to taste Garlic Powder [a light dusting]
  6. Get to taste Onion Powder [a light dusting]
  7. Take to taste Fresh Ground Cumin [a light dusting]
  8. Take ● For The Baby Russet Potatoes
  9. Prepare 2 Pounds Baby Golden Russet Potatoes [washed & halved]
  10. Prepare 1 tsp Herbs de Provence [crushed]
  11. Make ready 2 tbsp Fine Minced Garlic
  12. Make ready 1/4 Cup Quality White Wine [more if needed]
  13. Make ready 2 tbsp Quality Garlic Olive Oil
  14. Make ready 1 Cube Quality Sweet & Salted Butter
  15. Take 1/2 tsp Fresh Ground Black Pepper
  16. Get 1 Cup Vidalia Onions [thick sliced]
  17. Take 1 Cup Red Onions [thick sliced]
  18. Take 1/4 Cup Fresh Parsley [fine minced]
  19. Make ready to taste Sea Salt [a light sprinkle]
  20. Get ● For The Sides & Options
  21. Take Fresh Garden Salads
  22. Get Fresh Bread With Butter Or Garlic Oil
  23. Prepare Quality White Wine
Instructions to make Mike's Grilled Pork Chops & Baby Golden Russets:
  1. Add everything in the Marinade section to pork chops and marinate for 2 hours in fridge.
  2. Add all ingredients in the Baby Russet Section in a large skillet and melt. Chop your potatoes in half and slice your onions. Place in pan - potatoes face down.
  3. Steam potatoes for 10 minutes tightly covered then, uncover and pan sear on high to create a nice char on your spuds.
  4. Grill pork chops for 3 minutes on each side at 450°. Get some good char on em' too!
  5. Place hot, juicy grilled chops on a Himalayan Salt Brick for additional flavor if possible.
  6. Garnish plate with parsley and serve with a fresh garden salad, bread and a great Chardonnay. Enjoy!
