The ingredients needed to make Crazy Paneer 1:
  1. Make ready Gravy
  2. Take 1 dash sunflower oil
  3. Take 1 pinch mustard and cumin seeds
  4. Get 2 clove garlic
  5. Prepare 1 slice ginger
  6. Take 1 small white onion
  7. Get 1 medium red onion
  8. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp houmous
  9. Make ready 25 grams Wensleydale cheese
  10. Make ready 3 tomatoes
  11. Prepare 1 pinch turmeric
  12. Take 1 pinch chilli powder
  13. Get 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  14. Get 1/2 tsp Chaat Masala
  15. Make ready 4 mini bell peppers
  16. Take 1 dozen spinach leaves
  17. Make ready 250 grams diced Paneer

Steps to make Crazy Paneer 1:
  1. dice the garlic, ginger and onions
  2. fry the mustard and cumin seeds in the oil in a saucepan until they start to crackle
  3. add chopped garlic, ginger and onions to saucepan
  4. add houmous and Wensleydale cheese once onions have browned slightly
  5. chop and add tomatoes to saucepan
  6. add all spices and mix
  7. chop peppers and spinach
  8. add peppers, spinach and diced Paneer and mix thoroughly
  9. cover and let cook for approx 15-30 minutes
  10. uncover and let heat depending on your preference of consistency (e.g. watery or dry)
  11. serve with lettuce and sour cream or yoghurt and enjoy!
