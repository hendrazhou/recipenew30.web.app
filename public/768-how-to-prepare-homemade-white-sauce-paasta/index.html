The ingredients needed to make White sauce paasta:
  1. Prepare 1 bowl boiled paasta
  2. Get 2 tbs maida
  3. Prepare 1 onion chopped
  4. Get 1 capsicum chopped
  5. Get Salt, red chilli powder,black pepper powder as per taste
  6. Get 1 tbs oil

Steps to make White sauce paasta:
  1. Take a pan and heat the oil
  2. Put the chopped onion into the pan and cook it till the brown
  3. Put the chopped capsicum and cook it for 2 minutes
  4. Put maida in the pan and cook it for 3-4 minutes
  5. Now put salt, red chilli powder,black pepper powder as taste
  6. Now mix the boiled paasta in the pan and cook it for 2-3 minutes
  7. White sauce paasta is ready
