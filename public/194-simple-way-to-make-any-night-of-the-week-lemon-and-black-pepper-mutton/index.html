The ingredients needed to make Lemon and Black Pepper Mutton:
  1. Make ready 1 kg Mutton with fats
  2. Make ready 1/4 cup Oil
  3. Make ready 1 tbsp Ginger garlic paste
  4. Take 1 tbsp Green chili paste
  5. Make ready 1 tbsp Papita paste
  6. Get Lemon juice 1 /2 cup
  7. Make ready to taste Salt
  8. Prepare 2 tsp Black pepper pwd
  9. Prepare Green chili for garnish
  10. Get Ginger for garnishing
  11. Make ready 3 glasses Water
Steps to make Lemon and Black Pepper Mutton:
  1. First take a pan add oil and fats and fry them. Now add mutton and ginger garlic paste,papita paste and green chillies paste and fry for a while.
  2. Now add salt,and 3 glasses of water to cook meat until it's done. When meat cook dries out water, add black pepper and lemon juice.
  3. Cook for a while last add green chillies and ginger for garnishing. Serve with chapati.
