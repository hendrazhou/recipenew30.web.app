The ingredients needed to make Dorito crusted chicken fingers:
  1. Get 3 boneless, skinless chicken breast.
  2. Get 1 large bag of nacho cheese Doritos
  3. Take 2 cup milk
  4. Get 2 tbsp vinegar
  5. Take 2 cup flour
  6. Make ready 4 eggs
  7. Take 1 cooking spray

Steps to make Dorito crusted chicken fingers:
  1. Preheat oven to 375°F spray baking pan with cooking spray
  2. add 2 tablespoons of vinegar in a 2 cup measuring cup, add milk to the 2 cup line, let sit 5 minutes
  3. cut chicken into 1" strips, place in a bowl and pour milk mixture over chicken, cover and let stand in refrigerator for 3hrs
  4. in a blender pulse Doritos till they resemble course bread crumbs
  5. in a small bowl whisk eggs, in another small bowl put flour, and in another place the Dorito crumbs
  6. one piece at a time roll chicken strips in flour, then dip into eggs, then roll in Doritos
  7. bake for 20 - 25 minutes or until chicken finger are done
