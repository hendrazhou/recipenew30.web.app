The ingredients needed to make Taiwanese crispy salt and pepper chicken:
  1. Make ready 6 chicken leg portions
  2. Get 1 tablespoons chinese 5 spice
  3. Get 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  4. Make ready 1 tablespoon black pepper
  5. Take 2 tablespoon light soy sauce
  6. Make ready 1 tablespoon rice wine or Chinese cooking wine
  7. Make ready 5 tablespoon sweet potato flour
  8. Prepare 2 tablespoon corn flour
  9. Make ready Salt mix to serve
  10. Take 2 tablespoon table salt
  11. Make ready 2 tablespoon chinese five spice
  12. Prepare 2 tablespoon black pepper
  13. Take 1 tablespoon sugar
