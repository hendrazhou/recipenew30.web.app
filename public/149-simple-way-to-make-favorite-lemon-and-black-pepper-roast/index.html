Instructions to make Lemon and black pepper roast:
  1. Sb sy pehly chicken ko vinegar mily water mein dip kar ke 1/2 hour ke liye rakh dain
  2. Tamam masalon ko oil and dahi ke sath mix kar paste bana lain
  3. Chicken ko kitchen towel sy achi tarah saaf kar ke paste achi tarah laga kar marinate for 2 hours
  4. Ab pateeli ko garam krain chicken is mein rakh dain on medium flame for 10 min
  5. Then low flame par 1 hour ke liye rakhain darmian mein after half an hour side change kar dain
  6. Jab achi tarah gal jaye masala bhi hushk ho jaye then isy platter mein nikal kar enjoy with any sauce
