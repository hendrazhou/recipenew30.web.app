The ingredients needed to make Paneer(cottage cheese) in lemon garlic sauce.:
  1. Prepare 2 tsp garlic chopped finely
  2. Prepare 1 tbsp finely chopped ginger
  3. Get 250 grams paneer ( cottage cheese)
  4. Get 2 tbsp lemon juice
  5. Take 1 tsp lemon rind
  6. Make ready 2 tbsp olive oil
  7. Get 1/2 tsp Red chilli powder
  8. Prepare 1 tsp black pepper powder
  9. Take 2 tbsp honey
  10. Take salt
  11. Make ready 2 dried chillies

Steps to make Paneer(cottage cheese) in lemon garlic sauce.:
  1. In a nonstick pan add olive oil. And heat the oil.
  2. Now to hot oil add red chillies and sauté till crisp.
  3. Add ginger and garlic and sauté till garlic is browned.
  4. Add paneer cubes (cottage cheese) to the pan…add salt…mixand cook on high heat till paneer is brown.
  5. Add red chill powder and black pepper powder lemon juice and lemon rind and cook on high heat for 4-5 mins…
  6. Let the cottage cheese cubes become brown .
  7. Add honey and mix well.
