The ingredients needed to make Cheese & Onion Oat Bars:
  1. Take 1 cup oats,
  2. Prepare 1.5 cups grated cheddar cheese,
  3. Take 1/2 cup whole/semi skimmed milk,
  4. Make ready 1 medium grated carrot, excess moisture removed,
  5. Prepare 3 spring onions, chopped,
  6. Prepare 1 large egg,
  7. Prepare 1 tiny pinch of cumin powder,
  8. Make ready 1/2 tsp baking powder,
  9. Get 1 tbsp cooking oil,
  10. Get Salt and pepper to season,
  11. Make ready Spray oil or butter for greasing

Instructions to make Cheese & Onion Oat Bars:
  1. Preheat the oven to 180 (fan). Line a small high sided baking tray with some greaseproof paper and spray over some oil or grease with a little butter. Add the cooking oil to a small pan and fry off the spring onions until softened. Set aside for later.
  2. In a bowl add in the oats, baking powder, cumin, salt and pepper. Stir together.
  3. Next add in the egg, milk, grated carrot, cooked spring onions and 2/3rds of the grated cheddar. Stir together well until a nice thick batter is created.
  4. Spoon the thick mixture into the lined baking tray. Spread it evenly up to all sides. Sprinkle over the remaining 1/3rd of the cheddar. Bake in the oven for 20 minutes until cooked through and the cheese melted on top.
  5. Allow to cool for a couple of minutes then gently turn out onto a wire rack to cool and carefully peel away the greaseproof paper. Once cooled, cut into 6-12 squares, depending on how large you want the portions. :)
