The ingredients needed to make Seared pork chop with onions:
  1. Take 4 pork chops
  2. Get 1 tsp Cajun seasoning
  3. Make ready 1 tsp garlic powder
  4. Make ready 1 tsp onion powder
  5. Prepare 1 tsp black pepper
  6. Prepare 1 tsp rotisserie chicken seasoning
  7. Make ready 1 onion, chopped
  8. Get 1 tbsp olive oil, extra virgin
  9. Get 1 tbsp butter

Instructions to make Seared pork chop with onions:
  1. Season chops with all ingredients above on both sides
  2. Place oil in pan & sear on both sides for 5 mins
  3. Let chops rest meanwhile place butter in pan that u cooked the chops in add the sliced onions & place on top of chops after onions have cooked for 3 mins & enjoy 😀
