The ingredients needed to make Japanese Teriyaki Salmon:
  1. Get 1 pack Raw salmon
  2. Take 1/2 tsp Salad Oil (cooking oil)
  3. Prepare <Sauce>
  4. Get 3 tbsp Soy Sauce
  5. Make ready 4 tsp Sake(cooking rice wine)
  6. Get 4 tsp Mirin
Steps to make Japanese Teriyaki Salmon:
  1. If you buy the block salmon, slice it 2cm thick each. (When I buy the raw salmon at Fairprice, I slice it to 4 sheets.)
  2. Mix the all sauce ingredients and marinate the fillets for 10 to 15 minitues.
  3. Remove the fillets and use a paper towel to wipe the fillets dry. (Don't throw away the sauce)
  4. Heat 1/2 tbsp of salad oil in a frying pan, and fry the fillets.
  5. When golden brown, turn over, and cook the other side until golden brown. Turn to medium low and cook for 2 to 3 minute.
  6. Wipe off excess oil from Fillets with a paper towel well.
  7. Put in the remaining marinating sauce, and move the frying pan over the heat until the sauce covers the entire fish. (Add the marinating sauce when the fillets are mostly cooked through.)
  8. Soy Sauce - High recommend this soy soy sauce which is named Yamasa Konbutsuyu - SG$7~9/L at Dondon Donki, Mid Town Mart etc
  9. Sake- Cooking Rice Wine SG$5~7/500ml at RedMart, Sakuraya, Dondon DONKI etc
  10. Hinode Hon Mirin SG$4.6/320ml at RedMart
