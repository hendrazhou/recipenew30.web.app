Steps to make Honey mustard and garlic cubed pork chops:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Sprinkle cumin, garlic powder, salt, and pepper onto the pork chops (you'll want a fine layer of garlic powder on them to get the flavor).
  3. Flash fry the chops til nicely seared or white on both sides. Place them evenly into a pan (I used a 13x9).
  4. Combine honey, mustard, and just a touch of chilli powder into a bowl. (I had to microwave the honey to thin it out).
  5. With a tablespoon, coat one side of the chops.
  6. Bake at 350° for 20-25 minutes or until a deep, golden brown.
