Instructions to make Paneer satay:
  1. Cut Paneer in fingers
  2. Take maida and cornflour make batter of it
  3. Dip Paneer fingers in batter and deep fry it keep aside
  4. Heat oil in pan add garlic ginger paste saute it add crushed peanut
  5. Add Schezwan chutney soya sauce add water boil it
  6. Add cornflour slurry and add fried Paneer fingers cook it till gravy coat to Paneer enjoy as starters
