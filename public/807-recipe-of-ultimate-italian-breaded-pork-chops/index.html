The ingredients needed to make Italian Breaded Pork Chops:
  1. Make ready 3 large eggs eggs, lightly beaten
  2. Get 3 tbsp. milk
  3. Get 1 1/2 cups Italian seasoned bread crumbs
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  5. Get 2 tbsp. dried parsley
  6. Take 2 tbsp. olive oil
  7. Get 4 cloves garlic, peeled and chopped
  8. Prepare 4 raw chop with refuse, 113 g; (blank) 4 ounces pork chops

Steps to make Italian Breaded Pork Chops:
  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (160 degrees C).
  2. In a small bowl, beat together the eggs and milk. In a separate small bowl, mix the bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese, garlic and parsley.
  3. Dip each pork chop into the egg mixture, then into the bread crumb mixture, coating evenly.
  4. Heat the olive oil in a large, oven-proof skillet over medium heat. Place coated pork chops in the skillet, and brown abut 5 minutes on each side.
  5. Place the skillet and pork chops in the preheated oven, and cook 25 minutes, or to an internal temperature of 145 degrees F (63 degrees C).
