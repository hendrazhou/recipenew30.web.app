The ingredients needed to make Cheesy baked pork chops:
  1. Make ready 1 package pork chops
  2. Take 2 cans cream of mushroom
  3. Get 1 can cream of chicken
  4. Prepare 1 cup chopped onions
  5. Get 1 cup white rice
  6. Get 1 (8 oz) bag of shedded cheese

Instructions to make Cheesy baked pork chops:
  1. Pre heat oven for 400
  2. Cook the white rice until down drain and pour into a baking dish
  3. In the baking dish mix in cream of mushroom and cream of chicken with the rice add half of the onions
  4. Place the thawed pork chops on top between each pork chop put the rest of the onions
  5. Place in the oven when they are done put the shedded cheese on top let it bake long enough for the cheese to melt
