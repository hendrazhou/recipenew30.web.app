The ingredients needed to make Bubbled Chicken Cordon Bleu:
  1. Prepare 3/4 cup rotisserie chicken, diced
  2. Take 3/4 cup ham steak, diced
  3. Get 3/4 tsp Italian seasoning
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup alfredo sauce
  5. Get 1 Splash of milk
  6. Get 1 grated parmesan cheese, 1/8 c
  7. Prepare 1 refrigerated biscuits
  8. Get sliced or grated swiss cheese
  9. Get can cooking spray
Steps to make Bubbled Chicken Cordon Bleu:
  1. Preheat oven to 375. Spray pie pan or round baking dish with nonstick cooking spray.
  2. Mix chicken, ham, alfredo, milk, Italian seasoning, parm in LG bowl.
  3. Cut biscuits into 1/2in pieces. Add to alfredo mixture. Mix well.
  4. Add biscuit/alfredo mixture to sprayed pie pan.
  5. If using cheese slices, cut cheese into strips making a "lace" pattern over the pie. If using shredded cheese, sprinkle over pie.
  6. Bake 25-35 minutes, until biscuits are cooked and cheese is bubbly.
