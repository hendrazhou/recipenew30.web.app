Steps to make Parmesan pork chops:
  1. Preheat oven 400° Fahrenheit
  2. Add paper to an ovensafe pan.
  3. Mix in a shallow dish or paperplate, sage, garlic, mustards, oregano, onion powder, marjoram, basil, and parsley Set aside.
  4. Add salt and pepper to the pork chops, both sides. use more if needed or less if not wanted.
  5. Dredge, dip, the chops in mustard mixture. Then into the cheese. Set on paper which is on the pan as you coat both side of chops.
  6. Bake in oven 30-35 minutes. My chops were medium thickness. So cooking times very as to the thickness of your chops just make sure they are all the same thickness. If not then separate on separate pans by thickness of chops.
  7. Let rest undisturbed 7 minutes serve hope you enjoy!
  8. I use paper for easy clean up.
