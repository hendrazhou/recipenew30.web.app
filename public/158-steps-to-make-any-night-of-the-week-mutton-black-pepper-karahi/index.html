The ingredients needed to make Mutton Black Pepper Karahi:
  1. Make ready 750 kg mutton
  2. Make ready 1 &1/2 tbsp Ginger,Garlic paste
  3. Make ready 1/2 kg tomatoes
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup oil
  5. Get 1 & 1/2 tsp salt
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp freshly crushed black pepper
  7. Prepare Green chillies,coriander leaves and thin slices of ginger
Instructions to make Mutton Black Pepper Karahi:
  1. Boil mutton with salt and ginger garlic paste
  2. Now cut tomatoes in to two pieces
  3. Add tomatoes on mutton cover the lid and cook it 15 minutes on slow flame
  4. After 15 minutes removes the skin of tomatoes mashed them well and mix all things well
  5. Now add crushed black pepper and green chillies in to mutton
  6. Now cook 2 minutes more when oil seen separately serve it
  7. Garnish it with chillies,coriander leaves and ginger slices
  8. Enjoy it with nan and raita
