The ingredients needed to make Nutty Banana Muffins:
  1. Take 1 Pam spray, banana chips, peacans, milk, eggs, powder sugar, jiffy banana muffin mix
Steps to make Nutty Banana Muffins:
  1. Use 2 boxes of Jiffy muffin mix the banana kind. Do as directions say. Add half a hand full of banana chips & a half hand full of peacans & mix all together. Bake as it say on the box & when done sprinkle some power sugar. I hope you enjoy
  2. Put in a 12 cup cake pan & cook on 400°.
