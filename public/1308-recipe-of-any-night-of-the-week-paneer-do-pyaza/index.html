The ingredients needed to make Paneer do pyaza:
  1. Get 250 gm paneer
  2. Take 100 ml water
  3. Get 2 big size onion
  4. Take 2 bay leaves
  5. Take 1/2 tsp black cumin
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  7. Make ready 2 small Cardamon
  8. Get Salt as per taste
  9. Prepare 2 tsp oil
  10. Make ready 1/2 tsp black pepper powder
  11. Make ready 1/2 tsp garam masala
  12. Prepare 1/2 tsp fennel powder
  13. Take 4 big size tomato
  14. Get 1 cup curd
  15. Get 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  16. Prepare 1/2 tsp coriander powder

Instructions to make Paneer do pyaza:
  1. Wash and cut paneer in the medium size pieces
  2. Firstly, in a kadai heat 2 tsp oil and put cumin, bay leaves, saffon, black pepper and pinch hing.
  3. Cut 1 big size onion in long slice and tomato and add in the above pan.
  4. Take a bowl and add curd, red chilli powder, turmeric, salt coriander powder. Put this mixture in the above pan and set the flame to medium for 10 minutes
  5. Cut 1 onion in the different shape and fry it in another bowl for 2 minutes and add with the above bowl.Add fried paneer cubes also in the above pan and cover it on the gas.
  6. You can also add coriander leaves. Now Paneer do pyaza recipe is ready!
