The ingredients needed to make Flapjacks:
  1. Get 1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  2. Make ready 1 teaspoon sugar
  3. Prepare 1 tablespoon baking powder
  4. Prepare 1 1/4 cup coconut milk
  5. Make ready 1 teaspoon salt
  6. Get 3 tablespoons oil (not olive)
  7. Make ready 1 egg
  8. Prepare 1 teaspoon vanilla essence
  9. Get 1/2 cup berries (optional)
  10. Take Spray and cook (as needed)

Steps to make Flapjacks:
  1. Whisk all the dry ingredients together. In another bowl, mix the egg, oil, coconut milk, and vanilla essence. Combine both until smooth and wet.
  2. Grease a pan with spray and cook and spoon in mixture. Wait until it bubbles before flipping over. Do this with the rest of the batter. Make sure to clean your pan if the spray and cook burns or goes darker.
  3. Serve with optional extras. I used raspberries, cream, butter and maple syrup
