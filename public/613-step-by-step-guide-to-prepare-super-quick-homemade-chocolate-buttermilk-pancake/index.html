The ingredients needed to make Chocolate/Buttermilk Pancake:
  1. Make ready 21/2 cup flour
  2. Make ready 4 eggs
  3. Make ready 1 cup buttermilk
  4. Get 1 cup milk
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup sugar
  6. Make ready pinch salt
  7. Prepare cooking spray
  8. Prepare 1 tbsp cocoa powder
Steps to make Chocolate/Buttermilk Pancake:
  1. In a bowl mix buttermilk, milk, break in eggs, add sugar and salt then whisk everything together; you can alternate with your blender if you don't want to whisk
  2. Gently add the flour into the wet ingredient and fold together with your whisk or blender once you are done share barter into 2
  3. Sieve cocoa powder into one of the 2 bowls of barter and mix properly to get the chocolate barter
  4. Heat up frying pan and spray cooking spray into the pan, once it's hot pour in a cooking spoon of barter and flip to the other side once the side it ready. Continue till you complete the barter
  5. Serve with scrambled eggs and sausages
