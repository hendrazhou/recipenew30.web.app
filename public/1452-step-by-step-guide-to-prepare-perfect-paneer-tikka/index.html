The ingredients needed to make Paneer Tikka:
  1. Make ready 500 gms paneer diced
  2. Make ready 2 green bellpeppers
  3. Take 2 red onions roughly chopped
  4. Get Half cup greek yoghurt
  5. Get 1 lemon
  6. Get Salt, red chilli powder, black salt, black pepper, turmeric
  7. Prepare leaves Dry fenugreek
  8. Get 2 tablespoons chickpea flour (besan)
  9. Prepare Butter
  10. Take Cooking oil
  11. Take Skewers
  12. Take 1 tablespoon Ginger garlic paste

Steps to make Paneer Tikka:
  1. Mix the yoghurt and chickpea flour in a big bowl.
  2. Melt 2 tablespoon butter. Now add this butter to the flour and yoghurt mixture. Also add 1 teaspoon cooking oil.
  3. Add the spices - salt, chilli powder, turmeric, garam masala. Add a pinch of black salt.
  4. Rub 1 tablespoon fenugreek between your palms and add it to the mixture. This brings out the flavour from the leaves.
  5. Dice the vegetables and paneer. Add it to the mixture.
  6. Pop the vegetables on skewers.
  7. Heat a griddle pan. Pour a teaspoon oil. Spread it on the pan with a tissue.
  8. Heat the skewers in the pan evenly. Pour little drops of melted butter occasionally for flavour.
  9. Serve with green mint chutney or tamarind chutney.
