The ingredients needed to make Shahi paneer:
  1. Prepare 250 gms paneer
  2. Take 400 gms tomato
  3. Get 1 tbsp grated ginger
  4. Make ready 1 cup milk
  5. Make ready 2 tbsp fresh malai
  6. Prepare 3-4 green chilies finely chopped
  7. Make ready 1 tsp jeera
  8. Take 1/2 tsp. turmeric powder
  9. Prepare 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  10. Prepare 1/2 tbsp shahi paneer masala
  11. Make ready 2-3 tsp oil
  12. Prepare 1 pinch garam masala
  13. Prepare as par taste Salt
