The ingredients needed to make Matar Paneer:
  1. Make ready 1 cup onion paste
  2. Take 1 cup tomato pury
  3. Get 1-2 tablespoon ginger garlic paste
  4. Make ready 1-2 green chillis
  5. Make ready 2 tbsp oil
  6. Prepare 1-2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  7. Prepare 1-2 teaspoon red chilli powder
  8. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  9. Get 1-2 teaspoon coriander and cumin powder
  10. Take 200 gram paneer cut into small pieces
  11. Make ready 100 grams peas
  12. Take to taste Salt
  13. Prepare as required Water
  14. Take 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  15. Make ready 1 cup cashew paste
  16. Prepare 2-3 bay leaves

Instructions to make Matar Paneer:
  1. Heat 2 tbsp oil in the pan and add cumin seeds and bay leaves.
  2. After the cumin seeds and bay leaves are little roasted add ginger garlic paste and cook for at least a minute.
  3. Add, turmeric powder, red chilli powder, garam masala, onion paste and tomato pury and cook until the mixture leaves some oil
  4. After the mixture is cooked, add coriander and cumin powder, salt, cashew paste, peas and paneer to the mixture.
  5. If the mixture seems to be too thick, add water to get desired consistency of the gravy.
  6. Close the lid of the pan and let it cook for another 3-4 minutes.
  7. And the dish is ready to be served. Garnish the dish with chopped coriander.
