The ingredients needed to make Vienna Sausage Breakfast 🍳:
  1. Make ready 3 eggs
  2. Take 1/4 teaspoon dried onion flakes
  3. Make ready 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  4. Make ready Sprinkle salt
  5. Make ready 1 tablespoon milk or creamer
  6. Prepare 1 can (4.6 ounce) Vienna sausages, drained
  7. Take as needed Butter cooking spray
  8. Take to taste Sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
  9. Make ready to taste Chopped chives
  10. Get as needed Toasted bread

Instructions to make Vienna Sausage Breakfast 🍳:
  1. In a bowl with a fork or small whisk mix together eggs, dried onion flakes, black pepper, salt and milk or creamer……
  2. Place drained Vienna sausages into a bowl, cut each one in half…..
  3. Spray a skillet with butter cooking spray……
  4. Pour egg mixture into skillet and heat on low heat, then add into eggs your cut up Vienna sausages……
  5. Continue to cook eggs and Vienna sausages on low heat, stirring lightly……I flipped my eggs over…..
  6. Sprinkle your shredded cheddar cheese on top of eggs and sausages, then top with chopped chives…..cook until cheese has melted……
  7. Serve and enjoy 😉!!
