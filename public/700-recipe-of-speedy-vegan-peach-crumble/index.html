The ingredients needed to make Vegan Peach Crumble üçë:
  1. Get Filling
  2. Make ready 1-24 oz Can Peaches in light syrup
  3. Take 1 Tsp Vanilla Extract
  4. Make ready 1 Tb Lemon juice
  5. Get 1/2 Tb Corn Starch
  6. Make ready 1 Tb Sugar
  7. Take Crust
  8. Get 1/4 cup chilled Vegan butter
  9. Take 2 Tb Cane Sugar
  10. Take 1 Cup Flour
  11. Make ready 1/2 Cup Almond Milk
  12. Get 1 Tsp Baking Powder
  13. Prepare 1/2 Tsp Apple Cider Vinegar
  14. Prepare 1/4 Tsp Salt
  15. Prepare Sugar to sprinkle on top of crust (optional)
  16. Prepare Non-stick cooking spray
  17. Take Medium Baking pan
Steps to make Vegan Peach Crumble üçë:
  1. Preheat Oven to 375*F. Oil desired baking pans.
  2. In a mixing bowl start preparing filling. Dump in can of peaches (DO NOT DRAIN). Add in lemon juice, vanilla extract, salt and sugar. Combine until well incorporated. Once everything is combined transfer to baking dish.
  3. In a separate mixing bowl start preparing crumble. (Suggestion: use hand mixer or stand mixer) Cream together Butter and sugar until it reaches a fluffy consistency.
  4. Add in flour, almond milk, baking powder, and salt. Once everything is well incorporated add mixture on top of peaches already in baking dish. Smooth crush slightly to cover top of dish. Sprinkle a bit of sugar on crust.
  5. Place into oven and bake in oven for 50-55min or until top reaches a golden brown in color.
