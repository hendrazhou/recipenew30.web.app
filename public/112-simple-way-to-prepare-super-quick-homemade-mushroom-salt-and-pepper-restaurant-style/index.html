The ingredients needed to make Mushroom Salt And Pepper (Restaurant style):
  1. Get 200 grams Mushroom
  2. Prepare 6 pods finely chopped garlic
  3. Get 5 Spring onions (white part)
  4. Get 1/2 spoon Salt
  5. Prepare 1 spoon Pepper
  6. Prepare as needed Oil
  7. Get as needed Spring onion (green part) finely chopped
  8. Take For coating mushroom
  9. Get 3 spoons Corn flour
  10. Make ready 1 spoon Maida
  11. Get 1/2 spoon Salt
  12. Take 1/2 spoon Pepper
  13. Prepare as needed Water but very little

Steps to make Mushroom Salt And Pepper (Restaurant style):
  1. Chop mushrooms into bite size pieces and add 3 spoons corn flour, 1 spoon maida
  2. Add 1/2 spoon each of salt and pepper and gently mix the mushrooms. Add very little water if needed to coat the flour.
  3. Mix the coating well and fry the mushrooms to golden brown and set aside
  4. Take another pan and add oil and toss the finely chopped garlic to golden brown. Then add white part of spring onions and fry till transculent
  5. Add the fried mushrooms and saute for 30 seconds and then add 1/2 spoon salt and grounded pepper and toss again
  6. Finally add green part of spring onions give a toss and serve hot as starter.
