Steps to make Fried bread(evening snacks):
  1. Take a pan.
  2. Put some oil in it.
  3. Let it heat for 5 minutes.
  4. Now take another utensil.
  5. Put besan in it then add salt, red chilli powder, mashed potatoes, crushed onion and green chilli and aamchoor.
  6. Mix it well and some water in it.
  7. Now fill this mixture in between of 2 breads and fry it till brown.
  8. Now serve it with tomato ketchup and green chilli sauce.
