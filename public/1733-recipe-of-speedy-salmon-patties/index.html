The ingredients needed to make Salmon patties:
  1. Prepare 1 can (14.75 oz.) Arctic star pink salmon
  2. Get 2 cups soft bread crumbs
  3. Make ready 1/3 cup finely minced onion
  4. Make ready 2 eggs
  5. Prepare 2 TBSP minced parsley
  6. Make ready 1 TBSP lemon juice
  7. Make ready 1 dash pepper

Instructions to make Salmon patties:
  1. Drain salmon reserving 2 tablespoons of liquid
  2. Flake the salmon and combine all ingredients
  3. Shape into 8 1" patties
  4. Pan-fry on both sides in 2 tablespoons of Olive oil or butter until golden brown
