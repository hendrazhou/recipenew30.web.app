The ingredients needed to make 3 pepper Tamale Pie:
  1. Make ready 2 pounds ground sirloin
  2. Prepare 1 half large onion chopped
  3. Prepare 2 poblano peppers chopped
  4. Get 1 jalepeno chopped
  5. Take 1 tbsp butter
  6. Make ready 1/2 tbsp salt
  7. Get 1/2 tsp oregano
  8. Get 1 tsp chipotle powder
  9. Take 1 (16 oz) can enchilada sauce (hot)
  10. Make ready 3 boxes jiffy corn bread mix
  11. Get 3 eggs
  12. Get 1 cup milk
  13. Take 1 (16 oz) can mexicorn
  14. Take 1 1/3 cups Mexican blend shredded cheese
Steps to make 3 pepper Tamale Pie:
  1. Preheat oven to 350• F
  2. Sauté onions with butter in high rimmed pan until translucent, then add poblanos and continue for about 3 minutes.
  3. Add beef 🥩 to pan and brown, stirring in salt, oregano, and chipotle powder.
  4. When browned, add enchilada sauce, reduce to medium/low heat, and simmer for about 10 minutes.
  5. Make one box of cornbread using 1 egg and 1/3 cup milk 🥛 grease a large glass baking dish and spread batter along the bottom.
  6. Drain corn 🌽 and layer atop corn bread, spread beef over corn.
  7. Make remaining 2 corn bread boxes using 2 eggs and 2/3 cups milk, add jalepeno and 1 cup cheese to this mix, spread atop beef.
  8. Bake at 350• for 40 minutes
  9. Spread 1/3 cup cheese 🧀 on top, bake for another 10 minutes.
