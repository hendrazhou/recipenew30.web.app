The ingredients needed to make Dr Pepper Slow Cooker Ribs:
  1. Prepare 1 pack pork or beef ribs
  2. Prepare 2 sixteen oz bottles of Dr Pepper
  3. Prepare to taste seasoned salt
  4. Prepare to taste garlic powder
  5. Make ready 1 bottle barbecue sauce
Steps to make Dr Pepper Slow Cooker Ribs:
  1. Place ribs in slow cooker. Turn setting to high. 5 hrs for pork ribs. 6 hrs for beef.
  2. Add both bottles of Dr Pepper, seasoned salt and garlic pepper.
  3. Once ribs are TENDER, remove from pot and place in sauce pan. Cover. Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees.
  4. Once oven has reached desired temp, drizzle barbecue sauce over ribs. Place in oven for 15-20 min.
  5. Remove ribs from oven. Prepare to forget about your grill…
