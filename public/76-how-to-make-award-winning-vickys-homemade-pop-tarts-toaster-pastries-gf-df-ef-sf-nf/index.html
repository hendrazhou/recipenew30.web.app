The ingredients needed to make Vickys Homemade Pop Tarts / Toaster Pastries, GF DF EF SF NF:
  1. Take 1 whole recipe ready to roll Vickys Shortcrust Pastry, recipe link below
  2. Take 60 grams icing/powdered sugar
  3. Take 8 tsp jam, any flavours you prefer
  4. Take 4 tbsp coconut milk or preferred milk
  5. Prepare rainbow sprinkles to decorate
Steps to make Vickys Homemade Pop Tarts / Toaster Pastries, GF DF EF SF NF:
  1. Preheat oven to gas 7 / 210C / 425°F and line a baking sheet with parchment
  2. Roll out the pastry between 2 pieces of parchment paper into a large rectangle around 3mm thick - - https://cookpad.com/us/recipes/332957-vickys-sweet-savoury-shortcrust-pastry-gf-df-ef-sf-nf
  3. Cut into an even number of smaller rectangles around 4"tall x 3" wide. Keep re-rolling the pastry until you've got 12 or 14 rectangles
  4. Put a heaped tsp of jam on top of half of the rectangles and spread slightly, keeping a border around the edges of about half a cm
  5. Use a little milk or water to wet the border them place a plain rectangle on top
  6. Crimp around each edge with a fork, brush with a little more milk and prick the tops a few times
  7. Bake for 8 - 10 minutes or until golden
  8. Meanwhile mix a tbsp or so milk with the icing sugar to make a thick glaze
  9. Let the pastries cool a little, then spoon the glaze over and decorate with sprinkles
  10. You can use peanut butter or chocolate spread with chocolate sprinkles too
