The ingredients needed to make Coriander & Coconut Salmon:
  1. Get 150 g rice cooked according to the instructions on the packet
  2. Make ready 2 salmon steaks
  3. Prepare 2 tsps fish sauce
  4. Make ready 1/2 tsp ground ginger
  5. Get 125 ml coconut milk
  6. Take 1 garlic clove minced
  7. Prepare 5 g fresh coriander leaves
  8. Get 1 tbsp brown sugar
  9. Get 1 tbsp lime juice
  10. Take Salt and pepper for seasoning

Steps to make Coriander & Coconut Salmon:
  1.
