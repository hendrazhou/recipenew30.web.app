The ingredients needed to make Lababdar Paneer:
  1. Get 250 gm Paneer
  2. Get 4 tblspn Multi Purpose Gravy (see recipe)
  3. Get 1 onion finely chopped
  4. Take 1 tomato (finely chopped)
  5. Get 1 tblspn Red chilli powder
  6. Make ready 1/2 tspn Turmeric powder
  7. Prepare 1/2 tspn Garam masala (see recipe)
  8. Make ready 1 tblspn butter
  9. Make ready 2 tblspn cream
  10. Get 1 tblspn Ginger-Garlic paste
  11. Make ready 1 pinch asfoitida

Instructions to make Lababdar Paneer:
  1. Take a pan, add oil. Once oil is hot add asfoitida and ginger-garlic paste. Once it saute, add onion and let it cook on low flame. Once onion is tender, add tomato and masala.
  2. Let it cook for some time. After that add multi purpose gravy and butter. Continue to cook this on low flame.
  3. Add paneer. Mix it well. Add salt and 1/2 cup water. Mix it well and keep it for some time on low flame.
  4. Add cream, mix it well. After adding cream, switch off the flame. Serve hot with curry.
