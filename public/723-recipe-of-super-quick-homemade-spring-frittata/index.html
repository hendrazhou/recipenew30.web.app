The ingredients needed to make Spring frittata:
  1. Take 4 red potato, thinly sliced
  2. Get 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  3. Take 1 1/2 cup fresh asparagus, cut in 1" pieces and blanched
  4. Make ready 1 1/2 cup endives, coarsly chopped
  5. Prepare 1/3 cup crumbled feta cheese
  6. Take 6 eggs
  7. Prepare 1/3 cup milk
  8. Take 1 tablespoon flour
  9. Get to taste Lemon salt
  10. Make ready to taste Dry provence herbs
  11. Make ready to taste Fresh ground black pepper
  12. Prepare to taste Dry oregano
  13. Take Canola oil cooking spray
  14. Make ready Maple sirup to taste (optional)

Instructions to make Spring frittata:
  1. Spray a lasagna glass dish with the oil, lay the potato slices and sprinkle with lemon salt and Provence herbs.
  2. Top the potatos with the onions, than with the asparagus, than with the endives.
  3. Sprinkle with fresh ground black pepper and oregano. Top with crumbled feta cheese.
  4. Wisk the eggs with the milk and the flour in a small bowl. Pour over the veggies.
  5. Cover with foil, pierce foil so steam can come out and cook in a pre-heated oven at 375° for 30 minutes.
  6. Uncover and continue cooking for another 15 minutes. Finish at broil for a couple of minutes.
  7. Serve with a drizzle of maple sirup (optional).
  8. Enjoy!
