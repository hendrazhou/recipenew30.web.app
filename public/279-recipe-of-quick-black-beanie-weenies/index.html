The ingredients needed to make Black Beanie Weenies:
  1. Make ready Part one
  2. Prepare 1 pound hotdogs
  3. Prepare 1 large onion diced
  4. Make ready 1 large green bell pepper diced
  5. Get 29 ounce can of black beans separate 1 cup
  6. Get 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  7. Take 1 tablespoon molasses
  8. Make ready 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  9. Make ready To taste salt
  10. Take 1/2 cup water
  11. Make ready Part 2 for the one cup of separation
  12. Take 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  13. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon oregano
  14. Take To taste salt
  15. Take To taste ground black pepper
  16. Prepare Green bean and potato fried rice
  17. Get 1-1/2 cup cold cooked rice
  18. Prepare 15 ounce can green beans and potatoes
  19. Get 1/2 teaspoon seasoned salt
  20. Get 1/2 teaspoon granulated onion powder
  21. Get 1/2 teaspoon granulated garlic powder
  22. Take 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  23. Make ready To taste salt if needed
  24. Make ready 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  25. Take Optional
  26. Prepare To taste hot pepper vinegar sauce see my recipe
Steps to make Black Beanie Weenies:
  1. Separate 1 cup of black beans, and set aside. Heat two tablespoons oil in a deep pot, add the diced onion and peppers. Add the spices. Sauté till onions are translucent. Slice the hotdogs into bite sized pieces.
  2. Add the one cup of black beans. Add the oregano. Fry the beans and smash them. Add to the onions and peppers and sauté for 15 minutes.
  3. Add the hotdogs.
  4. Heat two tablespoons of olive oil. Drain the green beans liquids and save the liquids. Add green beans and potatoes to the hot oil.
  5. Fry till lightly browned. Now add the rice and spices. Stir in well. Add half the green bean liquid. Cook till liquids are absorbed and brown the rice and potatoes slightly.
  6. Add all the beanie weenie ingredients together and incorporate well. Simmer for 30 minutes stirring often.
  7. Add fried rice to a plate.
  8. Serve I hope you enjoy!!
