The ingredients needed to make Baklava Cheesecake Tarts:
  1. Prepare 5 sheets phyllo pastry
  2. Prepare 4 tbsp melted butter
  3. Make ready Filling:
  4. Get 1 tub cottage cheese (Parmalat)
  5. Make ready 1 egg
  6. Take 1 tbsp sugar
  7. Get 1/4 cup condensed milk
  8. Take 1 tbsp cornflour (Maizena)
  9. Get Topping:
  10. Get 3/4 cup mixed nuts, chopped
  11. Make ready 1 tbsp maple syrup/golden syrup
  12. Get 1 tbsp water
  13. Get Pinch salt
  14. Prepare 1/2 tsp cinnamon powder
Steps to make Baklava Cheesecake Tarts:
  1. For the baskets: Preheat oven to 180°C. Brush each sheet of phyllo pastry with melted butter using a pastry brush. Lay each layer on top of the buttered one until all are used up.
  2. Using a pastry cutter, cut 12 squares. Separate the layers and rotate each layer slightly so the the corners get staggered. Press into a greased cupcake pan. Bake for 10 minutes. Remove from oven and set aside.
  3. For the filling: lower oven temperature to 160°C. Whisk all filling ingredients (cottage cheese, egg, sugar, condensed milk, cornflour) together and place one tablespoon of mixture in each phyllo basket. Bake for 20-25 minutes until cheese filling has set. Remove from oven and let cool.
  4. For the topping: place all topping ingredients (mixed nuts, maple syrup, water, salt, cinnamon powder) in a non-stick pan over medium heat, allow water to dry a little and a syrup to form. Remove from heat.
  5. Spoon one tablespoon of nut mixture on top of the cheesecake amd allow to cool until set. Serve immediately.
