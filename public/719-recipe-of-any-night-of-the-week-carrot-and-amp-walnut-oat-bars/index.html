The ingredients needed to make Carrot & Walnut Oat Bars:
  1. Get 1 grated carrot (medium sized),
  2. Make ready 90 g oats,
  3. Get 1 large egg,
  4. Make ready 1/3 rd cup whole milk,
  5. Take 1/3 rd cup ground almonds,
  6. Make ready 1/3 rd cup raisins or sultanas,
  7. Get 1/3 rd cup crushed walnuts,
  8. Take 1 tbsp runny honey,
  9. Get 1.5 tsp ground ginger,
  10. Prepare 1.5 tsp ground cinnamon,
  11. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla essence,
  12. Prepare 1 tsp baking powder
  13. Get Spray cooking oil

Instructions to make Carrot & Walnut Oat Bars:
  1. Preheat your oven to 180 (fan) and line a small shallow baking tray with some greaseproof paper. Spray over a bit of cooking oil, this will help prevent any sticking during baking.
  2. In a mixing bowl add in the oats, ground almonds, baking powder, ginger, cinnamon and vanilla essence. Mix together. Next add in the honey, milk, and egg. Stir well to combine.
  3. Then add in the crushed walnuts, raisins and grated carrot. Give the batter a good mix.
  4. Pour the batter into the lined baking tray and flatten out neatly and evenly so it reaches to the sides. Bake in the oven for 20-25 minutes until cooked through, firm and springy to the touch.
  5. Leave to cool for a couple of minutes then gently turn out onto a wire rack to cool and peel away the greaseproof paper. Once cool, cut into squares or long bars.
