The ingredients needed to make Stuffed Pepper Bake:
  1. Take 3 Bell Peppers (different colors)
  2. Prepare 1 Onion Diced
  3. Get 1 lb Hamburger
  4. Prepare 2 tsp minced garlic
  5. Get 1 bag brown rice
  6. Get 1 can diced tomatoes
  7. Make ready 1 bag mozzarella cheese
  8. Prepare 1 can tomato soup
  9. Make ready A1 Sauce
  10. Get salt and pepper
Steps to make Stuffed Pepper Bake:
  1. Preheat oven to 375
  2. Brown beef, onions, and garlic in a skillet. Drain.
  3. Boil rice, chop and sautee peppers with salt. Add to beef mixture once they are done.
  4. Add tomatoes, salt, pepper, mozzarella cheese, and A1 sauce to mixture. Spray a 9x13 dish and pour mixture in.
  5. Top with parmesan cheese. Then mix 1 can of tomato soup with half a can of water. Pour over top of everything.
  6. Cover with foil and bake for 45 minutes to an hour.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
