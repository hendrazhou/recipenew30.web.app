The ingredients needed to make Nawabi Paneer:
  1. Take For Gravy
  2. Take 1 Spoon Ginger
  3. Take 1/2 cup Cashew
  4. Make ready 1/2 tsp Cardamom
  5. Take 1/2 tsp Black Pepper
  6. Take 250 gm Onion Cut into Cubes
  7. Take 5 garlic cloves
  8. Take 1/2 tsp Poppy Seeds
  9. Make ready as needed Butter for Cooking
  10. Get 1/2 cup Almond
  11. Make ready 1 Spoon Cumin Seeds
  12. Prepare as needed Oil for Cooking
  13. Get 500 gm PANEER
  14. Get 2 Bay leaves
  15. Make ready 1 cinnamon stick
  16. Make ready 2 Cup Milk
  17. Make ready 1/2 cup Curd
  18. Prepare 1 Cup Cream
  19. Make ready 3 Cardamom
  20. Take to taste Salt
  21. Make ready 2 Spoon Kewra Water
  22. Prepare 2 Green Chillies
  23. Take 1 tsp Kasuri Methi Crushed
  24. Get 2 Spoon Kesar (safforn)Milk
  25. Make ready 1/2 cup Dry Coconut
Steps to make Nawabi Paneer:
  1. White Masala Paste:Take a Kadhai or Pan add butter or oil heat for 2 min then add Onion,Ginger, Garlic, Black Pepper, Cardamom, Cashew, Almonds, Poppy Seeds, Dry Coconut Cook for 10 to 15 min then off the flame cool it down on room temperature Grind it in Mixer and make a smooth paste keep aside.
  2. Take a Kadhai or Pan add oil and butter heat for 2 min then add Cumin Seeds,Bay leaves, Cinnamon, then add White Masala Paste Heat for 10 min then add Curd half heat for 7 min till retain oil over surface.
  3. Afterwards add Amul fresh Cream 1 Cup mix well cook for 10 min then add Milk 2 Cup Green Chillies,Salt, Kasuri Methi Cook for 10 min mix well.
  4. Afterwards add PANEER 500 gm Cut into Cubes Cook for 5 to 7 min lastly add Kesar Milk 2 Spoon,Kewra Water 2 Spoon Cook for 5 to 7 min.Gravy becomes Smooth.
  5. Now Serve it hot with Rice,Naan, Chapati, Paratha your Delicious NAWABI PANEER is Ready to Enjoy
