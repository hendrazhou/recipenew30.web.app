The ingredients needed to make Apple Pancakes With Strawberries (from Smitten Kitchen):
  1. Get 1 Egg, beaten
  2. Prepare 3/4 cup Milk
  3. Prepare 1 cup All purpose flour, sifted
  4. Make ready 1/2 tsp Baking powder
  5. Get 1/2 tsp Salt
  6. Make ready 2 tbsp Sugar
  7. Take 2 Apples, cored, peeled, grated
  8. Get 1/2 tbsp Ground cinnamon
  9. Make ready 1 as needed Frying oil or cooking spray
  10. Prepare 10 Strawberries, sliced
Instructions to make Apple Pancakes With Strawberries (from Smitten Kitchen):
  1. Mix the egg with the milk in a large bowl.
  2. In a smaller bowl, whisk the flour, baking powder, cinnamon, salt and sugar together.
  3. Combine the wet and the dry ingredients and stir in the apples.
  4. Heat a thin layer of oil in a skillet over low to medium heat. Drop large spoonful of batter into the pan and flatten it out a little (otherwise, you might have trouble getting them to cook in the center) and cook until golden brown underneath. Flip the pancakes and cook them for an additional two or three minutes.
  5. Serve hot along with the strawberries.
