The ingredients needed to make Chicken with Choy Sum and Mushroom Black Pepper:
  1. Prepare 2 bunches choy sum
  2. Take 200 gr chicken breast
  3. Make ready 1 red onion
  4. Take 3 garlic cloves
  5. Get 1 tps salt
  6. Prepare 1 tsp black peper
  7. Make ready 1/2 tsp sugar
  8. Prepare 3 red jalapenos (optional)
  9. Prepare 2 tbsp cooking oil
  10. Prepare Water to boil chicken
Steps to make Chicken with Choy Sum and Mushroom Black Pepper:
  1. Chop onion, garlicd, and choy sum, chop red jalapenos (optional), put aside.
  2. Boil chicken breast, and shred it, put aside.
  3. Pre-heat cooking oil in a pan, add oniom, garlics, and jalapenos (optional), stir for 3 mins.
  4. Add salt, sugar, and black pepper, stir well.
  5. Add chicken, mix well.
  6. Add choy sum, mix properly, cook with low fire, wait until a little bit watery and the choy sum cooks.
  7. Ready to serve while hot.
