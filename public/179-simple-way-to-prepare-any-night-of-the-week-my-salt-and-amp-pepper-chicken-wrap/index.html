The ingredients needed to make My Salt & Pepper Chicken Wrap ðŸ˜‰:
  1. Prepare 2 Wholemeal Wraps warmed for 30 seconds in microwave
  2. Take 3 Salt & Pepper Chicken breasts ready made or made yourself
  3. Prepare Enough Spinach for 2 wraps
  4. Get 6 cherry tomatoes sliced
  5. Make ready 10 slices Cucumber slices and cut in half
  6. Take 2 tbls mayonaise or Sour cream on each wrap
  7. Prepare 1/2 tbls cracked black Peppercorns
