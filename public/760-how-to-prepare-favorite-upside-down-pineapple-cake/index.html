The ingredients needed to make Upside down pineapple cake:
  1. Get 1 packet cake mix
  2. Make ready 1 head ripe pineapple cut round and remove the skin and the hard centre too
  3. Prepare 3 fresh eggs
  4. Make ready 3 pieces ripe bananas
  5. Make ready 1 fresh cucumber
  6. Make ready 2 pieces fresh carrots
  7. Prepare 1 tin oil spray
  8. Take 4 table spoons of powder ed milk

Steps to make Upside down pineapple cake:
  1. Put the cake mix and powdered milk in a clean and dry bowl
  2. Put the bananas,cucumber, carrots and eggs in a blender and blend
  3. Pure in the cake mix and stir everything together for 10 minuets
  4. Heat up the cake pan and spray the oil spray to prevent from sticking to the pan
  5. Arrange the cut pineapple at the bottom of the pan
  6. Pure the batter inside the pan
  7. Put in the oven and bake for 15 -20 minuets
  8. Use the toothpick to check it’s probably baked in the middle
  9. Serve hot or warm
