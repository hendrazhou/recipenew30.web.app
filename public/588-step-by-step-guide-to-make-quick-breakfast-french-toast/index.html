The ingredients needed to make Breakfast French Toast:
  1. Take 4 eggs
  2. Get 1/2 cup milk
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup orange juice
  4. Get 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  5. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon powder
  6. Take Bread for French toast
  7. Prepare Butter cooking spray
  8. Get Maple syrup
  9. Make ready Confectioners sugar (sprinkled over top)
