The ingredients needed to make Stewz Creamy Rice:
  1. Take vegetable
  2. Prepare 5 Black Olives
  3. Get 2 Celery
  4. Take 2 Mushroom
  5. Make ready 1 Idaho Potato
  6. Take 1 can Cream of Broccoli Soup
  7. Get 1 garlic powder
  8. Take 1 Seasoned Salt
  9. Prepare 1 olive oil cooking spray
  10. Prepare 2 cup Rice
  11. Take 1 sunflower nuts
  12. Take cup milk
  13. Take ketchup
Steps to make Stewz Creamy Rice:
  1. Cut potato into wedges. Sprinkle with below stuff. Bake 15 minutes at 450.
  2. Cook rice in cooker same time as potato.
  3. Cut below ingredients and cook on skillet on medium heat. Add sunflower nuts for a nice crunch.
  4. Add soup to pot. Fill can with milk and add to pot. Cover and cook on medium.
  5. Add rice to plate, soup on top and skillet items on top of that. Add potato wedges and top with ketchup. Enjoy!
