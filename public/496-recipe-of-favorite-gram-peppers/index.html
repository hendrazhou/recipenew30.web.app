The ingredients needed to make Gram Peppers:
  1. Take —peppers———
  2. Make ready 5 large bell peppers
  3. Prepare 2 large eggs
  4. Make ready 1 tablespoon baking powder
  5. Take 1/2 cup finely shredded mild cheddar cheese
  6. Get 3 cups gram flour, chickpea flour
  7. Make ready —season mix——–
  8. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon mustard powder, dry
  9. Get 1/2 teaspoon poultry seasoning
  10. Take 1 teaspoon summer savory
  11. Make ready 1 teaspoon granulated garlic powder
  12. Get 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  13. Take 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  14. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
  15. Make ready 2 teaspoon salt
  16. Make ready –vegetable—–
  17. Take 14 can collard greens
  18. Make ready 1-1/2 tablespoons dehydrated onions
  19. Make ready 1 teaspoon extra virgin olive oil
Instructions to make Gram Peppers:
  1. Preheat oven 400 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Mix all the seasoning mix
  3. Heat a skillet. Drain the collards, keeping the liquids.
  4. Add collards and fry them for 7 minutes, salt to taste.
  5. Mix the liquids with gram flour, with collard greens, and 1 tablespoon of seasoning mix.
  6. Add eggs and baking powder mix well.
  7. Split the peppers in two pieces top to bottom. Remove seeds. Set in a baking pan, I used a cupcake pan
  8. Pour batter into pepper halves
  9. Bake in oven till done about 40 minutes
  10. Add cheese to top and put bake in oven for 10-15 minutes.
  11. Let sit 7 minutes and serve.
  12. I hope you enjoy!
