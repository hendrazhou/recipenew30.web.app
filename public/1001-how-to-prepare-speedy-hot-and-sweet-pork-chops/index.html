The ingredients needed to make Hot and Sweet Pork Chops:
  1. Make ready 1 pork chops
  2. Prepare 2 tbsp hot sauce
  3. Take 2 tbsp syrup
  4. Get 1 tbsp butter
  5. Prepare 1/2 tbsp brown sugar
  6. Get salt, pepper, Italian seasoning
Steps to make Hot and Sweet Pork Chops:
  1. Season pork chop
  2. Combine hot sauce, syrup, brown sugar & butter in microwavable container. Microwave for about 1 minute or until butter melts.
  3. Stir sauce and spoon or brush over pork chop
  4. (Fire) broil for 10 minutes
