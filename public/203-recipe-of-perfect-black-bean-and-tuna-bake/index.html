The ingredients needed to make Black Bean and Tuna Bake:
  1. Prepare Ingredients for tuna and black bean bake:
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup rinsed canned black beans
  3. Get 1/2 cup tomatoes seeded and diced
  4. Take 1/2 cup sour cream
  5. Take 1/2 cup shredded Mexican blend cheese plus extra to sprinkle on top
  6. Get 2 cans (5 oz.) each chunk light tuna in water, drained, flaked
  7. Prepare 1/4 cup pickled jalapenos diced or to taste
  8. Make ready to taste Salt and pepper
  9. Get Other ingredients:
  10. Get 2 tbsp finely sliced scallions optional
  11. Prepare 2 tbsp cilantro optional
  12. Prepare Tortilla chips
Steps to make Black Bean and Tuna Bake:
  1. In a large bowl, mix all the ingredients for tuna bake. Transfer the mixture to a lightly greased cake pan and cover with aluminum foil. Air fry at 360F (180C) for 15 minutes.
  2. Remove the foil and sprinkle some more cheese on top. Air fry at 360F (190C) for 4-5 minutes until the cheese is slightly golden brown.
  3. Sprinkle some scallions and cilantro and serve with tortilla chips if desired.
