The ingredients needed to make Paneer Paratha:
  1. Prepare 1 1/2 cup wheat flour
  2. Get 250 gm grated Paneer
  3. Get 1 finely chopped onion (small size)
  4. Take 1 chopped green chilli
  5. Make ready to taste Salt
  6. Make ready 1/2 spoon chopped ginger
  7. Get 1 spoon chopped fresh coriander
  8. Take 1/4 tsp Chaat masala
  9. Prepare 1/4 tsp roasted cumin powder
  10. Prepare as needed Ghee/oil
Steps to make Paneer Paratha:
  1. Take a wheat flour in a bowl add pinch of salt and then add sufficient water,Mix well and Knead a soft dough,then cover it and set aside for 20 minutes..
  2. In another bowl add paneer,onion,green chilli, cumin powder, ginger, corinader, chaat masala and salt(as per taste) and mix well
  3. Now take small portion of a dough make it round shape and stuff the portion of prepared mixture, and seal it completely and make a nice ball,sprinkle some flour on it and roll the dough into a paratha.
  4. Heat the tawa, add the paratha onto the tawa,once itis cooked from one side,flip it on other side apply ghee and cook it till golden brown
  5. And Ready to eat - Serve with curd and green chutney and butter
