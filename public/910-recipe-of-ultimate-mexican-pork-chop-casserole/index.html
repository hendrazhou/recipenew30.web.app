Steps to make Mexican Pork Chop Casserole:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. In casserole dish spread refried beans on bottom.
  2. Precook pork chops place on top of beans in casserole dish. Cook rice packages according to package directions.
  3. Spread rice on top of pork chops in casserole dish. Place in oven covered with foil.
  4. Cook for 20 minutes with foil on so rice does not get dry. Cook another 15 minutes without foil. Cook another 5 minutes with cheese added on top. Serve with love.
