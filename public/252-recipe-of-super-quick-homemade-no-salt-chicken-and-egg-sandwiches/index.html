  4. Make ready 3 tbsp Chicken spread
  5. Get 3 tbsp Cream
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp Milk
  7. Get Grated cheddar cheese (optional)

Instructions to make No salt chicken and egg sandwiches:
  1. Boil the chicken and egg.
  2. Grind both chicken and egg finely. Sprinkle black pepper
  3. Add cream, chicken spread and milk. Make a thick paste. Add more if required. You can add grated cheese if you are a cheese lover.
  4. Spread a thick layer on the bread slices and cut into 2 or 4.
  5. Serve.
