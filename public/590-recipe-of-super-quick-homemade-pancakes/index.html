The ingredients needed to make Pancakes:
  1. Prepare 1/2 cup flour
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup liquid milk
  3. Take 1 egg
  4. Get 1 tbsp sugar
  5. Prepare 1 pinch salt
  6. Get cooking spray
