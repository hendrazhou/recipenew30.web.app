The ingredients needed to make Chilli paneer:
  1. Make ready 1 bowl paneer cubes
  2. Get 2 onions
  3. Prepare 2 capsicum
  4. Take To taste salt
  5. Get 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  6. Get 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  7. Make ready 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  8. Make ready 1 tsp red chilli sauce
  9. Prepare 1 tsp soya sauce

Steps to make Chilli paneer:
  1. Firstly, Take a pan heat the oil add paneer cubes and fry it.
  2. Now take a another pan heat the oil add onion and capsicum and saute it.
  3. Now add salt,red chilli powder,coriander powder,turmeric powder and mix well.
  4. Now add red chilli sauce and soya sauce and mix well.
  5. Now add paneer cubes and mix it.
  6. Chilli paneer is ready to eat.
