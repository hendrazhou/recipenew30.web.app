Instructions to make Palak Paneer:
  1. Chop Palak, Dhaniya Leaves, Tomato, Onion, Ginger, Garlic, Green Chillies roughly.
  2. Stir Dahi properly so that it doesn’t get burned while cooking & remember to use dahi only to cut the bitter taste of Palak & don’t use sugar.
  3. Now take a Big Pot, add 1 Litre in it & keep the gas on medium flame.
  4. When water starts boiling, add roughly chopped Palak leaves in it & cook them for 3-4 minutes.
  5. Now 1 minute before taking them out, add chopped dhaniya leaves in it & cook them for 1 minute.
  6. After 1 minute, turn off the gas & take them out in a plate & immediately wash them with cold water so that their cooking gets stopped & their colour doesn’t change more.
  7. Now let them cool down before grinding them.
  8. Meanwhile, take a kadhai, add 3 Table Spoon Oil or 30gms Butter in it & keep the gas on medium flame.
  9. When oil or butter gets heated, add 2 Teaspoon Jeera in it & let them sizzle.
  10. When Jeera starts sizzling, add roughly chopped Onion, Ginger, Garlic, Green chillies in it & cook them till they become translucent or soft.
  11. After 3-4 minutes, when they become soft, add roughly chopped tomato in it with 1 Teaspoon Salt so that they start releasing oil quickly & become soft.
  12. After 5-6 minutes, when oil is completely released &they become into paste, turn off the gas & take them out in a bowl & let them cool down.
  13. Cut Paneer in cubes or whatever shape you want & keep them at room temperature of 30 minutes.
  14. After some time when they cool down properly, grind boiled palak & dhaniya leaves & onion tomato mixture together finely into gravy in a mixer grinder.
  15. Now take same kadhai, add 2 Table Spoon Oil in it & keep the gas on medium flame.
  16. When oil gets heated up, add spices mix & 10gms butter in it & roast them for 1-2 minutes.
  17. After 1-2 minutes, add finely grinded Palak gravy paste in it with some water, mix them properly & let them boil.
  18. When first boil comes up, lower the flame, add 3 Table Spoon Dahi in it & start stirring them immediately so that it doesn’t get burned.
  19. Then add paneer cubes in it & mix them properly.
  20. Now cover the lid & let them cook for 3 minutes.
  21. After 3 minutes, open the lid & add 1 Teaspoon Garam Masala in it & mix it properly.
  22. Your Palak Paneer is ready to be served.
