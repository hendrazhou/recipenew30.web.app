The ingredients needed to make Asian Inspired Pan Rushed Pork Chops:
  1. Get Marinade
  2. Prepare 1 tbsp fresh ginger
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup Hoisin sauce
  4. Take 1/2 tsp cayenne
  5. Take 1 sprinkle of peppet or steak sauce
  6. Prepare 1 tsp oregeno
  7. Get 1 tsp soy sauce
  8. Take 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  9. Get 2 tbsp red wine, rice and cider vinegars
  10. Make ready 1 dash turmeric
  11. Make ready Sauce
  12. Prepare 1/4 bell peppet
  13. Get 1/4 cup water
  14. Get 2 tsp ginger marmalade
  15. Take 1/4 tsp corn starch
  16. Get 4 -6 pork chops
  17. Get 1 tbsp coconut oil
Instructions to make Asian Inspired Pan Rushed Pork Chops:
  1. Combinemarinade and let sit with chops 1/2 hour to overnight
  2. Save some marinade for the sauce
  3. Preheat pan on high heat
  4. Heat oil
  5. Sear chops on high heat for 2 minutes each side Or until golden brown
  6. Simmer the rest of the marinade with sauce ingredients until it starts to thicken
  7. Put in a roasting pan or pan with sides along with the pork
  8. Cook in oven (covered) for 7-9 minutes or until they reach 145°F
  9. Put it on a plate, tented with tin-foil for a few minutes before serving
