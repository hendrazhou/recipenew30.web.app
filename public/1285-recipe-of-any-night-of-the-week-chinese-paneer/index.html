The ingredients needed to make Chinese Paneer:
  1. Get 200 gm Paneer
  2. Make ready 1 large onion largely diced
  3. Get 1 inch ginger cut into Julien
  4. Make ready 1 tsp onion paste
  5. Get 1 tbsp soya sauce
  6. Make ready 1tbp green chilli sauce
  7. Prepare 1 tsp vinegar
  8. Get 1 tbsp refined oil
  9. Make ready 1 cup refined oil for deep frying
  10. Get 1.5 tsp salt
  11. Take 1 tbsp corn flour
  12. Make ready 2 tbsp water
  13. Take 3 cloves garlic chopped
  14. Make ready 3 green chillies chopped
  15. Prepare 1 tsp ginger paste
  16. Make ready 1 tbsp garlic paste
  17. Prepare 1 tsp crushed black pepper

Instructions to make Chinese Paneer:
  1. Prepare all ingredients
  2. Deep fry panner cubes in 1cup oil for 2 to 3 minutes. We dont have to fry to brown colour… only slight golden brown will do.
  4. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a kadhai or wok and add paste of ginger and paste of garlic and fry for 30 sec and then add diced onion and cook until translucent
  6. Add green chilli, ginger jullien, chopped or sliced garlic and onion paste and cook for 3 to 4 minutes.
  7. Now mix chilli sauce, vinegar, salt, soya sauce well and pour into kadhai. mix well and cook for 3 minutes
  9. Now add fried paneer cubes, crushed black pepper and give a mix.. cover and cook for 4 to 5 minutes.
  11. Now mix corn flour and water to make a thin batter
  12. Pour little by little of this corn batter into the paneer and mix constantly but gently.
  13. Adjust the thickness of your gravy with water. Add more salt if you want.
  14. Cook for 4 to 5 minutes and your chinese paneer is ready.
  15. Serve with rumali roti, chapati, fried rice….
