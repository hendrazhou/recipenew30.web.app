The ingredients needed to make Fried Paneer:
  1. Make ready 200 gm paneer cut into pieces
  2. Make ready 2 tsp cornflour
  3. Take 1/4 tsp black pepper powder
  4. Prepare 1/4 tsp red chilli powder
  5. Prepare To taste salt
  6. Prepare As needed oil for frying
