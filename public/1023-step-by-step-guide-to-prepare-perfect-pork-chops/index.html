The ingredients needed to make Pork chops:
  1. Prepare 8 pork chops
  2. Take 1 packages stuffing mix
  3. Get 4 envelope sazon
  4. Prepare salt and pepper
  5. Make ready 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
