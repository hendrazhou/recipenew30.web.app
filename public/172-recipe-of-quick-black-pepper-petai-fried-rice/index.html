The ingredients needed to make Black Pepper Petai Fried Rice:
  1. Prepare 2 bowls cooked rice - preferably day-old (350g)
  2. Get 2 tbsp cooking oil
  3. Get 1 tbsp ginger - minced
  4. Get 1 clove garlic - minced
  5. Make ready 50 g chicken fillet - diced
  6. Prepare 50 g shelled prawns - diced
  7. Prepare 30 g carrot - diced
  8. Prepare 3 bird’s eye chillies - chopped
  9. Take 60 g petai - halved and diced (stink beans)
  10. Take 2 eggs
  11. Make ready Seasoning:
  12. Take 1/2 tsp salt
  13. Make ready 1/4 tsp pepper
  14. Take 1/2 tsp ground black pepper

Instructions to make Black Pepper Petai Fried Rice:
  1. Loosen the rice grains to separate them if they are in clumps. Heat the oil in a wok until hot. Stir-fry the ginger and garlic over low heat until fragrant.
  2. Add the chicken, prawns and carrot. Stir-fry until aromatic.
  3. Add the rice and toss briefly to combine. Make a well in the centre of the rice, add a little oil, and crack in the eggs. Let the eggs set for a few seconds before scrambling them over high heat together with the rice.
  4. Add the seasoning and sprinkle over a little water to moisten the rice. Add the chillies and petai. Stir-fry briskly until rice is fairly hot and fragrant.
  5. Dish out and serve.
