The ingredients needed to make Fluffy Pancakes:
  1. Prepare 3/4 cup milk
  2. Take 2 tbsp vinegar
  3. Get 1 cup all-purpose flour
  4. Prepare 2 tbsp sugar
  5. Get 1 tsp baking powder
  6. Get 1/2 tsp baking soda
  7. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  8. Get 1 egg
  9. Make ready 2 tbsp butter, melted
  10. Take 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  11. Prepare 1 cooking spray
Instructions to make Fluffy Pancakes:
  1. Combine milk with vinegar in a medium bowl and set aside for 5 minutes to "sour".
  2. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a large mixing bowl. Whisk vanilla, egg and butter into "soured" milk. Pour the flour mixture into the wet ingredients and whisk until lumps are gone.
  3. Heat a large skillet over medium heat, and coat with cooking spray. Pour 1/4 cupfuls of batter onto the skillet, and cook until bubbles appear on the surface. Flip with a spatula, and cook until browned on the other side.
