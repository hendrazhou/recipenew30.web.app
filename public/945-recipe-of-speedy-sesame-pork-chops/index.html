The ingredients needed to make Sesame Pork Chops:
  1. Take 2 pork chops
  2. Make ready 1 tbsp toasted sesame seeds
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp ginger; minced
  4. Take 2 tsp onion powder
  5. Get 1 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  6. Take 1/2 tsp ground white pepper
  7. Make ready 1/2 cup ponzu ( lime soy sauce )
  8. Get 1/4 cup sesame oil
  9. Make ready 1 sesame oil; as needed
  10. Get 1 pinch salt
Instructions to make Sesame Pork Chops:
  1. Whisk together ponzu, sesame oil, and ginger. Marinate chops for 4-48 hours.
  2. Discard marinade. Pat dry. Toss with enough sesame oil to coat. Season.
  3. Bake at 350° for approximately 25 minutes or until pork reaches desired doneness.
