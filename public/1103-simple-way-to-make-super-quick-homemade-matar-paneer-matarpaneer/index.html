The ingredients needed to make Matar Paneer

#matarpaneer:

  1. Make ready 1 Big onion
  2. Prepare 2 medium tomatoes
  3. Take Ginger garlic crushed
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp Cumin seed
  5. Take 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  6. Prepare 1 Tsp Kashmiri chilli powder
  7. Get 1/2 Tsp normal chilli powder
  8. Take 1 Tsp Coriander Powder
  9. Get 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  10. Get 1/2 tsp Garam Masala
  11. Make ready 1/2 cup Cream or more
  12. Take 2 Green chilies
  13. Get Coriander leaves for garnish
  14. Make ready 250 gm Paneer
  15. Take 1/2 cup Peas
  16. Take butter / oil
  17. Take Kasuri Methi
Instructions to make Matar Paneer

#matarpaneer:

  1. Heat butter and oil in pan add cumin seed.. Add chopped onion sauté till soft,then add ginger garlic crushed sauté till raw smells leave, then add all dry ingredients turmeric powder,chilli powder,coriander powder,garam Masala,cumin powder cook till oil separate.
  2. Then add the tomatoes i also used some from tin tomatoes (optional) cook well. Then use the hand blender and just crush it don’t make it a purée just add little water according to the consistency.. Once cooked add the paneer and peas and cook for while.Add salt to taste
  3. Then add the Cream and kasuri Methi and cook for few minutes and add the slit green chilies cook for few minutes and garnish with coriander leaves..
