The ingredients needed to make Egg Tarts:
  1. Take 200 ml evaporated milk
  2. Prepare 4 eggs
  3. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla extract
  4. Prepare 75 ml hot water
  5. Prepare 75 grams sugar, or to taste
Instructions to make Egg Tarts:
  1. Make tart shells as below. - - https://cookpad.com/us/recipes/360469-tart-shells
  2. Melt the sugar in hot water and leave to cool completely.
  3. Whisk the eggs and evaporated milk together.
  4. Strain the egg mixture into the sugar water and stir well.
  5. Carefully pour the egg custard into the shells. Skim off any air bubbles on the surface.
  6. Bake in preheated oven at 170℃ for ten minutes. To check if the custard is done, insert a toothpick in the middle. If it remains upright, the egg tarts are done.
  7. Replace water with pandan water (boil water with pandan leaves until tinged green) and add a drop of colouring for Pandan Egg Tarts.
  8. Serve warm :)
