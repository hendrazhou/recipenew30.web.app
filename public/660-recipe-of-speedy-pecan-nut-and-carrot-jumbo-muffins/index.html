The ingredients needed to make Pecan nut and carrot jumbo muffins:
  1. Take 2 cup self raising flour
  2. Get 1/2 tsp bicarb soda
  3. Get 1/2 cup brown sugar
  4. Get 1/2 cup chopped pecan nuts
  5. Take 1 cup grated carrot
  6. Take 1 tbsp ground cinnamon
  7. Prepare 1/2 tbsp ground nutmeg
  8. Take 1 cup milk
  9. Take 2 each eggs
  10. Make ready 2 tbsp oil
  11. Take cooking spray

Instructions to make Pecan nut and carrot jumbo muffins:
  1. Pre heat oven to 200 C. Spray muffin tray with cooking spray. I used a jumbo muffin tray so only made 6 out of this batch.
  2. Add all ingredients together and mix well
  3. Pour into trays. I sprinked brown sugar and chopped pecan nuts on top. Put into oven and bake for 20 - 30 minutes or until a skewer comes out clean when testing
