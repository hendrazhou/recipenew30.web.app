The ingredients needed to make Paneer Tikka:
  1. Prepare 100 gm Paneer
  2. Take 2 Capsicum
  3. Get 2 Onion
  4. Take 2 Tomato
  5. Get 1 cup Curd
  6. Prepare Gram flour 2 tablespoon
  7. Take 1 teaspoon Red chili powder
  8. Get 1 teaspoon Garam masala powder
  9. Prepare 1 teaspoon Chaat masala
  10. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon Amchoor powder
  11. Make ready 1 teaspoon Oil
  12. Take Salt as per taste
  13. Get Half lemon

Instructions to make Paneer Tikka:
  1. Cut paneer, Capsicum, onion and tomato in a diced shape
  2. In a bowl add curd and gram flour
  3. Now add red chili powder, chaat masala, garam masala & amchoor powder
  4. Now add salt, lemon & oil
  5. Mix all well
  6. Now add paneer and veggies in a marination mixture
  7. Mixed it well and coat all the pieces.. Keep it for 30 minutes
  8. Heat non stick pan greasewith little oil.. arrange veggies and paneer in a skewer and place it on a hot tawa.. flip occasionally and grilled from all sides
  9. If you don't have skewers arrange veggies and paneer on a toothpick.. flip occasionally and grilled from all the sides.
  10. Its done serve with chutney and salad
