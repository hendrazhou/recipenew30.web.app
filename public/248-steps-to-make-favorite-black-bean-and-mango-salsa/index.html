The ingredients needed to make Black bean and Mango salsa:
  1. Make ready 1/2 cup black beans drained & rinsed
  2. Get 3 ripe mangoes diced
  3. Make ready 1 medium chopped tomato
  4. Take 1/2 cup onion
  5. Make ready 1/4 cup fresh coriander leaves
  6. Prepare 1/4 cup fresh lime juice
  7. Prepare 1/4 tsp salt and black pepper powder
  8. Prepare 1 chopped green chilli
