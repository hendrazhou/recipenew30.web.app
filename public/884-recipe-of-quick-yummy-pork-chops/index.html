The ingredients needed to make Yummy Pork chops:
  1. Make ready Pork chops
  2. Make ready Onions
  3. Make ready Mushrooms
  4. Prepare Peppers
  5. Prepare 2 tsp pink salt
  6. Prepare 2 tsp cracked black pepper
  7. Prepare 2 tsp no salt season
  8. Prepare 3 cloves garlic chopped
  9. Get 2 tblsp butter
  10. Prepare 1 tblsp olive oil
Steps to make Yummy Pork chops:
  1. Season your chops with all seasons and garlic
  2. Slice peppers onions and mushrooms
  3. Fry chops in nonstick pan in 2 tblsp of butter 1 tblsp olive oil
  4. When Chops are almost done add onion and peppers once onion is translucent add mushrooms cook till done
