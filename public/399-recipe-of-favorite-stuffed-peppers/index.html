Instructions to make Stuffed peppers:
  1. Take a pan and roast onion paste
  2. Now stir to make it browned
  3. Add all the spices together and add tomatoes paste with ginger garlic paste and green chilli paste
  4. Stir the masala for 6 to 7 minutes and add mashed and boiled potatoes
  5. Add sweet corn and peas too
  6. Mix the ingredients for 7 to 8 minutes
  7. Fill the mixture into peppers by cut from center
  8. Now take a flat pan where you can spread all the peppers
  9. Put some butter into pan at very low flame and spread all the peppers
  10. Keep it for 8 to 10 minutes
  11. Yummy stuffed peppers are ready
