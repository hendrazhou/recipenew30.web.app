Instructions to make Easy pork chops:
  1. Put potato chips in large zip lock bag crush with rolling pin
  2. Prepare 2 eggs for dipping chops
  3. Dip chops into beaten eggs then potato chips. Place on baker's rack with cookie sheet under.
  4. Add salt , pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder to taste.
  5. Bake at 350°F for about 45 minutes
