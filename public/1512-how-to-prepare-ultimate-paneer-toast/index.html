The ingredients needed to make Paneer Toast:
  1. Prepare 6-8 bread
  2. Prepare 100 gms Paneer
  3. Make ready 1 capsicum chopped
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp. butter
  5. Prepare 1 onion chopped
  6. Prepare 2-3 spoon tomato ketchup
  7. Make ready 1/2 spoon chilli sauce
  8. Take 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  9. Prepare to taste Salt

Steps to make Paneer Toast:
  1. Spread butter on bread.
  2. In a pan heat oil and roast these bread pieces until golden browns
  3. Heat oil and then add onions and also add capsicum and bread crumbs to it.
  4. Cook for few mins.
  5. Now add meshed paneer to the onion capsicum mixture.
  6. Mix it by adding salt and all sauces
  7. Put one spoon on each toasted circle and cook for another 15 mins.
