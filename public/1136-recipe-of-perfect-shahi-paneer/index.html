The ingredients needed to make Shahi paneer:
  1. Prepare 250 gms paneer
  2. Take 400 gms tomato
  3. Take 1 tbsp grated ginger
  4. Make ready 1 cup milk
  5. Get 2 tbsp fresh malai
  6. Take 3-4 green chilies finely chopped
  7. Take 1 tsp jeera
  8. Prepare 1/2 tsp. turmeric powder
  9. Take 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  10. Prepare 1/2 tbsp shahi paneer masala
  11. Make ready 2-3 tsp oil
  12. Take 1 pinch garam masala
  13. Get as par taste Salt
