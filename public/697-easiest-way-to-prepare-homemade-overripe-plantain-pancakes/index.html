The ingredients needed to make Overripe plantain pancakes:
  1. Take 1 over-ripe plantain
  2. Take 1 & half cups flour
  3. Take 1/2 tsp salt
  4. Take 1/4 cup sugar
  5. Make ready 11/4 cup milk
  6. Get 2 eggs
  7. Prepare Cooking spray or veg oil to grease the pan

Steps to make Overripe plantain pancakes:
  1. Cut and remove plantain from the peel, wash and cut in small bits into your blender cup.
  2. Add in the eggs and blend till all blended
  3. Add in milk, flour, sugar, salt and blend everything together till its properly mixed
  4. Pour barter into a bowl, then heat up your frying pan. Once its hot, spray your cooking spray or grease pan with vegetable oil
