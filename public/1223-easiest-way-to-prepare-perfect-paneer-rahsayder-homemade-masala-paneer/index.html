The ingredients needed to make Paneer rahsayder (homemade Masala paneer):
  1. Take 750 ml milk
  2. Prepare 1 tbsp daniya whole
  3. Make ready 1 tbsp Jeera whole
  4. Prepare 2 dry red chillies
  5. Take 3 tbsp coriander leaves chopped
  6. Get 1 nos ginger paste
  7. Get as required Salt
  8. Prepare 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  9. Prepare 2 tbsp kasuri methi
  10. Take 1 cup curd (small cup)
  11. Take 1 tomato paste
