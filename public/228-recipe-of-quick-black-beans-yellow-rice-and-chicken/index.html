Steps to make Black Beans Yellow Rice and Chicken:
  1. Boil the water with the onion powder, garlic powder, pepper, and salt. Add the chicken pieces the skin will be on, and bone in. Cook till done.
  2. Remove the skins and remove the meat from the bones.
  3. Use the chicken broth made from boiling your chicken. Cook the rice in the broth as instructed on packet. It said add 2 tablespoons of olive oil.
  4. Add the peas and chicken meat to the rice and mix.
  5. Make the black beans.
  6. Serve with black beans on top.
