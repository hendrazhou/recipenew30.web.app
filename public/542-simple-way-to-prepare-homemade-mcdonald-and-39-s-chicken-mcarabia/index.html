The ingredients needed to make McDonald's Chicken Mcarabia:
  1. Make ready Dough Ingredients
  2. Get 2 cups Plain flour
  3. Take 1/2 tsp Salt
  4. Prepare 1 tsp Yeast
  5. Get 2 tbsp Milk powder
  6. Take 1 tsp Sugar
  7. Prepare 1 tsp Nigella seeds
  8. Make ready Warm water as you required
  9. Take 2 tbsp Oil
  10. Get Patty Ingredients
  11. Take 1/2 kg Boneless chicken
  12. Make ready Garlic cloves 3
  13. Make ready 1/2 tsp Paprika powder
  14. Take 1 tsp Salt
  15. Take 1/2 tsp Mustard powder
  16. Prepare 1 tsp Black pepper powder
  17. Prepare 1 Egg
  18. Take 1/4 cup Bread crumbs
  19. Take Other Ingredients
  20. Prepare Iceberg lettuce as you require
  21. Get Tomato as you require
  22. Make ready Cucumber as you require
Steps to make McDonald's Chicken Mcarabia:
  1. Take a bowl and add plain flour,salt,yeast,milk powder,sugar,nigella seeds, and mix it well and then add oil and mix it well and knead with warm water and then rest it for 2 hours at room temperature with cover.
  2. Now take a chopper machine and add boneless chicken,garlic cloves,paprika powder,salt,mustard,black pepper powder,egg,bread crumbs and chop it well and then make patty with this and then shallow fry them on pan/tawa. Now your chicken patty are ready.
  3. Now remove the cover and punch the dough and make small balls of the dough and then take a ball and rolling it and cook on the tawa/pan from both sides. Make all your breads as you require.
  4. Now take a bread and spread mayonnaise/ketchup as you like then place a iceberg lettuce,chicken patty, tomato,cucumber and fold it. McDonald's Chicken Mcarabia is ready.
