The ingredients needed to make Sweet & Spicy Pork Chops:
  1. Get 2 pork chops; 1/2 inch thick
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup orange juice
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup brown sugar
  4. Take 2/3 cup orange marmalade
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  6. Prepare 1 pinch red pepper flakes
  7. Make ready 1 tsp onion powder
  8. Make ready 1 tsp garlic powder
  9. Prepare 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  10. Prepare 1/2 tsp paprika
  11. Take 1/4 tsp ground white pepper
  12. Prepare 1 pinch salt

Instructions to make Sweet & Spicy Pork Chops:
  1. Whisk orange juice and brown sugar together. Marinate pork for 4 hours. Pat Dry
  2. Heat marmalade, vinegar, and red pepper flakes in a small saute pan until consistency is smooth.
  3. Season chops with onion & garlic powder, paprika, cayenne, salt, and pepper.
  4. Add chops to pan. Baste with sauce. Cover and bake at 300° for approximately twenty minutes or until desired doneness. Flip chops halfway through cooking time.
