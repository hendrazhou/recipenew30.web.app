The ingredients needed to make Veggie paneer sandwich:
  1. Get 150 gm paneer
  2. Make ready 2 tbsp cucumber
  3. Take 2 tbsp carrot
  4. Get 2 tbsp onion
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp tomato
  6. Get 1 tsp mint leaves
  7. Take 1 tsp Green chilli paste
  8. Make ready to taste Salt
  9. Make ready 1/2 tsp Black pepper powder
  10. Make ready 1/2 tsp mixed herbs
  11. Take 2 tsp Mint chutney
  12. Get 2 tbsp butter
  13. Get 4 Bread slices
