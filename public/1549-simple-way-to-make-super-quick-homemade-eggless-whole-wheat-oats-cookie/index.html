The ingredients needed to make Eggless whole wheat oats cookie:
  1. Prepare 3/4 cup whole wheat flour
  2. Get 3/4 cup grinded oats
  3. Make ready 1 tbsp baking soda
  4. Make ready 1/4 grinded cashew powder
  5. Make ready 1 tbsp coconut powder
  6. Take 1 tbsp coco powder
  7. Get 1 tbsp flex seed soaked in 3 tbsp warm milk for 10 mins
  8. Make ready 1/4 cup olive oil
  9. Make ready 3/4 cup brown sugar
  10. Make ready 1/4 cup chocochips

Steps to make Eggless whole wheat oats cookie:
  1. Sift together whole wheat flour,oats powder, coconut powder,coco powder,baking soda and cashew powder
  2. Mix and keep aside
  3. After 10 mins of soaking flex meal add oil and sugar…mix well till sugar melts
  4. Mix wet and dry ingredients well…add little milk if required…form a dough..do not knead much…add chocochips to it…mix well
  5. Heat oven at 350f
  6. Place lemon size balls flattened a bit on cookie sheet lined with parchment paper…keep gap in between balls as they rise
  7. Bake for about 12to 15 mins
  8. Take out and let them cool for 10 mins…enjoy
