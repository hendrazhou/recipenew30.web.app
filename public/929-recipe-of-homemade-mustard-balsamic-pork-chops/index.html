The ingredients needed to make Mustard Balsamic Pork Chops:
  1. Prepare 6 Center Cut Pork Chops
  2. Get 2 Tbsp Mustard (grainy)
  3. Take 1/4 Cup Balsamic Vinegar
  4. Take 1/4 cup Olive or Vegetable oil
  5. Take 2 Tbsp cup Mustard (grainy)
  6. Get 1 Tbsp Rosemary (Fresh or Dry)
  7. Take 2 Tsp Salt
  8. Get 2 Tsp Black pepper
  9. Get 2 Tsp Garlic Powder

Instructions to make Mustard Balsamic Pork Chops:
  1. To begin, preheat oven to 425°. Next, mix salt, pepper and garlic powder in a small mixing bowl and season generously on both sides of the pork chops.
  2. In another mixing bowl, add your Balsamic vinegar, mustard, oil and rosemary and whisk together until all ingredients are mixed well together.
  3. In a large casserole dish, place pork chops and cover them in the balsamic and mustard mixture and place in the oven uncovered for 45 minutes or until cooked through.
  4. Once finished, garnish with fresh parsley and serve w/ rice or mashed potatoes (personal preference).
