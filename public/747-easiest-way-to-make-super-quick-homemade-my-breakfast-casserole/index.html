The ingredients needed to make My breakfast casserole:
  1. Get 6 eggs scrambled
  2. Make ready 1 splash of milk
  3. Prepare 2 splashes of soy sauce
  4. Prepare 1 tator tots
  5. Take 4 clove garlic minced
  6. Make ready 1 small onion, chopped
  7. Make ready 1 any shredded cheese I used colby and jack
  8. Take 1 lb turkey bacon
  9. Get 1 McCormick bacon and chives spice
  10. Take 1 olive oil cooking spray

Steps to make My breakfast casserole:
  1. Set oven at 350'
  2. In s 9/12 casserole dish spray with cooking spray .put in tots to cover bottom, spread the garlic then a layer of cheese. Layer on Bacon
  3. Add milk and soy sauce to scrambled eggs .shake on Bacon and. Chives spice ,spread onions then pour on the eggs .layer more Bacon then top with cheese
  4. Cover with foil and foke holes in foil for steam to escape and bake for 1hr .
  5. Carefully take foil off and eat
