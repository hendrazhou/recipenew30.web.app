The ingredients needed to make Baked Parmesan Pork Chops:
  1. Take 1 large pack boneless pork chops
  2. Prepare 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  3. Prepare 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  4. Get 1 tablespoon onion powder
  5. Prepare 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  6. Get 1/2 tablespoon parsley flakes
  7. Prepare 1 cup Parmesan cheese
  8. Get Cooking spray
Steps to make Baked Parmesan Pork Chops:
  1. Rinse and pat dry pork chops……Trim off fat if necessary…
  2. Place chops in a large baking pan that has been lightly sprayed with cooking spray……
  3. Combine in a bowl : black pepper, Italian seasoning, onion powder, garlic powder, parsley flakes and Parmesan cheese, mix well……
  4. Lightly spray both sides of pork chops with cooking spray……
  5. Sprinkle dry Parmesan ingredients over pork chops, both sides….Pat down ingredients into chops…..Spray lightly again….
  6. Bake chops, uncovered, at 400 degrees for 30 minutes…
  7. Serve and enjoy 😉!
