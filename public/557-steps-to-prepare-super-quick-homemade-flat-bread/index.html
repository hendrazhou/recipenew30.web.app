Instructions to make Flat bread:
  1. Conbine the milk and oil
  2. Combine salt and flour
  3. Add in the flour and start mixing until it forms a dough
  4. Break into 3 pieces and roll until thin and flat
  5. Heat and pan and spray with spray and cook. Fry on one side and turn when bubbles form
  6. Flip and fry until brown spots form
  7. Serve immediately or keep warm until ready to serve
