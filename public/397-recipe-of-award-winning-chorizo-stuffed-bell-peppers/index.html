The ingredients needed to make Chorizo Stuffed Bell Peppers:
  1. Get 6 Bell Peppers
  2. Make ready 1/2-1 pound chorizo
  3. Prepare 1 small potato (optional)
  4. Make ready 1 small onion
  5. Make ready 4 cloves minced garlic
  6. Take To taste salt & pepper
  7. Take 1 tbspn Worcestershire sauce
  8. Prepare 1 bushel cilantro
  9. Prepare 1/2 cup sliced basil
  10. Take 1 can black or red beans
  11. Prepare 1 can hot rotel (or equivalent)
  12. Get 1/2 cup rice
  13. Make ready 1/2 cup Habanero Jack Shredded cheese
  14. Take 1 1/4 cup Mexican Blend Shredded cheese
