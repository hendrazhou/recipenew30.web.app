The ingredients needed to make Best Banana Peppers Bisque:
  1. Prepare 2 T Vegetable Oil
  2. Take 1 t Garlic Powder
  3. Prepare 1 t Dried Cilantro
  4. Get 5 Cleaned Sliced Fresh Banana Peppers
  5. Get 1 can Chicken Broth
  6. Prepare 1 can Condensed Cream of Mushroom Soup
  7. Prepare 1 Vegetable Beef-Style Boullion Cube
  8. Prepare 1/8 t Ground Lemongrass
  9. Make ready 1/8 t Ground Tumeric
  10. Make ready 1/8 t Ground Pepper
  11. Get 1/2 T Ground Ginger
  12. Take 1 large Bay Leaf
  13. Make ready 1 can Creamed Style Corn
  14. Prepare 1/4 c Milk
  15. Get 5 Chopped Dried Apricots
  16. Make ready 3 drops the Vinegar Water from top of prepared Yellow Mustard
  17. Take Dash Salt

Steps to make Best Banana Peppers Bisque:
  1. Warm garlic and cilantro in the oil in a large pot
  2. Add chopped peppers and saute
  3. Add Broth and simmer for 5 minutes
  4. Stir in Mushroom Soup and boullion
  5. Add Lemongrass, Tumeric, Black Pepper, Ginger and Bay Leaf. Simmer 2 minutes stirring frequently
  6. Stir in Corn simmer 3 minutes
  7. On low heat add milk and stir
  8. Add Apricots and stir.
  9. Cook on a low setting for a minute stirring as needed
  10. Add drops now and stir on low for a couple minutes. This makes the milk creamier and blends everything better
  11. Cool down a little to serving temperate. Remove Bay Leaf. Serve in bowls with a dash of salt.
