The ingredients needed to make Salt and Pepper Chicken Ribs:
  1. Take 1 package chicken ribs appox 1 kg
  2. Get 5 cloves garlic, minced
  3. Prepare salt and pepper
  4. Prepare 3 teaspoon chicken powder
  5. Prepare sesame oil
  6. Take flour
  7. Get spring onion
  8. Prepare chrushed blackpepper
Steps to make Salt and Pepper Chicken Ribs:
  1. Marinate chicken ribs with minced garlic,salt pepper,chicken powder, and sesame oil for about 1-2 hours. or overnight for a better taste
  2. Next, coat thr marinated chicken ribs with flour and deep fry in canola or vagetable oil in medium to high heat until cooked in golden brown colour
  3. Garnish with chrushed blackpepper and chopped spring onion. the best way to serve with hot rice, and dip in a soy sauce and chilli, yummm!
