The ingredients needed to make Eggless Wholewheat Oat Cookie Pups:
  1. Get Ingredients:
  2. Get 100 gm (1/2 cup) butter at room temperature
  3. Take 220 gm (1 cup) castor sugar or powdered sugar
  4. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla essence
  5. Get 2 tbsp milk
  6. Get 100 gm (1 cup) oats
  7. Take 140 gm (1 cup) whole wheat or plain flour
  8. Get 1 tsp cinnamon powder
  9. Take 1 tsp baking soda
  10. Make ready 40 pieces Chocos (I used Kellogg’s Chocos)
  11. Make ready 60 piece Choco Chips

Instructions to make Eggless Wholewheat Oat Cookie Pups:
  1. In a large bowl put in the butter, sugar, vanilla and milk and beat until smooth and creamy.
  2. In another bowl, assemble all the dry ingredients, the oats, flour, cinnamon powder and baking soda and mix well.
  3. Mix in the dry ingredients into the butter mixture and knead briefly until the cookie dough comes together.
  4. Preheat the oven at 175 C.
  5. Divide the mixture into equal parts and form into round balls.Place on a greased or non stick cookie tray at a distance from each other. - Now, take Chocos or any other cereal of your choice.
