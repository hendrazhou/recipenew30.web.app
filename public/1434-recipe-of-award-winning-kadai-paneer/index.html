The ingredients needed to make Kadai paneer:
  1. Make ready 250 gm paneer
  2. Take 2 onion
  3. Take 2 tomatoes
  4. Make ready 1/2 inch ginger
  5. Prepare 2 spoon coriander seeds
  6. Take 5 dry red chilli
  7. Take as per taste Salt
  8. Get 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  9. Prepare 2 capsicum
  10. Make ready 1 teaspoon malai

Instructions to make Kadai paneer:
  1. Roast coriander seeds and whole Red chilli in a pan.. and keep it aside.
  2. Make a fine paste of onion, tomatoes and ginger.
  3. Take a pan add oil and add cumin seeds.
  4. Add fine paste and roast it.
  5. Grind roasted coriander seeds and whole Red chilli into a powder.
  6. Add this powder in the pan and mix it well.
  7. Now add salt, and finely chopped capsicum.
  8. Add water and cook it until capsicum becomes soft.
  9. Now add malai and cook it well until it leaves oil.
  10. Now check the consistency if it looks thick then add boiled water.
  11. Now add paneer cubes and garnish it with coriander.
  12. Serve it hot with chapatis.
