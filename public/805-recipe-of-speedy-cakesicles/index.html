Steps to make Cakesicles:
  1. Crumble chocolate cake add chocolate ganache and set in cakesicle mould, refrigerate for 30 minutes
  2. Crumble vanilla cake add white Ganache and set in cakesicle mould, refrigerate for 30 minutes
  3. After 30 minutes remove from mould and dip in white and dark chocolate
  4. Drizzle andDecorate it with sprinklers
