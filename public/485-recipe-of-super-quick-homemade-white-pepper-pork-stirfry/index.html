The ingredients needed to make White pepper Pork Stirfry:
  1. Make ready 2-1/2 pounds pork loin
  2. Get 1 pound mixed vegetables
  3. Take 1 teaspoon ground white pepper
  4. Get 1-1/2 teaspoon salt
  5. Make ready 2 stalks celery thinly sliced
  6. Take 1/2 large onion diced
  7. Get 2 teaspoon granulated garlic powder
  8. Take 1/3 cup diced green bell pepper
  9. Take 1-1/2 teaspoon sesame oil
  10. Prepare 1/4 cup Chinese black vinegar
Steps to make White pepper Pork Stirfry:
  1. Mix the bell pepper, pork, onion and spices.
  2. Stirfry the pork mixture. Cook till just done.
  3. Add Chinese black vinegar and sesame oil.
  4. Add vegetables. Cook till vegetables are at your desired doneness.
  5. Serve I hope you enjoy!
