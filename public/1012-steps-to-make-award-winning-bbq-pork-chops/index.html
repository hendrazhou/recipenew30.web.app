Instructions to make BBQ pork chops:
  1. In a medium sauce pan add onion, cayenne, 1tsp salt, tomato, brown sugar and ketchup. Cook on medium heat for 25- 30 minutes
  2. Rub pork chops with salt and pepper. In a medium saute pan add oil heat on medium heat for 2-3 minutes. Add pork to oil, cook for 2-3 minutes per side.
  3. Remove pork from pan poor BBQ sauce over pork.
  4. Serve.
