The ingredients needed to make Cheese,Pepper And Salt,Chilli Garlic and Mayo Potato Tornadoes:
  1. Get Main Ingredients
  2. Prepare 4 Potatoes (medium sized)
  3. Make ready 1 big bowl full of Cold water
  4. Prepare 4 Sashlik Sticks
  5. Take Oil For deep frying
  6. Make ready For Batter Preparation
  7. Make ready 1 cup Plain Flour
  8. Prepare 1/2 tsp Baking Powder
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp Vinegar
  10. Take 1 tbsp Oil
  11. Prepare 1-1/2 cup Water (or as Required)
  12. Get Ingredients For Cheese Flavor
  13. Take 1 packet Cheetos (Snacks)
  14. Make ready Ingredients For Pepper And Salt Flavor
  15. Prepare 1 packet Potato Sticks (Snacks)
  16. Get Ingredients For Chilli Garlic Flavor
  17. Take Chilli Garlic Ketchup (As Required)
  18. Take Chat Masala (As Required)
  19. Take Ingredients For Mayo Flavor
  20. Take Mayonnaise (As Required)
Steps to make Cheese,Pepper And Salt,Chilli Garlic and Mayo Potato Tornadoes:
  1. First of all,wash and peel all the potatoes.
  2. Ninja Technique for Cutting Spiral Shaped Potatoes without any machine: Place a cutting board on a table or shelf.Place the potato near the edge of cutting board as shown in pic.(I have made video but cant post because i dont have iphone)
  3. Now cut straight thin slices of potatoes halfway only (untill your knife touches the board) as shown in pic.
  4. Now turn the potato.Again place near the edge of cutting board. This time cut the thin slices halfway (until your knife touches board) in "/" this titled shape.
  5. Hurrah We have now got tornado or spiral shaped potato without any machine.
  6. Now carefully insert a Shashlik stick all the way through the potato longitudinally. Then with the help of your hand separate each spiral to a little distance.Dont try too hard other wise spirals will break.
  7. Do the same method with other 3 potatoes and put them in a bowl full of cold water as shown in pic.Let them soak in water for about 20-25 minutes so that they starch will vanish away.
  8. For batter preparation,take a bowl and add plain flour,baking powder,oil and vinegar.
  9. Mix them and now slowly add water in it and at the same time keep wisking it with hand whisk so that no lumps can form. Keep the consistency thin like water.
  10. Now place one spiral in batter bowl and soak the spiral in batter with the help of spoon.
  11. Do the same with all the potatoes.
  12. Now for frying, heat a pan full of oil. Keep the flame high. When oil becomes too hot, place one or two tornadoes in pan depending on the size of pan,make sure your tornadoes must dip wholly in the oil.
  13. Wait till one side gets brown and crispy,then do the same with the other side.
  14. As soon as you remove the tornado from pan, do the following procedures.(Flavor pic given)
  15. For Cheese Flavor,Crush the cheetos snacks into a fine powder. Sprinkle the powder over the hot tornadoe. Our Cheesy Potato Tornado is ready.
  16. For Pepper And Salt Flavor,Crush the potato sticks snacks into a fine powder. Sprinkle the powder over the hot tornadoe. Our Pepper and Salt Potato Tornado is ready.
  17. For Chilli Garlic Flavor, Sprinkle the chat masla over the hot tornadoe. Then spread chilli garlic ketchup over it.Our Chilli Garlic Potato Tornado is ready.
  18. For Mayo Flavor,Spread the mayonnaise over the hot tornadoe. Our Cheesy Potato Tornado is ready.
  19. Yummy Cheese, Pepper And Salt,Chilli Garlic and Mayo Potato Tornadoes are ready.You can make Masala Lays and Kurkure flavor too. Eat and Enjoy.
