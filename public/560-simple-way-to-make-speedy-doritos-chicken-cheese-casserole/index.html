The ingredients needed to make Doritos chicken cheese casserole:
  1. Take Cooking spray
  2. Get 1 (14.5 ounces) nacho cheese doritos, crushed
  3. Take 3 cups chopped chicken
  4. Get 1 (15.25 ounce) can corn, drained
  5. Make ready 2 cups shredded Mexican blend cheese, divided
  6. Prepare 1 1/2 cups salsa
  7. Get 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of mushroom soup
  8. Take 1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup
  9. Prepare 8 ounces sour cream

Instructions to make Doritos chicken cheese casserole:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly spray a 9x13-inch baking dish with cooking spray.
  2. Spread crushed chips out into the bottom of the prepared baking dish.
  3. Stir chicken, 1 cup Mexican cheese blend, salsa, cream of mushroom soup, cream of chicken soup, and sour cream together in a bowl. Pour chicken mixture over crushed chips into the baking dish.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes. Spread remaining 1 cup cheese over the casserole and bake until cheese is melted and bubbling, about 5 minutes more.
