The ingredients needed to make Honey Soy Glaze Salmon:
  1. Prepare Sauce
  2. Get 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  3. Prepare 1 tablespoon honey
  4. Prepare 2 cloves mince garlic
  5. Take Water
  6. Prepare Salmon
  7. Prepare Olive oil
  8. Get Pinch salt and pepper

Steps to make Honey Soy Glaze Salmon:
  1. Rub olive oil, salt and pepper on both side of salmon, pan grill over medium heat until cooked. Don't need to wash pan.
  2. Combine ingredient for sauce in a bowl, mix evenly
  3. Pour mixed sauce ingredients into the pan, it will have some leftover oil from the salmon you grilled earlier, that's the good stuff. Let it simmer over low heat and stir constantly until sauce thickens, pour over salmon.
