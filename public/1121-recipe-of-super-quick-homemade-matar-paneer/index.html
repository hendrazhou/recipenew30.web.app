The ingredients needed to make Matar paneer:
  1. Prepare 200 gms Paneer
  2. Get 200 gms Peas
  3. Get 1 big Onion
  4. Get 2 big size Tomato
  5. Prepare as required Salt, coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chilli powder
  6. Make ready 2 spoons Oil
  7. Prepare 1 spoon cumin seeds
  8. Prepare 1/2 small spoon Garam masala

Instructions to make Matar paneer:
  1. First chopped tomatoes and onion finely. Boil the peas or take frozen and cut paneer into cubes.
  2. Now take a kadhai to add oil. When oil heated law the flame adds cumin then chopped onion.when onion turn golden add dry spices. Now add tomatoes and saute them.
  3. Now when the oil separates from the gravy add salt, paneer, and peas to it give a boil for 3 minutes on low flame. Mattar paneer is ready to serve.
