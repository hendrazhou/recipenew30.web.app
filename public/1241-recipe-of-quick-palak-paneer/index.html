The ingredients needed to make Palak paneer:
  1. Get 250 gms palak
  2. Take 200 gms paneer
  3. Make ready 2 tbsp butter
  4. Get 2 tsp jeera powder
  5. Take 1 tsp gorom masala
  6. Prepare 1-2 tsp Salt
  7. Get 1 onion
  8. Take 1 tomato
  9. Take 1 tsp turmeric powder
  10. Take 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  11. Get 2-3 green chillies, chopped

Instructions to make Palak paneer:
  1. Wash and cut the palak.
  2. Steam it in a pressure cooker.
  3. Or, microwave it for 30 seconds.
  4. Cool it and grind it.
  5. Heat a kadai with butter or refined oil.
  6. Add a tejpatta.
  7. Fry onion slices until brown.
  8. Add tomato.
  9. Add turmeric powder.
  10. Add ginger-garlic paste.
  11. Add a pinch of sugar for batter taste.
  12. And at the last add jeera, garam masala powder.
  13. Serve this lovely recipe with your roti and rice.
