The ingredients needed to make Baked pork chops:

  2. Take 1 cup shurfine or any kind of bread crumbs
  3. Get 1 tbsp seasoned salt
  4. Prepare 1 tsp parmesan cheese
  5. Get 1 pinch salt and pepper

Steps to make Baked pork chops:
  1. Preheat oven to 375° Pour a cup of bread crumbs into a bowl, add salt/pepper, seasoned salt, and parmesan. Mix around
  2. Rinse pork chops, trim fat. Prepare a pan with some cooking spray. (I used aluminum foil wrappers).
  3. Toss pork chops in mixture, coat well. Put onto pan and throw in the oven.
  4. Put pan into the oven for about 35 minutes turn halfway (17 mins) check pork chops to see if done. If not time it for another 10 minutes. Enjoy!
