The ingredients needed to make Black Pepper Pork Ribs:
  1. Prepare 4 pork rib / beef / lamb
  2. Get lg black pepper sauce
  3. Prepare 1 bottle black pepper sauce (300 gram), with 1 cup of water (just double all the amount for large portion of pork rib)
  4. Make ready Optional add on pounded paste
  5. Get 2 each tbsp ginger, shallot and ginger or 2 large onion
Steps to make Black Pepper Pork Ribs:
  1. It's very important to remove the membrane of the rib first
  2. Marinate rib with sauce (with optional add on pounded paste) for 1 or 2 hours or just bake it after marinating
  3. Put all ingredients and bake it on a baking tray or aluminium foil at 200 C for 2 hours or more depending on the thickness of the pork rib, cover top with aluminium foil. Turning over and basting with its juice after 1 hour then bake again
  4. Once it's done,remove the top foil and with its sauce and bake for another 10 minutes till its sauce start thicken,rest it for few minutes then slice up and enjoy
  5. Today,……. I Remake 4x8 inches Pork Rib Black Pepper Sauce With Onion
  6. ANOTHER OPTION….For Pork Belly Baking Time About 1 Hour Or 1 1/2 Hour, Once Cooked Then Remember To removed the The skin (as its rubbery) then slice it up and Enjoy
