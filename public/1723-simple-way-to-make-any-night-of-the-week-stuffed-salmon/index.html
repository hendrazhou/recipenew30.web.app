The ingredients needed to make Stuffed Salmon:
  1. Get 1 lb Salmon
  2. Take 1 cup cream cheese
  3. Prepare 1 cup Parmesan Cheese
  4. Get 2 cups Spinach
  5. Take 1/2 cup cooked bacon
  6. Prepare 2 cups cooked shrimp
  7. Prepare 2 tbsp blackened seasoning
  8. Make ready Teaspoon salt
Steps to make Stuffed Salmon:
  1. Cut salmon in half and pat dry
  2. Prepare stuffing mix well and season with Cajun seasoning
  3. Season salmon on both sides with salt and blackened seasoning
  4. Insert mix into salmon and fold over
  5. Prepare pan with drizzle of oil; Medium. Heat.
  6. Preheat oven 350 degrees
  7. Laying salmon in pan and sear Both sides.
  8. Place in oven for 10-15 minutes
  9. Voila'
