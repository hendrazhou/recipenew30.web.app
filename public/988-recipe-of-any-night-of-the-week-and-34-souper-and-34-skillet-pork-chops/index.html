The ingredients needed to make "Souper" Skillet Pork Chops:
  1. Make ready 1 tbsp Vegetable Oil
  2. Take 4 Pork Chops (I used boneless loin, any will do)
  3. Get 1 can Tomato Soup
  4. Take 1 tbsp Packed Brown Sugar
  5. Make ready 2 tbsp Worcestershire Sauce
  6. Get 2 tbsp Apple Cider Vinegar
  7. Get 1 tsp Liquid Smoke
  8. Take 1/2 cup Onion Diced
  9. Take 1/2 tsp Garlic Powder
  10. Take Black Pepper
Steps to make "Souper" Skillet Pork Chops:
  1. Heat oil in large skillet. Add the pork Chops and cook until lightly browned.
  2. Meanwhile in a bowl, whisk together remaining ingredients.
  3. Pour over pork chops. Bring to boil, reduce heat and simmer (spooning sauce over chops once in a while) for about 20-25 minutes or until done. Enjoy!
