The ingredients needed to make Hot dog Casserole:
  1. Get 6 hot dogs, cut into bite size pieces
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup onion, chopped
  3. Take 1/2 tsp oregano
  4. Get 2 tbsp butter
  5. Make ready 2 cup egg noodles, cooked
  6. Take 1 can cream of celery soup
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup milk
  8. Take 2 tbsp parsley
  9. Get bread crumbs
  10. Make ready cooking spray

Instructions to make Hot dog Casserole:
  1. Start water to make egg noodles while cutting up hot dogs and onions. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Brown in skillet: hot dogs, onion, oregano, and butter. Stir frequently to keep from burning. (When hot dogs appear cooked, its generally done) Add noodles to water when water comes to a boil.
  3. Drain noodles and combine in a greased casserole dish with: all ingredients in skillet, cream of celery soup, milk, and parsley. Mix together well.
  4. Sprinkle bread crumbs across the top to keep mixture from burning.
  5. Cook covered for 40-45 minutes at 350°F.
