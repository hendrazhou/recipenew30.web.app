The ingredients needed to make Chicken cheese and pepper tortillas:
  1. Make ready 900 g chicken breast
  2. Take 2 tbsp salt
  3. Make ready 1 tsp black pepper
  4. Get 1 tsp chilli powder
  5. Get 2 tbsp dried parsley
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp BBQ seasoning
  7. Take 2 tbsp Maggi liquid seasoning
  8. Make ready 2 tbsp salt
  9. Make ready 3 tbsp BBQ sauce
  10. Get 2 eggs
  11. Take Flour
  12. Make ready 1 large Onion
  13. Make ready 1 red and 1 green pepper
  14. Prepare Brown sugar
  15. Make ready 3 packs tortillas

Instructions to make Chicken cheese and pepper tortillas:
  1. Slice the chicken breast thin
  2. Add the seasonings
  4. Add the BBQ sauce and mix together
  5. Crack the eggs in a bowl and whisk
  6. Add some flour salt and black pepper on another plate
  7. Dip the chicken in the egg then the flour
  8. Put in oil on a medium heat. Should only take a short amount of time to cook. Flip when golden brown on the bottom
  9. Remove from the pan when cooked
  10. Make sure the pan only has a small amount of oil left and add the onions and pepper along with some brown sugar and salt until soft
  11. Turn the pan to a low heat and add a small amount of oil. Then place a tortilla inside
  12. Place the chicken then the onions and pepper, then the cheese on the tortilla. Then add another tortilla on top
  13. Press down the edges hard with a sauce pan lid to seal them.
  14. Once the cheese has melted flip over. It should only take around 2 minutes or less. Then remove from the pan.
  15. Enjoy
