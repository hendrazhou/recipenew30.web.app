The ingredients needed to make Corn and Black bean salsa:
  1. Get 3 ears fresh corn
  2. Take 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  3. Prepare 1 tomatoe, seeded and diced
  4. Prepare 1 shallot, finely diced
  5. Take 2 cloves garlic, grated or finely chopped
  6. Take Juice of 1 lime
  7. Get 2 Tbs finely chopped cilantro
  8. Take 1 tsp hot sauce
  9. Make ready Salt and pepper

Steps to make Corn and Black bean salsa:
  1. (Optional) Roast corn on indoor or outdoor grill
  2. Remove corn from cobs
  3. Combine corn, beans, tomatoe, shallots, garlic, cilantro and hot sauce in a large bowl
  4. Add lime juice about 1 Tbs at a time. Taste in between till it is to your liking.
  5. Add salt and pepper to taste.
