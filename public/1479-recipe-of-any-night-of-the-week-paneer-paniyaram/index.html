The ingredients needed to make Paneer paniyaram:
  1. Take 200 grams Grated paneer
  2. Take 1 finely Chopped onion
  3. Get 1 Grated carrot(optional)
  4. Make ready as required Salt
  5. Make ready 1 Chilli Finely chopped
  6. Get 1/2 tsp Chilli powder
  7. Get 1/2 tsp Grated ginger
