The ingredients needed to make Grilled center cut pork chop with sweet&sour pineapples, saffron rice and garlic spinach tomato saut:
  1. Prepare 1 center cut pork chop
  2. Prepare 3 tsp salt
  3. Take 3 tsp black pepper
  4. Make ready 3 tsp paprika
  5. Make ready 1/2 cup sweet&sour sauce
  6. Take 1/2 cup diced pineapples
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp butter
  8. Prepare 1 packages yellow rice saffron

  10. Take 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes
  11. Get 2 tsp garlic cloves minced

Steps to make Grilled center cut pork chop with sweet&sour pineapples, saffron rice and garlic spinach tomato saut:

  2. On the stove heat grill pan until nice and hot
  3. Season pork chop with half the Salt&pepper and all of the paprika
  4. Put chops on hot grill cook 4 -5 minutes on each side depending on how thick they are
