The ingredients needed to make Boneless Root Beer Pork Ribs:
  1. Prepare 8 thick finger sized boneless country pork ribs
  2. Get 2 cup root beer
  3. Take 1 tbsp malt vinegar
  4. Make ready 2 tsp onion powder
  5. Prepare 2 tsp garlic powder
  6. Get 1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  7. Take 1/2 tsp ground cardamom
  8. Prepare 1/2 tsp ground coriander seed
  9. Prepare 1/2 tsp white pepper
  10. Take 3/4 cup rum
  11. Take 2/3 cup bbq sauce
  12. Make ready 1 pinch salt
  13. Get 1 vegetable oil; as needed
Steps to make Boneless Root Beer Pork Ribs:
  1. Marinate pork in one cup of root beer for 4-48 hours. Drain and pat dry.
  2. Reduce rum in a saucepan by 1/2. Be careful of the flames! Add one cup of root beer, vanilla extract, and vinegar. Reduce by 1/2. Add bbq sauce and stir to incorporate.
  3. Place pork in a large mixing bowl. Toss with enough oil to cover. Add dried spices. Grill until pork reaches desired doneness. Brush with bbq sauce.
