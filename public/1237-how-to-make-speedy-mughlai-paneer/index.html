The ingredients needed to make Mughlai paneer:
  1. Get 100 grams paneer
  2. Get 4 tsp ghee
  3. Make ready 2 medium sized onions
  4. Make ready 8 cloves garlic
  5. Prepare 1 inch piece ginger
  6. Prepare 10-12 pieces sabut pepper
  7. Make ready 2 small cardamoms
  8. Take 1 tsp cumin seeds
  9. Make ready 10 pieces almond & 6 piece cashwenut
  10. Prepare 1 pinch cinnamon powder
  11. Prepare Half cup milk
  12. Prepare 4 tsp malai or cream
  13. Get 2 tsp curd
  14. Get 1 teaspoon garam masala
  15. Make ready 2 bay leaves

Instructions to make Mughlai paneer:
  1. Roughly chopped onion & ginger garlic sabut mirch cardamom & almond giri.
  2. Boil for 15 mins with half cup of water & cool it in room temperature & grind it in grinder.
  3. Now we add ghee in a pan and roast jeera & bay leave now add mashed masala & roast very well until the ghee comes out.
  4. Add salt cinnamon & garam masala powder after 2 min mix curd cream and milk.
  5. Stir continues on low flame add paneer cut of any shape I used round shape
  6. You can garnish it with fresh coriander or melt butter its ready
