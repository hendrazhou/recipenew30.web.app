The ingredients needed to make Evening snacks:
  1. Prepare 2 cup Sweet corn
  2. Get 1/3 cup Butter
  3. Get to taste Salt
  4. Take 1 tsp Roasted jeera powder
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp Black pepper powder
  6. Take 1 tsp Chat masala
  7. Get 2 tsp Lemon juice
  8. Prepare 1 tbsp Coriander leaves chopped
  9. Take 2 tsp Chopped onion
  10. Get 1/3 tsp Red chilli powder
