The ingredients needed to make Reshmi Paneer:
  1. Make ready 2 tbsp oil
  2. Make ready 15 pieces paneer
  3. Take 1/2 carrot sliced
  4. Take 1 onion sliced
  5. Take 1 capsicum sliced
  6. Take 1 tsp ginger paste
  7. Get 1 tsp garlic paste
  8. Take 1 tsp butter
  9. Take 1/2 cup water
  10. Take 1 tsp salt
  11. Prepare 1 tsp red chilli powder
  12. Take 1 tbsp cream
  13. Get 1-2 chopped green chilli
  14. Get 1 tbsp gravy masala
  15. Get 1 tomato sliced
Steps to make Reshmi Paneer:
  1. In a pan add oil, add paneer saute for 1 min. Take it out and keep aside. Now same pan add oil
  2. Add butter melt it. Add onion
  3. Add carrrot, add capsicum, add garlic paste
  4. Add ginger paste mix it well properly. Add tomato, add green chilli
  5. Mix it well. Now take it out. Now same pan add oil, add gravy masala
  6. Mix it well, add cream mix it again.
  7. Now add all cooked veggies mix it again. Now add salt, add red chilli powder
  8. Mix it again. Add water, add paneer
  9. Cover with lid and cooked for 2 min. Now open the lid and reshmi paneer is ready to serve.
