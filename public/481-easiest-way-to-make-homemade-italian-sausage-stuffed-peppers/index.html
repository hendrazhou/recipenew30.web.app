Instructions to make Italian sausage stuffed peppers:
  1. Halve peppers and clean out seeds and membrane, toss in olive oil, bake cut side up 5-7 minutes
  2. Brown sausage. Drain, set aside to cool
  3. Mix softened cream cheese with garlic, salt, pepper and Italian blend
  4. Roast peppers 5-7 minutes to soften
  5. Sprinkle extra cheese blend on top
  6. Put mixture in halved peppers
  7. Bake 5-7 minutes
  8. Makes 30
