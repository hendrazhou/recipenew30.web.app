The ingredients needed to make Spaghetti with Fire Roasted Pepper:
  1. Prepare Sauce———————–
  2. Get 1 whole pablano pepper
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup chopped ripe black olives
  4. Get 1 pint crushed tomatoes
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup water
  6. Prepare 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  7. Prepare 1 tablespoon sugar
  8. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  9. Make ready 1/2 cup Italian sausage
  10. Prepare 1-1/2 teaspoon red wine vinegar
  11. Take 1 pound ground beef 75 /25
  12. Take 1 teaspoon kosher salt more if needed to taste
  13. Make ready 1 teaspoon granulated garlic powder
  14. Prepare 1 large onion chopped
  15. Prepare Pasta———————————
  16. Take 4 quarts water
  17. Take 1 tablespoon kosher salt
  18. Prepare 2 tablespoons extra Virgin olive oil
  19. Prepare 1 pound Linguine pasta
  20. Prepare 1-1/2 tablespoons extra Virgin olive oil coating cooked pasta
  21. Make ready 2 tablespoons chopped parsley cooked pasta
  22. Get Topping——————————-
  23. Prepare To taste grated parmesan cheese
Instructions to make Spaghetti with Fire Roasted Pepper:
  1. Cook the pasta till your desired doneness with the water, 2 tablespoons extra Virgin olive oil, and 1 tablespoon salt. Drain and add olive oil and parsley.
  2. Fire roast the pablano pepper on an open flame. When the skin is blackened and blistered put into a ziploc bag and seal to allow to steam the skin. Set aside for a bit.
  3. After 10-15 minutes remove from the bag and take a spoon and scrape the skin off the pablano pepper. Then remove seeds and chop it up.
  4. Add the meat to a pan with the olives and onions.
  5. Finish Browning the meats, with the onions spices and add chopped pepper.
  6. Cover and simmer for 15 minutes. Add the sugar, tomatoes ingredients. Let simmer for 20 minutes.
  7. Serve with parmesan cheese on top, I hope you enjoy!!!
