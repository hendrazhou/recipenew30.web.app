Steps to make Deep fried pork chops:
  1. Mix egg and milk in med bowl
  2. Season all of the pork chops with the different seasoning
  3. Heat oil to 375 degrees
  4. Coat pork chops 1 at a time with egg milk mixture. Remove then place in flour.
  5. When oil is ready shake of excess flour and place pork chops in the oil.
  6. Cook for about 5 to 7 mins on each side
  7. Should look something like this when done
  8. Enjoy
