The ingredients needed to make Paneer tikka:
  1. Get 1 tsp turmeric powder
  2. Get 2 tsp besan/gram flour
  3. Make ready 2 tsp ginger garlic paste
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup hung curd
  5. Get 2 tsp kashmiri red chilli Powder
  6. Make ready 1 tsp rosted Jeera (cumin)powder
  7. Get 1 1/2 tsp garam masala
  8. Get 1 tsp crushed black pepper
  9. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp mustard oil
  10. Make ready 2 tsp lemon juice
  11. Take 1 tsp food color
  12. Take 1/4 tsp crushed jayfal
  13. Prepare 250gm paneer
  14. Get 2 capsicum
  15. Get 2 onions
  16. Make ready to taste Salt
