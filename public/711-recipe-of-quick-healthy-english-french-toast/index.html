The ingredients needed to make Healthy English French Toast:
  1. Get 5 each eggs
  2. Take 1 1/2 cup almond milk
  3. Get 1 tsp nutmeg
  4. Take 2 tsp cinnamon
  5. Prepare 2 tsp vanilla extract
  6. Get 1 tbsp coconut rum (optional)
  7. Take 5 whole-grain English muffins, halved (or favorite Breakfast Toast)
  8. Prepare 2 bananas, sliced
  9. Take 4 tbsp shredded coconut
  10. Prepare 1/4 cup sliced almonds
  11. Prepare 8 tsp maple syrup
  12. Prepare cooking spray
Steps to make Healthy English French Toast:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. In a bowl, whisk together almond milk, nutmeg, cinnamon, vanilla extract, rum and eggs.
  2. Place muffins on a baking dish lightly coated in cooking spray, then layer banana slices on top. Pour egg mixture over muffins. Press down to soak up all liquid mixture. Top with coconut & almonds. Bake 35 to 40 minutes or until cooked through.
  3. Enjoy with maple syrup.
