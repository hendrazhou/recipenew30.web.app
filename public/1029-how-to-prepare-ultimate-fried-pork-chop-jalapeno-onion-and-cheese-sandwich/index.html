The ingredients needed to make Fried pork chop jalapeno onion and cheese sandwich:
  1. Prepare 2 each boneless pork chops
  2. Get 1 1/4 slice onion
  3. Make ready 1 1/2 slice jalapeno
  4. Take 1 slice cheese
  5. Take 2 slice bread
  6. Prepare 1 vegetable oil
  7. Make ready 1 garlic salt
  8. Prepare 1 ground black pepper
