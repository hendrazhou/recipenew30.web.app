The ingredients needed to make Potato semolina cutlets in evening snacks:
  1. Get 1 cup semolina
  2. Take 5 big size boiled potatoes
  3. Prepare 2 tbs Boiled Green peas
  4. Take 1 tbs Ginger paste
  5. Get as per taste Salt
  6. Get 1 Green chilli finely chopped
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp dry mango powder
  8. Take 1/2 tbs chat masala
  9. Take 1/4 tbs red chilli powder
  10. Prepare 1/4 tbs cumin powder
  11. Prepare 1/2 tbs coriander powder
  12. Prepare as per need Oil for deep frying

Steps to make Potato semolina cutlets in evening snacks:
  1. Take boiled potato in a bowl add boiled peas
  2. Add all the ingredients like salt, finely chopped green chilli, red chilli powder, coriander powder, dry mango powder, chat masala, ginger paste, cumin powder and mix it well
  3. Add semolina and make a make a soft dough
  4. Keep it aside for 10 mins
  5. Now take some oil in hands and give different shapes to the dough
  6. Now deep fry the cuttlets in hot oil
  7. Flip and fry both the sides on medium flame till it turns golden and crisp.
  8. Finally, drain over kitchen paper to remove excess oil and serve semolina cuttlets with tomato sauce.
