The ingredients needed to make Paneeri Reshmi Handi:
  1. Make ready 1 kg chicken
  2. Make ready 3 tbsp oil
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp butter
  4. Take 1 tbsp Ginger garlic paste
  5. Get 1 tsp black pepper
  6. Get 1/2 tsp white pepper
  7. Take To taste salt
  8. Take 1 tsp Lemon juice
  9. Make ready 1 cup Fresh cream
  10. Get 1 cup cheddar cheese cubes
  11. Take 1/2 Garam Masala powder
  12. Get For garnish
  13. Make ready 2 Green chillies slices

Reshmi mean like silky, it refers to. Inspired by the version at Kolachi, this recipe of paneer reshmi handi consists of boneless chickenin a rich & creamy sauce flavored with coconut. Steps to make Paneeri Reshmi Handi:
  1. In a pot (handi)add 3tbsp of oil and 1tbsp of butter and let butter melts then add chicken and fry it for almost 10 mints
  2. Then add 1tbsp of ginger garlic paste and stir for 2to 3min
  3. Then add 1tsp of black pepper,1/2tsp of white pepper, to taste salt and 1tbsp lemon juice and stir for 5 to 6 mints
  4. Now add half cup of water and simmer for 10 mints.. Then add 1cup fresh cream and simmer for 5 mints more then add 1cup of cheddar cheese and 1/2tsp of garam Masala powder further cook for 5 to 10 mints
  5. Now garnish slices green chillies and serve with naan..
