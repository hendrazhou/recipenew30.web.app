The ingredients needed to make Paneer bhurji:

  2. Get 2 onions chopped
  3. Get 1 tomato chopped
  4. Make ready 1 green chilli chopped
  5. Prepare 3 tsp oil
  6. Take 1/4 tsp cumin seeds
  7. Make ready To taste salt
  8. Get 1/4 tsp Turmeric powder

Instructions to make Paneer bhurji:
  1. In a kadhai, add oil and cumin seeds.
  2. Then add onion and green chili. Cook for two minutes.
  3. Now add tomato and cook again. Add all the spices and mix well.
  4. Then add paneer and mix again.
  5. Cook for two minutes.
  6. Bhurji is ready to serve with chapati.
