The ingredients needed to make Pepper Gravy:
  1. Make ready 1 cup chopped yellow bell pepper
  2. Take 1/2 large onion
  3. Make ready 1 cup all purpose flour
  4. Prepare 2 teaspoon salt
  5. Prepare 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  6. Get 1/2 stick butter
  7. Take 1-1/2 quarts water
  8. Get 2-1/2 tablespoons oil
  9. Make ready 1 cube chicken Bouillon
Steps to make Pepper Gravy:
  1. Add the chopped peppers to the oil. Fry 6 minutes
  2. Add the onion
  3. Simmer 7 minutes and add the butter
  4. Add the flour when the butter melts mix well add more oil if necessary.
  5. Add the water and bring to a boil. Stirring constantly till thickened then simmer for 8-10 minutes.
  6. Serve I hope you enjoy!!
