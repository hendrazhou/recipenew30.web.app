The ingredients needed to make Bacon Wrapped Pork Chops:
  1. Prepare 6 1 inch think, boneless pork chops
  2. Prepare 1 packages bacon
  3. Take 1 salt and pepper, to taste
  4. Make ready 1 olive oil, enough to grease pan
  5. Prepare 1 seasoning salt
Instructions to make Bacon Wrapped Pork Chops:
  1. Preheat oven to 400°. Grease baking pan with olive oil. While preheating wash pork chops and cut off excess fat.
  2. Open bacon and wrap pork chops. I used 2 1/2 strips per chop.
  3. Place wrapped pork chops in prepared pan. And put in oven. Bake for approximately 40 mins.
  4. After baking turn oven on broil for 5 minutes to rise and brown bacon. Remove from oven and enjoy!!
