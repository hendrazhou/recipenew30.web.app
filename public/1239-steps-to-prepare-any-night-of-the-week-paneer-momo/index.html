The ingredients needed to make Paneer Momo:
  1. Prepare 1 cup Refined Flour
  2. Make ready 50 gm Paneer pieces
  3. Prepare 1 cup Grated Carrots
  4. Get 1 Cup Cabbage
  5. Make ready 2-3 Garlic cloves
  6. Get 2 inch Ginger
  7. Take 2 Green chillies
  8. Take 1/2 cup Capsicum(finely chopped)
  9. Take to taste Salt
  10. Prepare 1 Teaspoon Black pepper powder
  11. Prepare 1 Teaspoon Oil
  12. Get As needed Water
Steps to make Paneer Momo:
  1. Mix 1 cup Refined Flour and salt as needed.Make a soft dough with water.
  2. Keep the dough for 10 mins.
  3. Take a pan and heat the oil.
  4. Put chopped cottage cheese and fry lightly.
  5. Remove the fried Paneer.And put chopped 3 chopped garlic,grated ginger.
  6. Then add 1 cup of grated carrots, 1 cup Cabbage, 1/2 cup Bell pepper and chopped green chillies.
  7. Then add salt as needed and 1 Tsp Black pepper powder.
  8. Stuffing is ready.
  9. Now make small Dough balls and roll it.
  10. Then put the stuffing into it and make the dumplings as in the picture.
  11. Now put water in lower part of momo maker and boil it.
  12. Put the dumplings/momo into upper part of it and place it.
  13. Cover it for 20 mins.And serve hot with soup and Chutney.
