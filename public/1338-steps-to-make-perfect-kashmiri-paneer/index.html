The ingredients needed to make Kashmiri Paneer:
  1. Prepare 200 gms Cottage cheese (Paneer)
  2. Get Oil for deep frying, 1Table spoon for cooking
  3. Get 3 Cloves
  4. Make ready 1 Bay leaf
  5. Get 3 Green Cardamom
  6. Get 1 Cinnamon stick
  7. Make ready 1 Teaspoon Cumin seeds
  8. Get 1 Tablespoon Dry Ginger powder
  9. Take 1 Teaspoon Fennel powder
  10. Get 1 Glass Milk*
  11. Take 1/4 Glass Water
  12. Make ready As per taste Salt
  13. Get As per taste Pepper
  14. Make ready as required Chopped Cilantro for garnish
  15. Take as needed Mixed herbs for flavour (Optional)

It is cooked with a little ghee and. To prepare Kashmiri Chaman Kaliya Recipe, cut paneer into long rectangular slices. Switch off the flame and finish it off with saffron. Serve Kashmiri Chaman Kaliya Recipe warmly with white rice, rotis.

Instructions to make Kashmiri Paneer:
  1. Cut Cottage cheese (Paneer) into large chunks and deep fried.Keep them separate.
  2. In a wok heat oil then add bay leaf, cloves, green cardamom, cinnamon stick and cumin seeds. Let it splutter.
  3. Add the fennel and dry ginger powder. Saute vigorously. Do not let it burn.
  4. Immediately add the milk and water. Let it come to a boil and add salt and pepper.
  5. Add the deep fried paneer chunks. Let it simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Garnish with coriander leaves and add mixed herbs if needed for an extra flavour.
  7. Note: Let this dish rest for about half an hour before actual serving, so that the paneer can soak up the milk and become more soft & flavourful.

Switch off the flame and finish it off with saffron. Serve Kashmiri Chaman Kaliya Recipe warmly with white rice, rotis.
