The ingredients needed to make Paneer butter mutter masala:
  1. Make ready 200 Grams Paneer ( Indian cottage cheese)
  2. Take 1/2 Cup green peas (mutter)
  3. Make ready 1/2 Cup tomato puree
  4. Make ready 1/3 Cup onion puree
  5. Get 1.5 Tablespoons butter
  6. Take 1/4 Teaspoon cumin seeds
  7. Get 1/4 Tablespoon green chilli paste
  8. Take 1 Teaspoon garlic paste
  9. Take 1/4 Teaspoon turmeric powder
  10. Prepare 1 Teaspoon red chilli powder
  11. Get 1/2 Teaspoon garam masala
  12. Prepare 1/4 Teaspoon masala chat
  13. Prepare To Taste salt
  14. Make ready 2 Tablespoons cilantro coriander leaves chopped /
  15. Prepare 1 Cup water

Instructions to make Paneer butter mutter masala:
  1. Cut paneer* into square pieces of 1 inch 1 inch in size
  2. Take a pan and heat butter on medium heat for 20 seconds
  3. Add cumin seeds and wait till it sizzles
  4. Add onion puree and saute it for a minute
  5. Add tomato puree and saute it for a minute
  6. Add garlic paste, green chilli paste and mix it well
  7. Keep the pan covered for 5-7 minutes on medium heat and stir it occasionally
  8. Take another pan, add peas and 1 cup of water. Put it on medium heat
  9. Add chilli powder, garam masala, chat masala, turmeric powder and salt, mix it well
  10. Keep the pan covered for 4-5 minutes on slow heat and stir it occasionally
  11. Drain the water from pan having peas and add peas to other pan on the stove top
  12. Mix it well, cover the pan and let it cook for 2 minutes
  13. Add paneer and mix it well, carefully stir it to keep the paneer pieces intact
  14. Keep it on low heat for 3 minutes
  15. Tasty dish is ready garnish it with cilantro/ coriander leaves
  16. It is ready to be served with paratha/ naan
  17. Note: * If you are using frozen paneer then take 1 glass of warm water in a pan and put the paneer for about 10-15 minutes so that paneer will become soft and you can easily cut it into slices.
