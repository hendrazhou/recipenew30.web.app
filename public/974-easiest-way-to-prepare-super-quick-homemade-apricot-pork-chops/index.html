The ingredients needed to make Apricot Pork Chops:
  1. Take 4 4oz boneless loin pork chops
  2. Prepare 1/4 cup chopped onion
  3. Take 1/4 cup apricot preserves
  4. Take 1 tbsp low sodium soy sauce
  5. Get 2 tbsp minced garlic
  6. Prepare 1/4 tsp salt
  7. Make ready 1/4 cup sliced green onion (optional)
  8. Make ready can cooking spray

Steps to make Apricot Pork Chops:
  1. Heat a large non sick skillet over medium high heat. Coat pan with cooking spray. Add pork; cook 6 minutes on each side or until done. Remove from pan.
  2. Added copied onion to pan; sauté 4 minutes or until lightly browned. Stir in preserves, soy sauce, garlic, and salt; cook 3 minutes or until thickened. Add pork, turning to coat. Sprinkle with green onions.
