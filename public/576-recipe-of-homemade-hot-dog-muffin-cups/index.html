The ingredients needed to make Hot Dog 🌭 Muffin Cups:
  1. Make ready 2 1/4 cups Bisquick baking mix
  2. Take 2/3 cup milk
  3. Take 2 cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  4. Take 1 pack (1 lb.) hot dogs (your favorite brand!)
  5. Take Butter cooking spray
  6. Get American cheese slices (cut into small squares)
  7. Make ready Relish
  8. Get Ketchup
  9. Get Mustard
Instructions to make Hot Dog 🌭 Muffin Cups:
  1. In a large bowl mix together bisquick baking mix, milk and Monterey Jack cheese, set bowl to the side……you can add a little bit more milk if mixture is too difficult to stir, add just enough milk so mixture is moist but not runny…….
  2. Take your hot dogs and cut into chunks, set to the side……I used extra hot dogs today but you’ll be fine with just a 1 pound pack !
  3. Spray a muffin pan with butter cooking spray……
  4. Pour some of your Bisquick mix into each muffin cup, being careful not to overfill each cup…….
  5. Now add some hot dogs into Bisquick mix, add as much as you can without overfilling muffin cups……
  6. Bake in a preheated 450 degree oven for 10 minutes or until muffin tops are golden on top…..
  7. Place some cut up American cheese slices on top of muffins, cook for several more minutes or until all cheese has melted……
  8. Let stand for a few minutes in your muffin pan, then remove to a serving platter……continue until Bisquick mix and hot dogs are all gone……
  9. Serve each hot dog muffin with relish, mustard or ketchup and enjoy 😉!!
