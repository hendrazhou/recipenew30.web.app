Steps to make Brad's lemon pepper chicken:
  1. Fillet chicken breast in half. Place halves under saran wrap and pound meat until thin.
  2. Marinade in lemon pepper marinade for 1 hour
  3. Remove to a cutting board. Sprinkle seasoning over both halves of the chicken
  4. Place pieces of cheese on one half of the chicken breast. Lay the other half on top. Wrap with bacon.
  5. Place in an oven preheated to 400 degrees. Bake 10 minutes.
  6. Brush with more marinade. Bake 10 more minutes. Repeat process with 30 minutes total bake time.
  7. Remove to a plate. Drizzle a little more marinade on top if desired. Garnish with fresh basil and serve
