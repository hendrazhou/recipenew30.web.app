Instructions to make Paneer Tikka:
  1. Take paneer and cut into square pieces.
  2. Take a bowl and put yogurt in it and add all spices and salt and mix it well.
  3. Than heat 1 tablespoon oil and add Yoghurt and batter and mix it well.
  4. Now add paneer pieces in it and mix carefully.
  5. Marinate the paneer mixture for 1 hour.
  6. After one hour heat a pan and put marinated paneer in it.
  7. Cook it in medium flame until paneer get dry.
  8. Now mix well and cook for 2 minutes
  9. Paneer tikka is ready. Serve hot and enjoy with naan or chapattis or fulkies.
