The ingredients needed to make Stuffed salmon:
  1. Prepare 2 half pound salmon filets
  2. Take 1 tbsp olive oil
  3. Prepare 1/3 cup sun-dried tomatoes
  4. Prepare 3 cups baby spinach
  5. Take 1 cup basil leaves
  6. Take 1/2 cup feta cheese
  7. Make ready to taste Salt & pepper

Instructions to make Stuffed salmon:
  1. Preheat oven to 375. Coat an oven safe dish with oil
  2. Make the filling - Use a paper towel to dab some oil of the tomatoes - Chop the tomatoes - Add oil and tomatoes to a pan over med. heat. - Cook 1 min, stir occasionally
  3. Add basil and spinach to the skillet - About 3 min. Stir occasionally - Remove from heating unit and add the feta cheese stir well
  4. Slice salmon through center - Stuff salmon - Sprinkle with salt & pepper - Bake center rack for 20-30 min.
