The ingredients needed to make Oreo cake baked in bati cooker:
  1. Prepare 5 Oreo biscuits packet
  2. Make ready 2 tbsp maida
  3. Make ready Half pouch eno
  4. Get 1 tbsp sugar powder
  5. Prepare 2 dairy milk chocolate
  6. Prepare 2 pouches of Jam
  7. Make ready 1/2 cup warm milk

Steps to make Oreo cake baked in bati cooker:
  1. Take big jar of mixture add oreo and mix this all biscuits
  2. Than add maida,sugar,eno and heated milk than mix all this in mixture with Oreo biscuits powder
  3. Put all this mixture in a greased with ghee cake pot
  4. Than place it in preheated bati cooker and keep flame on medium.
  5. After 10 minutes check with toothpic if your toothpick is come out clean than it means your cake is ready
  6. After cooling cake decorate with melted chocolate and Jam.
