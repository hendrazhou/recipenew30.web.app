The ingredients needed to make Chicken and broccoli dinner with stuffing:
  1. Take 1 box seasoned stuffing
  2. Take 4 Chicken breast
  3. Take 1 lb Broccoli
  4. Make ready 1 packages Sherred cheddar cheese
  5. Prepare 1 can Condensed mushroom soup
  6. Make ready 1 pinch Salt and pepper
  7. Prepare 1 pinch Dehydrated parsley and garlic
  8. Get 1 pinch Black pepper
  9. Prepare 2 Onions
  10. Prepare 1 Red pepper
  11. Get 1 tbsp Oil
  12. Take 1 Non stick cooking spray

Steps to make Chicken and broccoli dinner with stuffing:
  1. Boil in different pots, the brocoli and the chicken
  2. Put oil to warm in low while peel the onions and clean the pepper. Cut everything into thin pieces and throw to cook into oil
  3. Once onions and pepers are cooked, add the named spices and water into it, then add stuffing and mix well
  4. Once chicken is cooked drain and cut into cubes, do the same with the broccoli, cutting into bite size pieces.
  5. Add brocoli and chicken to the stuffing and mix well
  6. Get the condensed soup into a bowl and add a cup of hot water, 2 if necessary since some cups are smaller than others, mix well and pour into food mix. Mix well
  7. Add 1/2 of the cheddar cheese to the food and mix well again. Put into an oven pan and spread the remaining chesse on top.
  8. Set oven into 300 and finish cooking until the chesse is melted
  9. The cooking spray is for your oven pan, but if you do have a non stick pan then is not needed.
