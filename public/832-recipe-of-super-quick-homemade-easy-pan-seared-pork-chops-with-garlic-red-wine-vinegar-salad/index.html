The ingredients needed to make Easy Pan Seared Pork Chops with Garlic Red Wine Vinegar Salad:
  1. Prepare 4 pcs pork chops (thin in size) garlic powder
  2. Get salt
  3. Get fresh ground black pepper
  4. Make ready 1 Tbsp olive oil
  5. Prepare For the salad:
  6. Get 25 gr purple cabbage, thinly sliced
  7. Prepare 300 gr iceberg lettuce leaves, torn into bite size pieces
  8. Get 1 carrot, shredded or julienned
  9. Prepare 150 gr cherry tomatoes, halved
  10. Get 1 English cucumber, thinly sliced
  11. Get Garlic red wine vinegar dressing (mix well in a small bowl):
  12. Make ready 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  13. Get 30 ml red wine vinegar
  14. Get 60 ml olive oil
  15. Take to taste salt and black pepper powder
Steps to make Easy Pan Seared Pork Chops with Garlic Red Wine Vinegar Salad:
  1. In a big bowl, mix all salad ingredients. Add the dressing. Mix well. Set aside.
  2. Pat dry pork chops with paper towel. Sprinkle them generously with garlic powder, salt, and black pepper on both side. Set aside.
  3. Heat olive oil over medium high heat. When itâ€™s shimmering hot, add in the pork chops.
  4. Sear on one side for 2-3 minutes without moving them until it turn golden brown. Flip and sear the other side as well. Remove from the heat.
