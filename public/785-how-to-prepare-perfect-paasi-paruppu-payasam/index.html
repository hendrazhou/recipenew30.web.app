The ingredients needed to make Paasi Paruppu Payasam:
  1. Take 1/2 cup Moong DaI (I used spilt one)…
  2. Get 2 tbsps rice Uncooked . …
  3. Take 3/4 cup Jaggery Little less then [I used syrup] - Adjust … to taste .
  4. Get Handful cashewnuts .
  5. Take 1 tsp Cardamom powder . …
  6. Prepare 1 - 2 tbsps coconut Grated . …
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup Milk . …
  8. Prepare 1/2 tbsp ghee … .
Steps to make Paasi Paruppu Payasam:
  1. Wash and soak the dal for few min.
  2. Put it in the cooker with double the water and cook for 2 whistles on full and 5-7 min on low flame.
  3. Mash it well.
  4. Add the milk and boil it.
  5. Add the jaggery and mix it well.
  6. Taste and adjust the sweetness.
  7. Now stir in the coconut and cardamom powder.
  8. Let it boil for 2-4 min on medium heat.
  9. In another pan, heat ghee. Add cashews and fry till they are nice and golden.
  10. Add the ghee and cashews to the kheer.
  11. Serve with love.
