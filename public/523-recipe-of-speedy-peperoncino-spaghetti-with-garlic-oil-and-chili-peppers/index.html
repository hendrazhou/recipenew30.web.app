The ingredients needed to make Peperoncino (Spaghetti with Garlic, Oil, and Chili Peppers):
  1. Take 100 g spaghetti
  2. Take 2 teaspoon salt
  3. Take 1 garlic
  4. Get 2 teaspoon olive oil
  5. Make ready Chili peppers as much as you want
  6. Take Water
Steps to make Peperoncino (Spaghetti with Garlic, Oil, and Chili Peppers):
  1. Put spaghetti and water to a microwaveable container, and microwave it for 7 minutes.
  2. Fry sliced garlic by low temperature fire and some minutes later add chili peppers.
  3. After spaghetti is boiled, put the water from the container to the pan little bit.
  4. Turn the fire high and put salt and spaghetti and stair them well.
