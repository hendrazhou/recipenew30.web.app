The ingredients needed to make Whole Weat Crepe with Nutella & Banana Filling:
  1. Prepare 1/2 cup whole wheat flour
  2. Get 1/2 cup almond milk
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp oil (I substitute for plain yogurt)
  4. Take 1 tsp vanilla extract
  5. Get 1 pinch salt
  6. Make ready 2 large egg whites
  7. Get 1 pinch cinnamon
  8. Make ready 1 pinch nutmeg
  9. Get 1 oil spray or unsalted butter
  10. Get 1/4 banana
  11. Get 1 tsp raw sugar
  12. Take 1 tsp nutella

Instructions to make Whole Weat Crepe with Nutella & Banana Filling:
  1. Sift the flour, add the dry ingredients and mix.
  2. In a non stick pan, spray with oil or add a little bit of unsalted butter.
  3. With a 1/3 measuring cup, fill it with the mixture and add it to the hot pan, in a circular motion spread the mixture. Wait 2 to 3 minutes or until you see its done.
  4. Flip the crepe, and wait few minutes.
  5. Spread nutella and add pieces of banana and fold it. Add a scoop of miracle wipped and enjoy.
