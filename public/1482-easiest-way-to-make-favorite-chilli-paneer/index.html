The ingredients needed to make Chilli Paneer:
  1. Get 5-7 Paneer Cubes
  2. Make ready 1 and 1/2 tsp Maida
  3. Take 1 and 1/2 tsp corn flour (optional)
  4. Get 5 to 7 Capsicum cubes
  5. Prepare 10 to 12 Onion cubes
  6. Make ready 6 to 7 Garlic cloves, finely chopped
  7. Get 1 Small Onion, finely chopped
  8. Take 1 to 2 tbsp Soy sauce
  9. Take 1 to 2 tbsp Tomato Ketchup
  10. Prepare as per taste Salt
  11. Prepare As required Black pepper
  12. Take 1 and 1/2 tsp Red chilli powder
  13. Prepare As required Chilli flakes
  14. Get As required Oregano
  15. Prepare As required Water

Steps to make Chilli Paneer:
  1. In a small bowl, add maida, corn flour, red chilli powder, salt, black pepper powder, paneer cubes, 2-4 tbsp of water and mix. Heat some oil in a pan on medium flame. Shallow fry these marinated paneer on medium low flame. Flip them when they turn golden in color. Once done, transfer these to plate.
  2. Heat some oil in pan on medium low flame. Add onion cubes, chopped onions, garlic, capsicum, chilli flakes, salt, red chilli powder, black pepper and mix.
  3. Stir and then add tomato ketchup, soy sauce, oregano, 1 tsp corn flour + 2 tsp water and mix. Let it cook for few seconds on low flame and stir.
  4. Add cooked paneer cubes to this sauce. Add half tsp of sugar and mix well. Turn off the flame.
  5. Serve hot chilli paneer with fried rice. Enjoy! 🥰
