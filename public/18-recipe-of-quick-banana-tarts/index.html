The ingredients needed to make Banana Tarts:
  1. Prepare 500 lbs flour
  2. Prepare 220 gm Butter
  3. Take 1 tsp milk powder
  4. Make ready Some ripe bananas
  5. Make ready to taste Sugar
  6. Take 1 tsp cardamom powder
Instructions to make Banana Tarts:
  1. In a cooking pan add cut ripe bananas add sugar and keep stirring and let it become a thik consistency
  2. Add cardamon powder stir keep it to cool
  3. Take flour add milk powder and butter mix it well until it become a granulated texture.. sprinkle a little water to make a dough… place in small moulds or a pan frick with fork..
  4. Add cooked bananas and make sone batonete with a little dough place on it and with a help of a brush add some liquid milk on it
  5. Put to bake at 180 degrees for 20 mins in a preheated oven at 10 minutes..
