The ingredients needed to make Paneer Lapeta:
  1. Get 1 cup dice paneer
  2. Take 1/2 cup cashew
  3. Make ready 1 long chopped onion
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup tomato puree
  5. Take 1/2 cumin seed
  6. Make ready 3 dry red chilli
  7. Get 1/2 kasuri methi
  8. Take 1/2 spn salt, red chilli powder, turmeric, coriander powder
  9. Prepare 1/2 spn garam masala
  10. Make ready 3 tbsp oil
  11. Get 1/2 milk
  12. Take 7-8 garlic cloves
  13. Make ready 1 inch ginger

Instructions to make Paneer Lapeta:
  1. Heat kadhai heat oil in it fry onion in it in same oil fry cashew and ginger garlic. Take a grinder pot fried onion cashew ginger garlic cumin seed and dry red chilli and grind it
  2. In same oil add masala then after 1 min add cashew onion paste saute it for 5 mins now add tomato puree
  3. Saute it till oil leaves it's sides now add kasuri methi mix it add paneer mix it add milk and give a nicely mix. It's gravy should b thick so that it cover all the paneer add some grated paneer
  4. Serve with hot hot phulka
