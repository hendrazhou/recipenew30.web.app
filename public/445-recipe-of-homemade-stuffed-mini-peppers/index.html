The ingredients needed to make Stuffed Mini Peppers:
  1. Get 1 bag mini peppers, hollowed
  2. Get 1 Owen's sausage, browned and chopped finely
  3. Prepare 1 fresh sage, as in a bunch, finely chopped
  4. Get 1/2 red cabbage chopped finely
  5. Get 1 yellow onion, chopped finely. Browned
  6. Take 1 bunch spinach, finely chopped
  7. Get 3 blocks cream cheese
  8. Prepare 1 tbsp salt and pepper
  9. Take 1 block parmesan, grated
  10. Make ready 3 jalepenos, chopped finely

Steps to make Stuffed Mini Peppers:
  1. Hollow out mini peppers
  2. Combine all other ingredients. Mix well.
  3. Stuff peppers with mixture
  4. Spread on baking sheet evenly and bake for 45 minutes at 375 degrees
  5. If feeling fanciful, add parmesan to tops of peppers prior to baking
