Instructions to make Paneer Paratha:
  1. First, make a soft dough with wheat flour and keep it aside.
  2. Mix sugar into the Paneer.
  3. Take lemon size dough and stuffing the sugar mixed paneer into it.
  4. Now roll the paratha into circle shape.
  5. Pre-heat tha pan (Tawa)and bake the paratha Completely, now applying oil /ghee.
  6. Tasty Paneer Paratha is ready.
