The ingredients needed to make Veggie Quiche:
  1. Prepare 1 cup mixed veggies
  2. Prepare 1 small onion, chopped
  3. Prepare 1/4 teaspoon parsley flakes
  4. Get 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  5. Prepare 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  6. Get 1/8 teaspoon salt
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup Bisquick mix
  8. Take 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  9. Get 1 cup milk
  10. Get 2 eggs
  11. Prepare Cooking Spray
Steps to make Veggie Quiche:
  1. In a bowl combine : mixed veggies, onion, parsley flakes, garlic powder, black pepper and salt, mix well…..
  2. In a separate bowl combine : Bisquick, shredded cheddar cheese, milk and eggs, mix all well……
  3. Combine veggie mix with Bisquick mix, stir well and pour into a 9 inch pie plate that has been sprayed with cooking spray…..
  4. Bake in a 400 degree oven for 35-40 minutes, or until golden on top…..Let rest for 10 minutes before cutting…….
  5. Serve and enjoy 😉!!!!
