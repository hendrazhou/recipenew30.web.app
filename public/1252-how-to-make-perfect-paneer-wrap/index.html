The ingredients needed to make Paneer wrap:
  1. Take 100 gms paneer
  2. Make ready 1 chopped capsicum
  3. Take 1 cup boiled corn
  4. Make ready 1/2 chopped cabbage
  5. Make ready 3 green chillies
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp butter
  7. Take 8-10 Chapati
  8. Get For puree:
  9. Get 3 tomatoes
  10. Make ready 1 pc ginger
  11. Prepare For Gravy:
  12. Make ready 1/4 tbsp pizza pasta sauce
  13. Take 1 tsp red chilli powder
  14. Get as per taste salt
  15. Prepare 1/2 tsp garam massla
  16. Make ready as required butter for cooking

Instructions to make Paneer wrap:
  1. Heat butter in a pan, add chillies and all the veggies. Saute it for 2 mins.
  2. Add puree and all the spices and sauce to it.
  3. Add paneer and cook well.
  4. Let it cool.
  5. Take one chapati. Spread paneer gravy. Roll and toast it on tawa with butter.
