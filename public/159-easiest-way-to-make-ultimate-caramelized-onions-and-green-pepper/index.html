The ingredients needed to make Caramelized Onions 🧅 and Green Pepper:
  1. Take 2 onions, sliced
  2. Prepare 1 green pepper, sliced
  3. Make ready 2 tablespoons margarine OR butter
  4. Get 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  5. Take 6 cloves garlic, minced
  6. Make ready 1 tablespoon teriyaki sauce
  7. Get 1/2 tablespoon soy sauce
Instructions to make Caramelized Onions 🧅 and Green Pepper:
  1. Place margarine OR butter in skillet on low heat…..
  2. Place your sliced onions, green pepper, black pepper, minced garlic, teriyaki and soy sauce into skillet……
  3. Cook veggies, stirring occasionally, on medium to high heat in skillet…….At this point you can lower the heat just a bit and cook until browned……
  4. Serve over cooked burgers 🍔, sausages or anything else you need a good topping for and enjoy 😉!! I served mine on top of a grilled hot Italian sausage! Yummy 😋!!
