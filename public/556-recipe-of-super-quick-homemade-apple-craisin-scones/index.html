Steps to make Apple craisin scones:
  1. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl
  2. Using a large hole grater, grate cold butter into dry ingredients mixture
  3. Add cold milk, mix until dough comes together, add in chopped apple and craisins
  4. Turn out onto a piece of wax paper a form into a circle, about 1 inch thick, cut into wedges
