The ingredients needed to make Lemon tarts:
  1. Prepare For tart crust
  2. Prepare 1 cup flour
  3. Make ready 1 tbsp cornflour
  4. Prepare 2-3 tb spoon butter
  5. Take 1 pinch salt
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp sugar
  7. Prepare 1 tsp Lemon zest
  8. Prepare 2 tbsp Lemon juice
  9. Make ready For tart icing
  10. Prepare 1/2 cup sugar
  11. Make ready 1cup milk
  12. Take 1tbsp custard powde
  13. Prepare 1/2 cup Whipped cream
  14. Take as needed Mango or strawberry
  15. Make ready 1tbsp butter
  16. Make ready 1 tbsp coconut powder
  17. Make ready as needed Lemon zest

Instructions to make Lemon tarts:
  1. 1 bowl m flour cornflour sugar salt baking powder dal mix karein.
  2. Phir lemon juice zest & butter dal mix karein
  3. Cold water dal 1 smooth dough ready karein
  4. 1/2 hour ke liye fridge m rakhein
  5. Ab isko bail kar tart mould m rakhein & folk se prick kar de 180 par 20 to 25 mnts bake karein
  6. For fillling
  7. 1pan m milk custard powder sugar mix kar boil karein
  8. Ab butter & coconut powder mix kar thanda kar le
  9. Ab isme essence lemon zest & whipped cream mix karein
  10. Tart m fill kar uppar se mango or straw berry se decorate karein.
