The ingredients needed to make Stuffed Pepper Soup:
  1. Make ready 1 lb ground beef
  2. Take 1 bell pepper, diced
  3. Take 1 onion, diced
  4. Take 1 (15 oz) can diced tomatoes
  5. Take 1 (15 oz) can tomato sauce
  6. Prepare 3-4 C chicken broth
  7. Prepare 1 t garlic powder
  8. Get 1 t salt
  9. Take to taste Pepper,
  10. Make ready 1 (16 oz) bag cauliflower pearls

Instructions to make Stuffed Pepper Soup:
  1. Cook ground beef, onion and pepper over medium-high heat in large pot until beef is cooked through.
  2. Add tomatoes, tomato sauce, chicken broth, garlic powder, salt and pepper.
  3. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, cover and simmer for 10 min.
  4. Stir in cauliflower pearls and continue simmering for another 10 min or so.
  5. Serve with a sprinkling of mozzarella cheese on top, if desired.
