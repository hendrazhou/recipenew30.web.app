Steps to make English Breakfast:
  1. Making of the french toast: break eggs in a bowl, add liquid milk, dash of salt and dash of black pepper, whisk together
  2. Heat up pan, spray cooking spray on the pan; soak bread in the whisked mix and place in hot pan; flip over once the other side is brown and set aside
  3. Preheat Oven 10mins at 200c; Spray oven tray with a cooking spray and place Sausages and Bacon, grill for 8mins and turn off oven.
  4. Sunny Side Up: Heat up pan and spray cooking spray on the pan, break egg into the pan after 50sec flip over and do same and set aside.
  5. Serve French Toast with grilled bacon, sausages, sunny side runny and baked beans
