The ingredients needed to make Paneer masaledar:
  1. Take 500 gms amul paneer cubes
  2. Prepare 1 tbspn ginger garlic paste
  3. Get 1 small green bell pepper
  4. Take 2-3 medium onion
  5. Make ready as per taste Salt
  6. Get as required Oil
  7. Get 1 tsp red chilli powder
