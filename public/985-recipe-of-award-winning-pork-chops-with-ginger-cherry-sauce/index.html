The ingredients needed to make Pork Chops With Ginger Cherry Sauce:
  1. Take chops
  2. Prepare 4 1/4 inch thick center cut pork chops
  3. Take 1/4 tsp salt
  4. Take 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  5. Make ready 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  6. Make ready 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  7. Make ready sauce
  8. Prepare 3 1/4" thick slices of fresh ginger
  9. Take 3 scallions cut into thirds
  10. Make ready 3/4 cup frozen black cherries ( thawed )
  11. Get 3 tbsp balsalmic vinegar
  12. Prepare 2 tbsp salted butter
  13. Get 1/2 cup vegetable broth

Steps to make Pork Chops With Ginger Cherry Sauce:
  1. In a large plate mix salt with flour. Dredge chops in flour on both sides and set aside.
  2. Prepare your ginger and scallions. In a deep saute pan over medium heat, add butter and oil. Let butter melt then add the ginger and scallions. Keep them to the sides of the pan.
  3. Place chops in and cook on one side about three minutes. Flip, cook another 2-3 minutes. When done, set them aside and cook the other two.
  4. In the same pan add the balsamic vinegar. Cook one minute. Add in cherries and half of remaking butter.
  5. Cook one minute, add in vegetable broth. Cook five minutes.
  6. With the back of a fork, smash down cherries. Add last amount of butter. Let butter melt and sauce is done.
  7. Remove ginger and scallions before spooning sauce over pork chops. Enjoy!
