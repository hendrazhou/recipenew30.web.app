The ingredients needed to make Kadai paneer:
  1. Take 200 gms paneer(long strips)
  2. Make ready 2 tsp oil
  3. Make ready 1 bowl capsicum in long strips
  4. Get 1 bowl onion finely chopped and 1 piece onion in cubes
  5. Prepare 1 1/2 bowl tomatoes roughly chopped
  6. Make ready 50 gms khoya
  7. Take 1 inch grated ginger
  8. Make ready for masala:
  9. Get 2 tsp dhaniya seeds
  10. Take 1 tsp saunf
  11. Make ready 1 tsp Jeera/cumin seeds
  12. Make ready As Required Red chilli powder
  13. Prepare 1 tsp. haldi powder
  14. Prepare 1/2 tsp. Kasuri methi
  15. Take 2 tsp Red Chilli Powder
  16. Prepare 2 bay leaves
  17. Prepare 1 tsp garam masala

Instructions to make Kadai paneer:
  1. Heat a pan and dry roast dhaniya, saunf, and jeera seeds for a sec till aroma starts coming from.it on a low flame otherwise, it will burn. Then grind this masala.
  2. Take a pan and add 2 tsp oil. Then add bay leaf red chili and chopped onion on a low flame till it becomes soft.
  3. When the onion turns reddish brown add tomatoes to it. Now add salt so that tomatoes become mushy. Once it's soft add the roasted masala, red chili powder and haldi powder, and ginger.
  4. Fry the masala and add khoya and then saute till the time pan start leaving oil. Now it's time for capsicum and onion cubes. Leave it for a few minutes.
  5. At last add paneer, kasuri methi and garam.masala. And it's done.
  6. Enjoy it with garama garam paratha or chapati.
