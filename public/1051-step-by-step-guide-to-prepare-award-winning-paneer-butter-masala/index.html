The ingredients needed to make Paneer butter masala:
  1. Take 200 gm paneer cubes
  2. Get 1 onion diced
  3. Make ready 1 tomato sliced
  4. Make ready 1 stick butter (unsalted)
  5. Make ready Handful cashews
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp Ginger-garlic paste
  7. Take leaves Kasauri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  8. Make ready Full cream (optional)
  9. Make ready 1 bay leaf
  10. Make ready 2 green chilis
  11. Prepare Cumin seeds
  12. Get 1 tbsp Red chilli powder
  13. Prepare 1 tbsp Coriander powder
  14. Get 1 cinnamon stick, 2-3cardamoms, 2 cloves (garam masala whole)

Instructions to make Paneer butter masala:
  1. Fry the paneer separately and blend the cashews to make a fine paste, meanwhile make a curry of onion, tomatoes, ginger-garlic paste. Blend the mixture and sieve it.
  2. To make the gravy : pour some oil in a pan and add the cumin seeds, green chillies, whole garam-masala and also pour in the sieved mixture of onion/tomatoes, cashews paste coriander/cumin powder and some water
  3. When the mixture comes to boil add in the paneer cubes and top it with some butter and fresh cream(optional) and sprinkle over with kasauri methi (fenugreek) leaves.
