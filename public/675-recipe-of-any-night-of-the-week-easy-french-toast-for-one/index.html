The ingredients needed to make Easy French Toast for One:
  1. Get 2 eggs
  2. Get 1 tsp vanilla extract
  3. Get 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  4. Make ready 1/3 cup milk
  5. Prepare 4 stale or day old bread - any kind
  6. Prepare dash salt
  7. Make ready 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  8. Prepare 2 tbsp white granulated sugar
  9. Prepare Butter for browning or cooking spray

Steps to make Easy French Toast for One:
  1. In a flat bottom dish (I use a pie plate - holds 2 slices bread), beat the eggs with the milk. Add remaining ingredients until well blended.
  2. Dip the bread in the egg/milk mix. Make sure both sides are well coated. Don't soak too long, though.
  3. Lightly grease a griddle or fry pan with either spray or butter. About medium high is good. Brown the bread for about 2 mins then turn and brown the other side. Don't cook too fast, as the outside will brown and you will have uncooked "custard" on the inside. Your toast will also fall apart.
  4. When toast is done, you can add fruit toppings, sprinkle with powdered sugar or serve with butter and maple syrup. I like the taste of vanilla, you can always increase or decrease as well as the cinnamon and nutmeg. These freeze well.
