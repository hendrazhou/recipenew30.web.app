The ingredients needed to make Paprika and Salt n Pepper Chicken Deep Fried 😀:
  1. Prepare 1 chicken breast cut into strips
  2. Prepare 3 tbls Flour
  3. Prepare 1/2 tsp Paprika
  4. Take 1/2 tsp salt
  5. Prepare 1 Egg White
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp Black pepper
  7. Take Enough Veg oil for Deep frying
Instructions to make Paprika and Salt n Pepper Chicken Deep Fried 😀:
  1. Cut the chicken into strips
  2. Mix all the rest into a bowk and mix well.
  3. Egg white in another bowl
  4. Using tongs or a fork lift the piece of chicken dip in the egg white.
  5. Then the flour mix and dip into the hot oil.( try first with a little flour if it bubbles quick its ready hot enough
  6. Then hold in the hot oil for a secound so as it does not go to the bottom and stick.Do these in batches 4 or 5 at a time.serve these with a nice Salad. Or in a wrap with salad.
  7. Drain off on kitchen roll for a few minutes
  8. Add to a serving dish with a salad
