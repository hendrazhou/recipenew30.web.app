The ingredients needed to make Simple Masa and pepper soup:
  1. Get Masa
  2. Take 4 cups rice (shinkafar tuwo)
  3. Make ready 3 teaspoon yeast
  4. Make ready 4 tablespoon sugar
  5. Prepare Pinch salt
  6. Make ready Pepper soup (beef and bpomo)
  7. Make ready 1/2 kilo beef
  8. Prepare Pomo as desired
  9. Make ready Seasoning
  10. Take Curry
  11. Take Salt
  12. Make ready Grinded medium onion
  13. Take 5 scotch bonnet and 3 pepper
Steps to make Simple Masa and pepper soup:
  1. Soak rice overnight or for hours and blend
  2. Add yeast, sugar and salt then mix. Cover and keep in warm place.
  3. It’ll double it size(i had to share mine into other bowl). Add masa pan on fire and add little oil to each
  4. Gently pour mixture and allow to cook for about 2-3 mins. Gently turn to other side
  5. Continue until you’re done. You can serve the masa with kulikuli, honey, sugar, or vegetable soup
  6. For the pepper soup. You’ll cook you pomo in pressure pot to soften it or cook until soft with little salt, onion and white pepper. And also the beef
  7. Add them together and add the grinded onion, scotch bonnet and pepper add moderate amount of water and allow to boil
  8. Add the seasoning and everything together.
  9. Leave for about 15-20mins to cook and serve
