The ingredients needed to make Smoked Italian meatballs:
  1. Get meatballs
  2. Get 1 lb Italian sausage, mild
  3. Take 1/2 cup milk
  4. Get 1 cup panko breadcrumbs
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  6. Get 1 tsp whole leaf oregano
  7. Make ready 1 large egg
  8. Prepare 1 tsp chives oil
  9. Prepare bottom liner
  10. Take 1/2 large onion, chopped
  11. Take 1 as needed nonstick spray
Instructions to make Smoked Italian meatballs:
  1. Mix all the meatball ingredients throughly
  2. Take a steamer spray bottom and sides line bottom with chopped onion
  3. Form meatball mixture into balls I got 21 out of this
  4. Set meatballs aside after all are made line them in steamer take to grill and grill turning occasionally
  5. When done you can freeze them or use them for sandwiches spaghetti pizza soup stews whatever you use meatballs in hope you enjoy
