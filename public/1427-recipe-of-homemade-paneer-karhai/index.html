The ingredients needed to make Paneer karhai:
  1. Take 250 grm paneer
  2. Make ready 1 capsicum
  3. Make ready 3 onion chopped
  4. Prepare 3 tomato
  5. Make ready 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  6. Take 1 tbs dry fenugreek leaves
  7. Make ready to taste Salt
  8. Prepare 1/4 cup oil
  9. Get 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  10. Get 1 tsp cumin powder
  11. Take 1/4 tsp tarmaric powder
  12. Take 1 tsp coriander powder
  13. Get 2 green chili
  14. Take leaves Some coriander
  15. Prepare 1/2 cup curd beated
