The ingredients needed to make Black Beans and Rice:
  1. Make ready 1 pound dried black beans, soaked in water overnight, drain
  2. Take 2 cups brown rice
  3. Make ready 3 &1/2 cups water plus salt to taste to cook rice
  4. Prepare 1 large onion, chopped well
  5. Take 4 cloves garlic, rough chop
  6. Make ready 2 tablespoons olive oil
  7. Get 1 teaspoon chile powder; mild, medium or hot, to your taste
  8. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  9. Make ready 2 tablespoons dried oregano
  10. Take salt and pepper
  11. Take 3/4 cup chopped tomatos
  12. Take Optional ×* Serve with fresh chopped cilantro

Steps to make Black Beans and Rice:
  1. Cook beans in water for 45 minutes or until soft. (You will save 1 cup of cooked bean water). Cook rice at the same time, rice will take closer to an hour to cook over low heat. While beans and rice are cooking, prep veggies and measure out spices in a small bowl.
  2. Heat olive oil in a skillet and add onion, cook for 3 minutes then add garlic, gently cook 2 more minutes. Add spices. Add cooked beans and reserved bean water and tomatoes. Mix. Simmer for 2o minutes.
  3. Serve on top of hot cooked rice.
