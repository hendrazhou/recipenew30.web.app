The ingredients needed to make Salt and Chilli Chicken:
  1. Get 4 chicken breasts (cut into strips)
  2. Get 2 tsp baking powder
  3. Make ready 100 g plain flour
  4. Take 100 g cornflour
  5. Get 1 tbsp garlic salt
  6. Prepare 2 tsp salt
  7. Take 1-2 tsp chilli powder
  8. Take 2-4 small chillis
  9. Take Bunch chopped scallions
  10. Get 2 egg whites
  11. Make ready 2 tbsp soy sauce
  12. Take 2 tbsp sesame oil
  13. Get 2-3 garlic cloves chopped
  14. Prepare 1/4 pepper p/p
  15. Get 1/4 onion p/p
  16. Get Bunch fresh coriander
  17. Prepare Bunch fresh parsley
Instructions to make Salt and Chilli Chicken:
  1. Mix egg whites with soy sauce,garlic salt, chilli powder and salt. Add chicken strips and marinade in fridge for 30 mins.
  2. Mix flour, cornflour and baking powder then coat 5-6 strips at a time and lay on tray.
  3. Once all strips are coated, deep fry them in batches in hot oil at 180 degrees until golden brown (just few minutes). Then place on a tray.
  4. Heat pan and add sesame oil. Quickly sauté julienne of onion and pepper along with garlic, chilli and chopped scallions. Add few drops of soy sauce and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Pan fry chicken strips for few seconds and add fresh coriander and fresh parsley.
  6. Serve on its own or with egg fried rice or chips or noodles.
