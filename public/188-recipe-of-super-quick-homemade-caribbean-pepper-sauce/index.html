The ingredients needed to make Caribbean Pepper Sauce:
  1. Prepare 20 scotch bonnet peppers
  2. Prepare 8 habanero peppers
  3. Get 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  4. Take 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  5. Take 1 large white onion
  6. Get 2 small carrots
  7. Take 2 tsp cumin
  8. Prepare 2 tsp salt
  9. Make ready 2 tsp black ground pepper
  10. Get 8 cloves garlic
  11. Take 3 limes juiced and zested
  12. Get 1 mango
  13. Make ready 2 cups pineapple w/ juice
  14. Take 1/4 cup brown sugar
  15. Make ready Optional
  16. Get 10 thai chili peppers
  17. Make ready 3 tbsp olive oil
