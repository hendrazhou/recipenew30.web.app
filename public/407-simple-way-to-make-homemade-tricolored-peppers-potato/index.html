The ingredients needed to make Tricolored peppers potato:
  1. Make ready 3 peppers RED+GREEN+ORANGE
  2. Prepare 1 big diced onion
  3. Prepare Ginger garlic estimated as well optional
  4. Take 1 teaspoon salt
  5. Make ready Half teaspoon red chilli powder
  6. Make ready Few red chili flakes
  7. Prepare 1 green chilli long sliced
  8. Take 1 teaspoon turmeric

  10. Make ready 1 teaspoon chaat masala
  11. Prepare 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  12. Prepare 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  13. Take 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  14. Get 1 teaspoon hot salsa

Instructions to make Tricolored peppers potato:
  1. First deep fry diced potatoes.make brown them
  2. Now in one pan put some oil and roast cumin seeds+ginger+garlic+onion
  3. Roast at medium flame until it spreads oil
  4. Now add salt and turmeric only and put peppers
  5. Keep it on low flame for 3 to 4 minutes
  6. Now add fried potatoes and all the spices left
  7. Cover the utensil for 5 minutes and uncover it until peppers looks very soft
  8. Now in the end put both sauces and turn the gas off.
  9. Optional garnish with green onions to decorate and make it more flavorful
