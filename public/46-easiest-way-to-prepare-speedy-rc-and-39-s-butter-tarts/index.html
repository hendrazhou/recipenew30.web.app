The ingredients needed to make RC's Butter Tarts:
  1. Prepare 1/3 cup butter
  2. Take 2 tbsp milk
  3. Take 1 egg, beaten
  4. Get 1 tsp vanilla
  5. Make ready 1 raisins
  6. Take 12 pre-baked pastry tart shells
  7. Prepare 1 cup packed brown sugar
Steps to make RC's Butter Tarts:
  1. Preheat oven to 450°
  2. Mix butter, brown sugar, milk, egg and vanilla in saucepan
  3. Heat on medium until mixture comes to a low boil. Make sure all ingredients are well mixed and butter is melted
  4. Put raisins in bottom of tart shells
  5. Pour melted mixture in tart shells
  6. Cook filled shells at 450° for 8 minutes, reduce heat to 350° and continue baking until lightly browned
