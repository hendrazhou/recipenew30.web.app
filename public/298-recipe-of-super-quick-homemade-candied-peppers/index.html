The ingredients needed to make Candied Peppers:
  1. Make ready 1 yellow bell pepper; julienne
  2. Get 1 red bell pepper; julienne
  3. Get 1 orange bell pepper; julienne
  4. Prepare 1 red bell pepper; julienne
  5. Prepare 1 yellow onion; julienne
  6. Prepare 1 poblano pepper; julienne
  7. Prepare 4 cloves garlic; slivered
  8. Prepare 1/2 C red wine vinegar
  9. Prepare 1/4 C honey
  10. Prepare 1 large pinch kosher salt
  11. Make ready as needed olive oil
Instructions to make Candied Peppers:
  1. Heat enough olive oil to cover the bottom of a large saute pan.
  2. Add peppers and onions. Season. Toss.
  3. Saute on high heat for 2 minutes to get a little sear on the peppers.
  4. Turn heat to medium-low. Cook another 6-8 minutes or until peppers begin to soften. Stir occasionally.
  5. Add garlic. Cook 1 minute.
  6. Add honey and red wine vinegar. Cook approximately 30 seconds to one minute or until vinegar is nearly dry.
