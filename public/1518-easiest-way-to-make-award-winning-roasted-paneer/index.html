Steps to make Roasted paneer:
  1. If you are using frozen paneer cubes,place the cubes in a bowl of hot water for around 5 min.
  2. Drain the. Water & pat dry with a tissue.
  3. Add turmeric powder,red chilli powder & salt in the bowl containing the cubes.mix well with a spoon Or your hands to coat the spices nicely on the cubes.
  4. Heat a tawa. Add the oil & spreda it on the tawa.
  5. Place the paneer cubes on the tawa.
  6. Cook till the bottom side turns golden brown
  7. Keep flipping all the sides with a spatula till they are golden brown.
  8. Sprinkle some chat masala.
