The ingredients needed to make Paneer and vegetables cutlets:
  1. Make ready 1 cup grated paneer
  2. Prepare 1 potato boiled & mashed
  3. Get 1 tsp ginger chilli paste
  4. Get 2 tbsp coriander finely chopped
  5. Prepare 4-5 Tbsp grated carrot
  6. Prepare 1/4 cup boiled peas
  7. Make ready 1/2 cup finely chopped cabbage
  8. Make ready 2 Tbsp cornflour
  9. Get 1 tsp salt
  10. Make ready 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  11. Take 2-3 Tbsp bread crumbs
  12. Prepare 4-5 Tbsp Oil for pan frying

Instructions to make Paneer and vegetables cutlets:
  1. Take all the ingredients in a big bowl (except oil)
  2. Mix and mash them well so that everything gets well combined
  3. Now take a small portion of the mixture and flatten slightly forming tikki.
  4. Use a cutter to give any shape if you want and put it on a greased plate. - Like this make all - I have used star shaped cutter
  5. Now heat oil in a pan and shallow fry on medium flame. - flip over once the base turns golden brown.
  6. Fry both sides until it turns golden brown and crisp.
  7. Enjoy crispy vegetable cutlets with tomato sauce and evening tea.
