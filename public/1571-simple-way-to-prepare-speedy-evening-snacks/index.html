The ingredients needed to make Evening snacks:
  1. Prepare 1 cup Suji
  2. Take 1 Chopped onion
  3. Take 2 tsp Chopped green chillies
  4. Prepare 3/4 tsp Coriander leaves
  5. Prepare 1 tsp Chopped ginger
  6. Get 2 tbsp Curd
  7. Take as required Oil for frying
  8. Prepare as required Water
  9. Prepare to taste Salt
  10. Get 1 tsp Jeera powder
  11. Get 1 tsp Black pepper powder
  12. Take 1/4 tsp Garam masala
  13. Get 2 tsp Boil pea's

Steps to make Evening snacks:
  1. Firstly take a bowl,put the suji add curd, water as required..mix and make a slurry now add salt,jeera Powder, garam masala, black pepper powder,keep it cover for 15 minutes.
  2. Now heat the Pan,put oil after that pour slurry of suji into the Pan and stir continuously cook it on low flame for 5 minutes after that it Will make like soft dough now off flamel et it cool completely.
  3. Now take Dough in bowl,add onion, ginger, green chillies, coriander leaves, peas mix it well and make a cutlets.
  4. Now heat oil,when oil is hot then fry all cutlets until golden colour fry it for 5-8 minutes.Ready to serve suji cutlets.
