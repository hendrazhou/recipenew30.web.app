The ingredients needed to make Plum & Ginger Pork Chops:
  1. Take 2 thick pork chops
  2. Take 2.4 oz plum paste
  3. Take 1/3 C heavy cream
  4. Make ready 1 inch ginger; peeled and grated
  5. Get 2 t ground allspice
  6. Take 1/4 t garlic powder
  7. Prepare 1/2 t onion powder
  8. Prepare 1 pinch kosher salt and black pepper

Steps to make Plum & Ginger Pork Chops:
  1. Cover pork chops with enough oil to coat. Season with dried spices, salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat cream, plum paste, and ginger in a small saucepan to a simmer. Puree with an immersion blender or pour into a food processor.
  3. Bake chops at 350° for approximately 25 minutes or until pork reaches desired doneness. Pour plum cream sauce over chops during last 5 minutes of cooking.
