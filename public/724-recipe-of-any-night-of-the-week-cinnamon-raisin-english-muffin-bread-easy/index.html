The ingredients needed to make Cinnamon Raisin English Muffin Bread / Easy!!!:
  1. Prepare breads
  2. Prepare 5 cup bread flour
  3. Make ready 5 tsp yeast
  4. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp baking soda
  5. Take 2 tsp salt
  6. Get 1 tsp brown sugar
  7. Get 2 tbsp cinnamon
  8. Make ready 3 1/2 cup milk, heated to 120-125 degrees
  9. Prepare 1 1/2 cup raisins
  10. Get 1 non stick spray to use on plastic wrap and loaf pans
  11. Prepare 1/4 cup corn meal
Instructions to make Cinnamon Raisin English Muffin Bread / Easy!!!:
  1. Soak raisins in cold milk and set aside.
  2. In large bowl mix flour, yeast, brown sugar, salt, cinnamon, baking soda. Combine well.
  3. Heat milk with raisins to 120°F in small sauce pan. Using a thermometer gets you to perfect temp.
  4. Pour into dry ingredients and stir until all is well incorporated.
  5. Spray a piece of plastic wrap with non stick spray or brush lightly with oil. Place greased side down on dough, seal edges and rest one hour in warm, draft free area.
  6. Prepare 2 , 8 inch loaf pans or 1, 10" pan by greasing or spraying with non stick spray. Take corn meal and sprinkle 2 tablespoons into each pan. Coat sides and discard any leftover.
  7. Divide dough evenly amongst both pans or all into larger pan.This is a very wet, sticky dough.
  8. Cover loaf pans with greased plastic wrap. Let rest another one half hour.
  9. In preheated 400°F oven on middle rack, place loaves.
  10. Bake 15 minutes, rotate and bake another 15 minutes.
  11. Let loaves cool 15 minutes. Slice english muffins to your desired thickness and toast.
  12. Top with my cinnamon honey butter. - - https://cookpad.com/us/recipes/369064-cinnamon-honey-compound-butter
  13. Or just plain butter, jams, and enjoy! You may also use craisins, dried blueberries to substitute raisins! This is the best english muffin I have ever had!
