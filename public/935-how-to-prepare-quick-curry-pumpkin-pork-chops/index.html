The ingredients needed to make Curry-Pumpkin Pork Chops:
  1. Make ready 2 pork chops
  2. Get 2 tsp Madras curry powder
  3. Get 3/4 tsp garlic powder
  4. Get 1/3 tsp ground white pepper
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp onion powder
  6. Make ready 1 pumpkin pie spice; to taste
  7. Prepare 1 heaping spoonful easy pumpkin pie mix per chop
  8. Take 1 pinch kosher salt
  9. Prepare 1 peanut oil; as needed
Steps to make Curry-Pumpkin Pork Chops:
  1. Cover pork chops with enough oil to coat. Season with curry powder, onion powder, garlic powder, and white pepper.
  2. Bake at 300° for approximately 20 minutes or until pork reaches desired doneness.
  3. After chops are cooked, top with pumpkin pie mix. Sprinkle pumpkin pie spice over the pumpkin puree.
