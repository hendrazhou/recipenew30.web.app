The ingredients needed to make Chicken cheese seekh:
  1. Get 1/2 kg chicken machine kheema
  2. Take 6-8 green chillies
  3. Get 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  4. Get 1 tbsp dhaniya powder
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp zeera powder
  6. Take 1 tsp garam masala
  7. Take 1 bunch kotmir (mutthi)
  8. Prepare 1 bunch pudina(mutthi)
  9. Make ready 1 tbsp ghee
  10. Prepare 2 tbsp onion birasta crushed
  11. Take block Cheese
  12. Take Garlic sauce ingredients:
  13. Get 1 box curd(drain water)
  14. Make ready 2/3 garlic cloves crushed

Instructions to make Chicken cheese seekh:
  1. Mix all the ingredients well except cheese.
  2. Make small patti of the chicken kheema.
  3. Make sticks of cheese. That is just cut the cheese in a long shape.
  4. Put the cheese stick in the Patti and roll it.
  5. When it's done just shallow fry it.
  6. For garlic sauce take curd and drain the water add 2/3 cloves of crushed garlic, white pepper and little salt and 1 tbsp mayonnaise. Mix it well.
