The ingredients needed to make Marinated Pork Chops and Cooked Vegetables.:
  1. Prepare 3 Pork Chops from butcher
  2. Get 1/2 Yellow Onion
  3. Prepare 1 Squash
  4. Get 1 Zuchini
  5. Prepare 1 Tomato
  6. Take 2 Whole White Mushrooms
  7. Make ready Salt & Pepper
  8. Prepare Olive Oil
Instructions to make Marinated Pork Chops and Cooked Vegetables.:
  1. Place Pork Chops (3) and desired marinade in gallon sized zip-lock bag and place bag in refrigerator for 2-5 hours.
  2. Grill pork chops on medium high heat
  3. Sautée onions until tender, then add in sliced mushrooms, zuchini, and squash.
  4. When all veggies are tender add in tomatoes for a few minutes.
  5. Cut pork chops into strips.
  6. Garnish with avocado.
