The ingredients needed to make Cauliflower buns:
  1. Prepare 1 large head of cauliflower
  2. Make ready 1-1/2 cups mozzarella cheese i used 2% milk
  3. Take 3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  4. Take as needed nonstick spray
  5. Get 2 large eggs
  6. Make ready to taste ground black pepper
  7. Prepare to taste seasoned salt
  8. Prepare 1 blender

Instructions to make Cauliflower buns:
  1. Preheat oven 400 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Take the blender and blend the cauliflower making rice like pieces
  3. Get the cheeses, and eggs to room temperature beat the eggs.
  4. On a large pan spread the riced cauliflower.put into oven 20 minutes. take out and stir. spread out and let cool.
  5. Mix all the ingredients together squeeze out any excessive liquids.
  6. Form into patties spray pan with nonstick. make sure pan is warm.
  7. Add patties to pan,. add spices. bake in oven 25-30 minutes
  8. Put in broiler till crisp on top.
  9. You can chill in the refrigerator the day before. you can fry them to heat back up or warm in oven or microwave
  10. Hope you enjoy!
