The ingredients needed to make Black Bean and Burger Casserole:
  1. Take Bottom layer
  2. Get 30 ounces black beans
  3. Make ready 2 teaspoons chives
  4. Take To taste pink Himalayan salt
  5. Prepare 1 teaspoon granulated onion powder
  6. Get 1 teaspoon granulated garlic powder
  7. Prepare Middle layer
  8. Make ready 2 pound ground beef
  9. Prepare 1 teaspoon pink Himalayan salt
  10. Make ready 1 teaspoon granulated garlic powder
  11. Get 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  12. Get Top layer
  13. Prepare 15 ounces diced tomatoes
  14. Take 1 pound shredded mozzarella cheese divided
Instructions to make Black Bean and Burger Casserole:
  1. Add the beans to a 9x11 pan. Add a bit of salt, 1 teaspoon garlic and onion powder. Sprinkle the chives over the beans. Cover with half the cheese.
  2. Flatten the ground beef and lay it over the beans till they are covered. Season the beef with salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Add the diced tomatoes. Top with the remaining cheese.
