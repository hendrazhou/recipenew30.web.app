  1. Make ready 1/2 onion, diced
  2. Get 1 garlic cloves, minced
  3. Prepare 2 tsp Fresh ginger, minced
  4. Prepare 1 tsp Coconut oil
  5. Prepare 1 1/2 cup parboiled rice
  6. Make ready 1 can Coconut milk (400ml)
