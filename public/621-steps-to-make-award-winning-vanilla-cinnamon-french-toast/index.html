The ingredients needed to make Vanilla cinnamon French toast:
  1. Prepare 4 bread slices
  2. Get 2 eggs, large
  3. Prepare 2.5 ml cinnamon, ground
  4. Take 1 ml vanilla extract
  5. Make ready 20 ml milk
  6. Make ready Pinch salt
  7. Prepare As needed Spray & Cook
  8. Make ready 1 banana
  9. Take TT honey

Steps to make Vanilla cinnamon French toast:
  1. Whisk eggs, cinnamon, vanilla, milk and salt together in a shallow wide bowl
  2. Spray & Cook frying pan. Heat pan to medium-high
  3. Dip bread both sides in egg-mixture
  4. Fry bread until golden on both sides
  5. Serve immediately with banana and honey
