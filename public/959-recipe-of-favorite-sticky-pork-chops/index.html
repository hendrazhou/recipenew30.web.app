The ingredients needed to make Sticky pork chops:
  1. Make ready 1 packages boneless pork chops (4-6)
  2. Get 3 tbsp water
  3. Make ready pork rub
  4. Take 1 jar Safeway selects soy honey garlic sauce

Steps to make Sticky pork chops:
  1. Rub the pork rub on both sides of your pork chops.
  2. Put 3 tablespoons of water into a skillet may need to add a little more water while cooking! Cook on medium heat.
  3. Place pork chops into your skillet. Spread about a tablespoon on each pork chop then cover with a lid and allow the pork chops to brown slightly.
  4. Then flip the chops and spread another tablespoon on them. Repeat step three and four until pork is fully cooked and the sauce that mixed with the water thickens up. Serve with your favorite side dishes!
