The ingredients needed to make Oil free Pepper soup and pounded yam:
  1. Prepare Half tuber of Yam
  2. Prepare Medium size Dry fish
  3. Prepare 1 :2 Chicken part
  4. Get 1 Okpeyi (local spice)
  5. Make ready Yellow pepper and Cameroon pepper (pounded)
  6. Make ready Pepper soup spices
  7. Get Uziza and utazi
  8. Prepare Onion
  9. Get 2 Seasoning cubes
  10. Make ready Crayfish (grounded)
  11. Get to taste salt

Instructions to make Oil free Pepper soup and pounded yam:
  1. Peel and Cut yam, wash and cook until soft. Pound until very smooth, put small portion aside for thickener and put others in a food flask.
  2. Wash dry fish and chicken, add seasoning cubes, pepper, crayfish and salt then cook until the meat is done
  3. Pour a little more water, mould and add the yam- for thickener, add the remaining crayfish, pepper soup spices, enough pepper, okpeyi, seasoning cubes, uziza, utazi and salt. Stir and check the ingredients to taste.
  4. Allow to cook for 5mins. PS- you can put a drop of palm oil just to give it a facelift🥰
