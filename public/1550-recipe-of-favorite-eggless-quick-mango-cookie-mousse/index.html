The ingredients needed to make Eggless Quick Mango Cookie Mousse:
  1. Make ready 10 oatmeal digestive cookies
  2. Get 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  3. Prepare 2 medium size mangoes, chopped
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup thick whipped cream
  5. Take 2 tablespoons vanilla flavoured thick cooked custard
  6. Make ready 1 tablespoon honey - add more or less for your preferred sweetness
  7. Get For the garnish
  8. Prepare 2 tablespoons mango chunks
  9. Make ready 1 tablespoon chopped dry fruits
  10. Get 1 tablespoon any fruit preserve or chocolates or chocolate syrup
Instructions to make Eggless Quick Mango Cookie Mousse:
  1. Crush the cookies to powder in a grinder
  2. Melt the unsalted butter and mix it well with the powdered cookies
  3. Spread it as the base of mousse in the serving glasses.
  4. Press gently with the back of a spoon and chill for one hour in the refrigerator.
  5. Meanwhile in a blender, add the mangoes, custard and honey and blend until smooth.
  6. Now add and fold in the whipped cream until smooth and mixed evenly.
  7. Pour it over the biscuit base of each serving glass equally.
  8. Chill the mousse to set for 2 hours.
  9. Garnish the mango mousse with mango chunks, chocolate syrup or chopped dry fruits or any fruit preserve and enjoy the delicious mango mousse.
