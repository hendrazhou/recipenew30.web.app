The ingredients needed to make Quick and Easy Pepper Steak Sauce:
  1. Make ready 7 Oz. Heavy Cream
  2. Take 3 Oz. Fresh Milk
  3. Make ready 2 Tablespoons Salted Butter
  4. Make ready 1 Tablespoon Whole Black Pepper Grains
  5. Take To Taste Salt
Instructions to make Quick and Easy Pepper Steak Sauce:
  1. Place the Black Pepper on a pan at low heat and toast the grains.
  2. Grind the toasted Black Pepper grains until you get a thin dust.
  3. Place the Black Pepper along with the Salted Butter ok a pan at low heat.
  4. Add Heavy Cream to the mix and let it cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Add fresh milk. Cook for 2 more minutes And add Salt to taste. Serve and enjoy!
