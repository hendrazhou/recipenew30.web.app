The ingredients needed to make The Best Biscuits, Gravy, Sausage and Bell Peppers EVER! 🙂:
  1. Get 1 can 12 biscuits
  2. Take 4 pounds sausage. I call it (the intestine)
  3. Take 8 eggs
  4. Take Garlic, black pepper and onion powder
  5. Make ready Pinch salt and table pepper
  6. Make ready 6 packs gravy
  7. Take 3 bell peppers. (Colored)
