The ingredients needed to make Cinnamon Sugar Baked Donuts:
  1. Make ready 1 Baking Spray
  2. Take 2 cup all-purpose flour
  3. Prepare 1 1/2 cup sugar
  4. Make ready 2 tsp baking powder
  5. Take 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
  7. Take 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  8. Get 1 large egg (extra large) slightly beaten
  9. Take 1 1/4 cup whole milk
  10. Make ready 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  11. Get 2 tsp pure vanilla extract
  12. Get 1 stick unsalted butter
  13. Make ready 1/2 cup sugar
  14. Take 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
Instructions to make Cinnamon Sugar Baked Donuts:
  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F. Spray 2 doughnut pans well.
  2. Into a large bowl, sift together the flour, sugar, baking powder, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt. In a small bowl, whisk together the egg, milk, melted butter, and vanilla. Stir the wet mixture into the dry ingredients until just combined.
  3. Bake for 17 minutes pr until toothpick comes out clean.
  4. For the topping, melt the 8 tablespoons of butter in an 8-inch saute pan. Combine the sugar and cinnamon in a small bowl. Dip each doughnut first in the butter and then in the cinnamon sugar, either on one side or both sides.
