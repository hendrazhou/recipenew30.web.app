The ingredients needed to make Black Beans:
  1. Get 2 cans black beans
  2. Prepare 1 teaspoon chili powder
  3. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  4. Get pinch allspice
  5. Take 1 garlic clove, crushed and chopped
  6. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon salt
  7. Take 1/4 teaspoon sugar
  8. Prepare 1 chipotle pepper in mole, skinned, seeded, and minced
Steps to make Black Beans:
  1. Drain one of the two cans of black beans. Add both cans to a small pot and bring to simmer.
  2. Add spices, chopped garlic, and flesh of minced chipotle pepper in mole.
  3. Cook until garlic cooks through, beans soften, and liquid reduces.
