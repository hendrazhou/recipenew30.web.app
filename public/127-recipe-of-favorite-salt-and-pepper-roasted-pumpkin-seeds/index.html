The ingredients needed to make Salt and Pepper Roasted Pumpkin Seeds:
  1. Make ready 1 large pumpkin, just the seeds
  2. Make ready 5 tbsp olive oil, extra virgin
  3. Take 1 tsp ground black pepper
  4. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt, for initial seasoning
  5. Take 1/2 tsp salt, for second seasoning
Steps to make Salt and Pepper Roasted Pumpkin Seeds:
  1. Mix the olive oil and seeds in a bowl, and then spread on a large baking sheet.
  2. Add the pepper and the first half teaspoon of salt to the seeds evenly distributing.
  3. Roast at 375°F for 25 minutes.
  4. Stir, the seeds, and re-spread on the sheet.
  5. Distribute the rest of the salt and cook for another 3 or so minutes, until desired brownness.
  6. Serve and enjoy.
