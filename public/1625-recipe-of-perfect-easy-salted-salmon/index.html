The ingredients needed to make Easy salted salmon:
  1. Prepare 700 g salmon fillets
  2. Take 1 tbsp salt
  3. Take 1 tbsp brown sugar
  4. Make ready 1/2 tsp Santa Maria for fish and seafood
  5. Prepare 1 bay leaf
  6. Prepare Ground black pepper
