The ingredients needed to make Kadai paneer:
  1. Get 1 tsp oil
  2. Prepare 100 gms paneer chopped
  3. Take 1 onion chopped
  4. Make ready 1 capsicum chopped
  5. Take 1/2 tomato chopped
  6. Get 1-2 red chilli whole
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp kasoori methi
  8. Make ready Pinch chilli powder
  9. Get to taste Salt
  10. Get 1/2 tsp whole coriander or dhania
  11. Get tbsp Red gravy -2
  12. Prepare 1 tsp Garam masala
  13. Get 1 tsp cream
  14. Take 1 tsp butter
  15. Get 1 tsp mawa and 1/2 tsp ghee
  16. Prepare 2 tsp milk
  17. Make ready 2 tsp lemon juice
  18. Make ready Coriander leaves finely chopped
