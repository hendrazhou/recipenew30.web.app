The ingredients needed to make Hot chocolate with spiced pumpkin ☕️🧡:
  1. Take 1 cup milk (I use soy milk)
  2. Prepare 2 tbsp good chocolate powder
  3. Get 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp Spiced pumpkin purée
  5. Prepare Ready make whipping cream or spray cream (to top up), optional
