The ingredients needed to make Simmered Barbecued Pepper Chops:
  1. Get 3-1/3 pounds center cut pork chops
  2. Prepare 28 ounce your favorite barbecue sauce, I used hickory brown sugar, sweet baby rays
  3. Prepare 1 large bell pepper, green
  4. Take 1 medium yellow onion
  5. Get 1 teaspoon granulated garlic powder
  6. Prepare 1 teaspoon salt
Steps to make Simmered Barbecued Pepper Chops:
  1. Dice the peppers
  2. Dice the onions
  3. Rinse the chops add salt to both sides of the chops.
  4. Layer the chops on top of the onions and peppers
  5. Then cover with sauce.
  6. Add the rest of the chops
  7. Cover with sauce and garlic.
  8. Simmer covered for an hour till done.
  9. Serve I hope you enjoy!
