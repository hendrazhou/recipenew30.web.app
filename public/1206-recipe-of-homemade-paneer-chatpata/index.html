The ingredients needed to make Paneer Chatpata:
  1. Prepare 150 gm paneer
  2. Get 1/2 spoon Turmeric powder
  3. Take 1/3 spoon red chilli powder
  4. Take to taste Salt
  5. Get 1/2 tsp Black pepper powder
  6. Prepare 2 spoon cooking oil

Steps to make Paneer Chatpata:
  1. Cut paneer in cubs.add all dry spices (red chilli powder salt turmericpowder salt black peper coat paneer with all this dry spices for 10-15 min.
  2. Heat up oil in pan add coated paneer (don't make crowd) shallow fry on slow flame
  3. Remove on serving plate add chat masala.
  4. Your tasty easy chatpati paneer ready to eat. Enjoy
