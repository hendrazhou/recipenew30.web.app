The ingredients needed to make Black Pepper Steak and Mushroom:
  1. Make ready Ingredients for steak:
  2. Prepare 1 pound rib eye steak about 500g, cubed (about 1/2 inch pieces)
  3. Prepare 1 tsp cornstarch
  4. Get 1 tbsp rice wine
  5. Get 1 tbsp lime juice
  6. Take 2 tsp light soy sauce
  7. Get 2 tsp dark soy sauce
  8. Get 2 tbsp grated ginger
  9. Take 1/4 tsp black pepper or to taste
  10. Take Other ingredients:
  11. Make ready 8 button mushrooms thinly sliced
  12. Prepare 1 tbsp garlic finely chopped
  13. Prepare 1 tbsp oyster sauce
Instructions to make Black Pepper Steak and Mushroom:
  1. In a Ziploc bag, mix all the ingredients for the steak and marinate for about one hour.
  2. Line the fryer basket with a sheet of lightly greased aluminum foil.
  3. Put the steak inside the fryer basket and air fry at 380F (190C) for about 5 minutes.
  4. Add all other ingredients to the steak and stir. Air fry at 380F (190C) for another 4-5 minutes or until the desired doneness is reached.
  5. Carefully pour the drippings from aluminum foil into a wok and bring it to boil. Stir constantly until the sauce thickens.
