The ingredients needed to make Boneless Pork Chops In A Cherry Sauce:
  1. Take sauce
  2. Prepare 1 tsp olive oil, extra virgin
  3. Prepare 3/4 cup fresh black cherries, pitted
  4. Get 1 1/2 tbsp shallots, finely chopped
  5. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp balsamic syrup, recipe attatched
  6. Take 1/2 cup vegetable stock
  7. Get pork chops
  8. Get 2 , 3/4 " thick boneless chops
  9. Prepare 1/2 tbsp olive oil, extra virgin
  10. Take 1/4 tsp salt
  11. Get 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  12. Prepare 1/8 tsp ground pepper

Steps to make Boneless Pork Chops In A Cherry Sauce:
  1. In a skillet, heat oil for the pork chops over medium heat.
  2. Season the flour with the salt and pepper. Dredge the chops in the flour.
  3. Cook chops 3 minutes each side. Remove from pan and place on a plate covered with aluminum foil to keep warm. Now start the sauce
  4. In the same pan that you used to cook chops add the extra oil for the sauce.
  5. Saute the shallots in oil over medium heat until they become transparent. About 2 minutes.
  6. Add the cherries, balsamic syrup and vegetable stock. - - https://cookpad.com/us/recipes/346062-homemade-balsamic-syrup
  7. Cook for 10 minutes until cherries soften and sauce reduces by 1/4.
  8. Place chops back into pan to warm through.
  9. Serve with sauce. A nice accompaniment is mashed potatoes , couscous or a simple risotto.
