The ingredients needed to make Paneer pakoda:
  1. Take 200 gms besan
  2. Take 1 tsp cumin seeds
  3. Prepare 1 tsp red chilli powder
  4. Prepare 2 tsp coriander leaves chopped
  5. Take Pinch hing
  6. Prepare 1 tsp haldi
  7. Make ready to taste Salt
  8. Get 1/2 tsp baking powder for pakoda softness
  9. Get as required Water
  10. Make ready 200 gms paneer
  11. Take as required green chutney, lasun chutney for felling
  12. Make ready 1 tsp chaat masala
  13. Get As required Oil for frying

Instructions to make Paneer pakoda:
  1. First you can take one bowl and add besan, red chilli powder, salt, baking powder, coriander leaves and cumin seeds.
  2. And add water as per your need but it should not more thin or more thick. And prepare besan mixture.
  3. As per your choice prepare green chutney or lasun chutney for filling. I have green chutney.
  4. And take paneer and cut rectangle shape.
  5. And take a one paneer piece and apply green chutney on top and again cover paneer slice. One by one prepare paneer and chutney filling.
  6. And use spoon to place stuffed paneer in to besan mixture and then fry in to oil in pan.
  7. And both side fry in golden brown. And remove on pan.
  8. And serve hot with chutney and onion.
