The ingredients needed to make Squash & Green Pepper Rice:
  1. Prepare 1 C yellow squash; small dice
  2. Get 1 yellow onion; small dice
  3. Prepare 1/2 C green bell pepper; small dice
  4. Take 2 cloves garlic; creamed
  5. Take 4 T butter
  6. Make ready 2 C vegetable stock
  7. Make ready 1 C rice
  8. Make ready 1 t smoked paprika
  9. Get 1/2 t chili powder
  10. Get 1 C shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  11. Take 1 large pinch kosher salt & black pepper
  12. Take as needed olive oil

Instructions to make Squash & Green Pepper Rice:
  1. Heat enough oil to cover the bottom of a medium sized saucepot. Add 2 T butter.
  2. Saute squash, onion, and bell pepper with a pinch of salt and pepper on medium heat for approximately 3-4 minutes, or until squash is caramelized. Add garlic during last 30 seconds of cooking.
  3. Transfer 3/4 the veggies to a food processor. Transfer remaining veggies to a plate. Puree until smooth.
  4. Add enough oil to cover the bottom of the saucepot again. Add rice, chili powder, smoked paprika, and a pinch of salt and pepper. Stir. Cook on medium heat 1 minute.
  5. Add veggies, veggie puree, and vegetable stock. Stir. Bring to a simmer. Cover. Simmer 15 minutes or until water is evaporated. Remove from heat and do not uncover. Let steam 5-7 minutes.
  6. Dice remaining butter. Fold butter and cheese into rice with a fork.
