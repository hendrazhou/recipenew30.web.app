Steps to make Paneer kheer:
  1. Boil milk
  2. Grate paneer
  3. Boil milk till the quantity reduced to half
  4. Add grated paneer and sugar in it
  5. Add condensed milk for the nice taste
  6. Let it cook till milk gets thickened and quantity reduced to 1/4
  7. Delicious paneer kheer ready,garnish with dryfruits of your choice
