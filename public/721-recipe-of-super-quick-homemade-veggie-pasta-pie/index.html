The ingredients needed to make Veggie pasta pie:
  1. Make ready 1 red onion
  2. Make ready 1 large capsicum
  3. Take 1 cup wheat pasta
  4. Take 1 cup lentils
  5. Take 1 chopped tomato
  6. Make ready 2 boiled mashed potatoes
  7. Get As needed Cooking oil spray
  8. Get 1 tsp salt
  9. Take 1 tsp vinegar
  10. Take 2-4 Cheese slice
  11. Prepare 2-3 spoon skimmed milk
  12. Prepare as needed Fresh parsley

Instructions to make Veggie pasta pie:
  1. Heat the oven at 200c, spread med saucepan with a little olive oil b set over medium heat.
  2. Add the onion, capsicum and cook for 5min,add the garlic and cook for another 1min.
  3. Now add lentils,tomato,vinegar and salt to the pan and bring to the boil.
  4. Reduce the heat and simmer for 20min.
  5. 5.meanwhile cook the pasta with white sauce and keep aside.
  6. Now cook the potatoes in a large pan of boiling water for 15 min.
  7. Mashed potato with the milk.
  8. Spoon the lentils mixture into preheat dish now layers of pasta.
  9. Top with the mashed potatoes then sprinkle with the cheese slices.
  10. Bake for 15-20 min until golden and piping hot then garnish with the fresh parsley.
