The ingredients needed to make Garlic Black Pepper and Cheese Flatbread:
  1. Prepare 1 package rapid rise dry yeast
  2. Make ready 1 cup warm water
  3. Get 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  4. Prepare 3 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  5. Take 1/4 cup full fat sour cream
  6. Take 3/4 teaspoon salt
  7. Prepare 2 tablespoons fresh grated romano cheese
  8. Make ready 1 teaspoon fresh cracked black pepper
  9. Take 2 teaspoons baking powder
  10. Take 1 large egg
  11. Get 4 cloves garlic, minced
  12. Make ready 2 tablespoons olive oil, I used chili infused
  13. Take 2 tablespoons butter
Steps to make Garlic Black Pepper and Cheese Flatbread:
  1. Whisk yeast, warm water and sugar in a bowl. Let sit 5 minutes
  2. In a large bowl whisk flour, romano cheese, salt, pepper, baking powder and garlic
  3. Whisk in egg and sour cream
  4. With a fork add yeast mixture until a dough forms
  5. Then knead dogh on a floured surface until a soft sticky dough forms
  6. Place dough in a non stick sprayed bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Place in a warm spot until doubled in size, 1 to 2 hours
  8. Combine butter, melted with olive oil in a small bowl
  9. Cut 8 portion of dough
  10. Roll each portion into 6 to 8 inch ovals
  11. Heat a non stick skillet with a brushing of the butter/ oil mixture
  12. Add rolled dough, cooking just until golden then flip and cook other side about 1 to 2 minutes per side
  13. This bread is delicious served with soups and stews, or topped with cheese and meats and vegetables. It also freezes well, individually wrapped
