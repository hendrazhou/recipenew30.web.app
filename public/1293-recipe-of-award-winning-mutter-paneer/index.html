  1. Make ready 200 gms green peas

  3. Make ready 1 big onion
  4. Make ready 2 tomatoes
  5. Get 6,7 cashews
  6. Make ready Kitchen king masala
  7. Take Red chili powder
  8. Prepare Garam masala
  9. Make ready to taste Salt
  10. Prepare Oil
