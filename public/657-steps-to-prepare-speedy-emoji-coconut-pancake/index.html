The ingredients needed to make Emoji coconut Pancake:
  1. Prepare 1 Cup self raising flour
  2. Take 2 large eggs
  3. Take 1 1/2 cup coconut milk
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp desicated coconut
  5. Make ready 1 tbsp sunflower or vegetable oil
  6. Prepare Cooking oil spray
  7. Take Emoji pancake pan
  8. Get Preferred fruits

Steps to make Emoji coconut Pancake:
  1. Mix all the ingredients in a large mixing bowl. Leave pancake batter to rest for at least 15 min. Pan on low heat, use cooking oil spray spray on your pan. Use a clean brush to spread all the oil all over.
  2. Pour some pancake mixture on the pan.
  3. Wait until one side cooked then turn over
  4. Put some dairy free butter on top if you prefer, add any preferred fruits. I use blackberry, grape and orange. Drizzle with a little bit of honey üçØ
