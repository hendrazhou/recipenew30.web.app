The ingredients needed to make Pizza Puto:
  1. Make ready 1/2 cup flour
  2. Get 1/3 cup sugar
  3. Prepare 1/2 tbsp baking powder
  4. Take 1 egg
  5. Prepare 2 ounce evaporated milk
  6. Prepare 1/4 cup water
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp melted butter
  8. Get 1/2 tsp vanilla
  9. Get 10 small slices of cheese
  10. Get 10 small slices of ham
  11. Take 10 small slices of hotdog
Steps to make Pizza Puto:
  1. Sift flour.
  2. Mix all dry ingredients (flour, baking powder, sugar).
  3. Mix all wet ingredients (egg, milk, water, butter, vanila)
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients. Mix well.
  5. Grease moulds with oil.
  6. Pour batter til 3/4 full.
  7. Top with cheese, ham and hotdog.
  8. Steam in already boiling water for 15 minutes.
  9. Remove from heat.
  10. Allow to cool before removing from mold.
  11. Remove from mold and enjoy!
