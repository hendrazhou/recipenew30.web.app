The ingredients needed to make Salt and Pepper Beef Boti:
  1. Take To Taste Salt
  2. Take 1 Tbsp Black Pepper crushed, Salt Crushed
  3. Prepare 2Tbsp Black Pepper Whole
  4. Take 2 Tbsp Cooking oil
  5. Get Half kg Beef cut in small cubes
  6. Get 2 Tbsp Lehsan Adrak Paste
Instructions to make Salt and Pepper Beef Boti:
  1. Wash the beef cubes with water..
  2. Add The beef in a Deep Pan with 6-7 Glass of tap Water, add ginger garlic paste,, salt, whole black pepper,and cover with lid, on medium high Flame, (keep watching so that the water doesn't fall out)Boil the beef until it's Ready fully..
  3. Now remove the Beef from pan and warm up another pan with 2 tbsp oil, when oil is heated add the cubes in oil and fry until the beef Dries up it's water,, add salt and pepper crushed while frying, as per taste..(you can add after removing them from pan also) when the beef is dried out, the Boti are Ready to be served!
