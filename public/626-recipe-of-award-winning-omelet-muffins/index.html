The ingredients needed to make Omelet Muffins:
  1. Take 1 Can cooking Spray
  2. Take Ham
  3. Make ready Onion
  4. Make ready Tomatoe
  5. Get 1/2 Teaspoon Baking powder
  6. Make ready Cheese
  7. Prepare 1/4 Cup Milk
  8. Take 1/2 Teaspoon Coooking Oil
  9. Get 4 Eggs
  10. Get Salt (To taste)
  11. Make ready Black pepper (To taste)
Steps to make Omelet Muffins:
  1. Spray your muffin pan.
  2. Add the filling (chopped) Half.
  3. Top with cheese
  4. Whip up the eggs and add baking powder, saot, pepper and oil.
  5. Pour into your muffins
  6. Bake at 375° for 20-25 minutes.
  7. Cool for 5 minutes. Top with desired topping. (Ketchup etc)
