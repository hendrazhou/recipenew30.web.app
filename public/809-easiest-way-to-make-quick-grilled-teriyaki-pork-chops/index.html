The ingredients needed to make Grilled teriyaki pork chops:
  1. Prepare 2 pork chops. About 8 oz. each
  2. Make ready Teriyaki sauce JON
  3. Prepare 1/3 cup soy sauce
  4. Make ready 1/3 cup water
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp. mirin (Japanese sweet rice wine)
  6. Make ready 4 tbsp. brown sugar
  7. Make ready 1 1/2 tsp minced ginger
  8. Prepare 2 minced garlic cloves
  9. Prepare 1 tsp. honey
  10. Make ready 1 tsp. rice vinegar
  11. Make ready 2 tsp. sesame oil
  12. Make ready 1/4 tsp. ground black pepper
  13. Take 3/4 tsp. cornstarch

Instructions to make Grilled teriyaki pork chops:
  1. In a bowl mix all the ingredients for the sauce, except the cornstarch. - Dry the pork chops with paper towel. - Marinate the pork chops with ¼ cup of the sauce.
  2. Dissolve the cornstarch with the rest of the sauce and cook slowly in a pot until thickens the sauce.
  3. Heat up a top stove griddle well. Mark and cook the pork chops on both sides. - Brush with the sauce over the pork chops while cooking.
  4. Serve the pork chops with some of the sauce.
