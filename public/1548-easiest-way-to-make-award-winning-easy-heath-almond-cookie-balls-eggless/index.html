The ingredients needed to make Easy Heath Almond Cookie balls (eggless):
  1. Make ready 1/2 cup margarine, softened
  2. Prepare 1/4 cup firm packed brown sugar
  3. Make ready 1 cup all purpose flour
  4. Get 1 tsp vanilla extract
  5. Get 1/2 tsp almond extract
  6. Make ready 1 cup heath bits
  7. Get 3/4 cup almond slivers

Instructions to make Easy Heath Almond Cookie balls (eggless):
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. In a medium bowl, mix all ingredients together until well combined. Should be slightly crumbly but moldable.
  3. For dough into 1 inch balls.
  4. Place on parchment lined cookie sheet.
  5. Bake 15-20 minutes or until bottoms are golden.
  6. Cool on wire rack.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
  8. Makes about 20-24 balls.
