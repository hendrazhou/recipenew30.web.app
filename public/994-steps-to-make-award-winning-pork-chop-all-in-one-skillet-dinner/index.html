The ingredients needed to make PORK CHOP ALL IN ONE SKILLET DINNER:
  1. Get 1 Tbl unsalted butter
  2. Make ready 4 1/2 Inch center cut pork chops
  3. Get 1 salt & pepper to taste
  4. Get 8 Baby cremini mushrooms(MORE IF YOU LIKE)
  5. Prepare 1 I Small onion chopped
  6. Get 1 garlic clove minced
  7. Take 1 stalk celery chopped
  8. Take 8 small red potatoes cut in half
  9. Get 1 small bag of baby carrots
  10. Get 1 I Can cream of mushroom soup
  11. Take 1/4 cup water or chicken broth
  12. Make ready 1 MRS DASH original seasoning, to taste

Steps to make PORK CHOP ALL IN ONE SKILLET DINNER:
  1. MELT 1 TBL BUTTER IN LARGE NON STICK SKILLET. - SALT AND PEPPER CHOPS. SAUTE TILL GOLDEN BROWN BOTH SIDES(DO NOT OVER COOK). REMOVE FROM SKILLET AND SET ASIDE.
  2. CHOP UP ALL VEGGIES AND SAUTE IN SAME SKILLET CHOPS WHERE DONE IN(CAN ADD OTHER TBL BUTTER IF NEEDED) ADD CHOPS BACK TO SKILLET.
  3. IN BOWL MIX CAN OF SOUP AND WATER(or broth). - POUR OVER CHOPS AND VEGGIES. SEASON WITH MRS DASH. TURN HEAT TO LOW AND SIMMER TILL CHOPS ARE DONE.
