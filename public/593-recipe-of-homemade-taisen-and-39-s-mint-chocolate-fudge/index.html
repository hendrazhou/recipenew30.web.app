  4. Take 1 foil
  5. Make ready 1 pan for setting
  6. Make ready 1 cooking spray

Instructions to make Taisen's mint chocolate fudge:
  1. Measure out chocolate and mint chips.
  2. Take foil and line your pan with it. Spray it with cooking spray so the chocolate doesn't stick
  3. Open can of milk
  4. Pour milk into a saucepan. Add all of the chocolate and mint chocolate chips. Put on medium heat. Stir
  5. Continue stirring and melt down the chips until smooth and creamy.
  6. Spread the mixture evenly into your foil lined pan. You can top this with anything you like from sprinkles, to nuts to extra chips or candy canes !
  7. Cover and place in fridge for at least 2 hours
  8. Pull out the fudge from the fridge and then pull the foil out. No mess :)
  9. Cut into small squares. To store use wax paper in between the fudge. Place wax paper , then fudge , wax paper then fudge so they don't stick together. Enjoy !
