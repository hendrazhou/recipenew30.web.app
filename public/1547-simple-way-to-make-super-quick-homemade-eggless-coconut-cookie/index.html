The ingredients needed to make Eggless coconut cookie:
  1. Prepare 2 cup all purpose flour
  2. Make ready 1 tsp baking soda
  3. Prepare 1 tsp baking powder
  4. Prepare 1 cup butter
  5. Take 1.5 cup Unsweetend desiccated Coconut
  6. Make ready 1 pinch Salt
  7. Take 1 cup Sugar
  8. Make ready 5 teaspoon Milk
  9. Get Few drops of Vanilla essence

Instructions to make Eggless coconut cookie:
  1. Assemble dry ingredients. Flour, baking soda, salt and baking powder
  2. Sift dry ingredients together
  3. In a mixing bowl,add butter and sugar
  4. Cream sugar and butter together. Add desiccated coconut
  5. Add flour
  6. Vanilla essence and milk.
  7. Mix until it forms a soft dough. The dough should not be sticky or runny. It should be well-binded.
  8. Shape as logs. Wrap cookie dough in a plastic wrap and chill in refrigerator for 30 minutes as the dough performs best when chilled.
  9. Grease a baking tray
  10. Take chilled dough logs out of the refrigerator and cut in desired shape.
  11. Start arranging the cookies on the greased baking sheet
  12. Bake at 300 degree F for 15-20 minutes
  13. Crispy cookies are ready to be eaten.
