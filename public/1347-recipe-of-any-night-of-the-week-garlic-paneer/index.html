The ingredients needed to make Garlic Paneer:
  1. Make ready 4 tbsp Oil
  2. Take 2 tbsp Garlic, chopped
  3. Take 1 tbsp Red Chilli Powder
  4. Take 2 tsp Cumin-coriander Powder
  5. Take 1 pinch Salt
  6. Get 1 1/2 cup Paneer cubes
  7. Get 2 tsp Soy Sauce
  8. Take 2 tsp Lemon Juice
  9. Prepare as required Coriander Leaves and Spring Onions for garnishing

Instructions to make Garlic Paneer:
  1. In a pan heat oil on medium flame.
  2. Add chopped garlic and saute.
  3. Add red chilli powder and saute.
  4. Add cumin-coriander powder, salt and saute.
  5. Add paneer cubes, mix and cook for 2 minutes.
  6. Add soy sauce and cook for 2 minutes. Add lemon juice, mix well and turn off the flame.
  7. Transfer it to the bowl and garnish with spring onions and coriander.
  8. You can top up with some grated cheese as well. Serve hot.
