The ingredients needed to make Coriander & Lemon Pork Chops:
  1. Make ready 2 pork chops (rib eye chops shown)
  2. Make ready 1 lemon; zested and juiced
  3. Prepare 2 tsp ground coriander
  4. Take 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  5. Take 1/2 tsp ground mace
  6. Get 1/2 tsp ground cardamom
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  8. Take 1/2 tsp turmeric
  9. Make ready 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
  10. Prepare 1/3 tsp ground white pepper
  11. Get 1 pinch kosher salt
  12. Take 1 vegetable oil; as needed

Instructions to make Coriander & Lemon Pork Chops:
  1. Crank the heat on a small non-stick saute pan. Combine spices except lemon zest and salt in a bowl. Put all spices into the pan and toss constantly until spices are fragrant. Do not burn spices.
  2. Cover chops with enough oil to coat. Season. Bake at 350° until pork reaches desired doneness. Garnish with lemon zest.
