The ingredients needed to make Cheesesteak Stuffed Peppers:
  1. Take 6 Red Bell Peppers
  2. Prepare 1 lb flank steak
  3. Make ready 1 tbsp olive oil
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp paprika
  5. Take 1 tbsp garlic powder
  6. Take 1 tsp pepper
  7. Take 2 tsp salt
  8. Get 2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  9. Get 2 tbsp butter
  10. Make ready 3 onions sliced
  11. Get 3 green bell peppers
  12. Prepare 15 slices provolone cheese
  13. Get 2 tbsp parsley
Instructions to make Cheesesteak Stuffed Peppers:
  1. Preheat oven to 400
  2. Cut steak into 1/8 strips
  3. In large skillet heat oil on medium heat. Add steak, garlic powder, paprika, have of pepper, half of salt and Worcestershire sauce.
  4. Mix well and cook meat through. Move only meat to bowl.
  5. Add butter to skillet and saute onions. Add rest of salt and pepper. Add sliced green pepper. Saute.
  6. Cut off top of red peppers and remove core and seeds.
  7. Cut 9 slices of provolone in half.
  8. Place red peppers in 9x13 pan. Put in some steak followed by peppers and onions topped with 3 half slices of cheese. Repeat layers but top with full slice of cheese.
  9. Bake for 30 minutes then top with parsley.
