The ingredients needed to make Stuffed Peppers Sausage, Beef, and Apples:
  1. Get 3-1/2 bell pepper different colored
  2. Make ready 1 pound ground sirloin
  3. Make ready 1 pound sausage
  4. Make ready 1 teaspoon pink Himalayan salt
  5. Take 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  6. Prepare 1 pound mixed vegetables
  7. Make ready to taste barbecue sauce I used peppered batch 89 optional
  8. Get 3 cups shredded apples
Instructions to make Stuffed Peppers Sausage, Beef, and Apples:
  1. Preheat oven 350 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Mix the pink Himalayan salt, sausage, beef, and apple.
  3. Wash and cut the peppers in half and stuff with the mixture
  4. Bake in oven for an hour and 15 minutes till done
  5. Cook the vegetables and add stuffed Peppers to the top and serve
  6. Add barbecue sauce if you desire. I hope you enjoy!
