The ingredients needed to make Waffle press omelette:
  1. Take 2 large eggs
  2. Take 1/4 cup milk
  3. Prepare 1/2 chopped tomato
  4. Get 1/4 chopped onion
  5. Get 1/4 cup cheese (grated)
  6. Prepare To taste salt and pepper
  7. Prepare Spray and cook for greasing

Instructions to make Waffle press omelette:
  1. Break the eggs mix with the milk. Add all the other ingredients - chopped tomato, onion, grated cheese, salt and pepper - and mix together
  2. Grease the waffle press and add ingredients to the press.
  3. Close and let it cook. About 5 minutes. Remove and serve.
