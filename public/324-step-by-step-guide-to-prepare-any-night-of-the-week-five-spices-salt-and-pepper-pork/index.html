The ingredients needed to make Five Spices Salt and Pepper Pork:
  1. Get Ingredients for pork:
  2. Make ready 1/2 pound pork shoulder cut into thick slices (about 250g)
  3. Prepare 2 Tablespoon soy sauce
  4. Make ready 1/2 Tablespoon rice wine
  5. Take 1 teaspoon corn starch
  6. Prepare 1 Tablespoon minced garlic
  7. Make ready 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  8. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  9. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon Chinese five spices powder
  10. Make ready 1/4 cup tapioca starch
  11. Make ready Other ingredients:
  12. Take to taste Salt
  13. Get to taste white pepper powder
  14. Take to taste Chili pepper thinly sliced to garnish
  15. Get Cilantro chopped to garnish

Steps to make Five Spices Salt and Pepper Pork:
  1. Marinate the meat with all the pork ingredients, except tapioca flour, for at least 1 hour.
  2. Dredge the pork slices in tapioca flour, shake off excess, and let sit for about 5-10 minutes until you don’t see dry flour.
  3. Place the meat in the fryer basket and spray some oil. Air Fry at 380F for 12-14 minutes, flip once in the middle until the surface appears to be nice and crisp.
  4. Toss in the pork slices with chili pepper and chopped cilantro. Then, sprinkle some salt and pepper to serve.
