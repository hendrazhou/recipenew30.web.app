The ingredients needed to make Paneer Dragon:
  1. Get 300 gms Paneer
  2. Make ready 30 gms Corn flour
  3. Make ready As required Oil for frying
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp Ginger
  5. Make ready 1 tbsp Garlic
  6. Take 1 Tsp Green chili
  7. Make ready 2 tbsp Tomato sauce
  8. Prepare 1 tbsp Chilli sauce
  9. Take 1/2 tbsp Soya sauce
  10. Take 1/4 cup Water
  11. Take 1 tbsp Coriander Leaves chopped.(also Required)
  12. Get to taste salt
  13. Make ready 3 tbsp Oil
  14. Prepare 1 large Onion
  15. Take 1 tsp. Red chili paste

Steps to make Paneer Dragon:
  1. Chop Paneer Like French Fries. Add Salt, Mix Well And Set Aside For 10 Minutes.
  2. Now Salt Will Release Its Moisture So It Will Be Easy For Cornflour To Get Coat.
  3. So Add Cornflour And Toss It Gently.
  4. Deep Fry Paneer Till Crisp And Golden.
  5. Next Step Is Preparing The Sauce. Heat A Pan And Add Oil For Seasoning. When It Is Hot Add Finely Chopped Ginger, Garlic & Green Chilli One By One.
  6. Further Add Chopped Onion And Saute Till Golden.
  7. Now Add Water. I Have Added Water To The Blender First In Which I Prepared Dry Red Chilli Paste And The Same Has Been Added To The Gravy.
  8. When Water Starts Boiling Add Dry Red Chilli Paste, Chilli Sauce 1tbsp, soya sauce 1/2 tbsp,And Tomato Sauce 2 tbsp.
  9. Mix Everything Well And Let It Cook For Next 30 Seconds Till Its Raw Smell Disappear.
  10. Now Add Fried Paneer And Start Combining Well.
  11. Let It Coat Nicely And Garnish With Chopped Coriander Leaves.
