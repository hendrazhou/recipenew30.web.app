The ingredients needed to make Cheesy Stuffed Green Bell Peppers:
  1. Take 4 large green bell peppers
  2. Prepare Salt
  3. Make ready 1 lb lean ground mince
  4. Prepare 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  5. Get 2 garlic cloves, minced
  6. Take 1 chicken (or beef) boullion cube
  7. Prepare 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  8. Prepare 1 teaspoon Greek seasoning
  9. Prepare 1 (14 oz) tin diced tomatoes
  10. Get 1 cup cooked long grain rice
  11. Make ready 1 cup (+ extra) grated hard cheese, eg cheddar
Instructions to make Cheesy Stuffed Green Bell Peppers:
  1. Cut off the tops of the green peppers so that they are all the same height. Remove and discard the seeds and membranes. Keep the tops.
  2. Finely chop the tops of the peppers after discarding the stalks.
  3. Place whole peppers in a pan of boiling water so they are completely covered. Boil for 5 minutes. Remove and drain upside down on paper towel.
  4. Lightly sprinkle the inside of the peppers with salt.
  5. Meanwhile, brown the ground beef in a skillet, breaking up any lumps. Add the chopped onion and tops of green pepper and cook until tender.
  6. Add the minced garlic, boullion cube, Worcestershire sauce and Greek seasoning. Stir and cook for 3 minutes.
  7. Add the tin of diced tomatoes and bring to a boil. Reduce heat. Add the cooked rice and cup of grated cheese. Stir to combine and heat through.
  8. Stuff the green peppers with the beef mixture.
  9. Place the stuffed green peppers in a baking dish, cover with foil and cook in 350°F oven for 25 minutes. Remove from oven, add remaining cheese to top of peppers and cook uncovered for a further 5 minutes.
  10. Serve with a vegetable side or salad. Enjoy!
