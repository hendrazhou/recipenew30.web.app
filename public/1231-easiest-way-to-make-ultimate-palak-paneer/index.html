Steps to make Palak paneer:
  1. Add 1 tbsp of oil in a pan. Add 1tbsp of kesuri methi and 1/2 tbsp chopped coriander. Place paneer cubes on pan. Sprinkle 1/4th tbsp turmeric powder 1/2 tbsp chilli powder and 1/2 tbsp Coriander powder. Fry them well on medium flame until golden brown. Keep it aside
  2. In the same pan, Add 2 tbsp of butter and 2tbsp of oil. Add Green chilles, finely chopped ginger and garlic
  3. Add chopped onion. Fry them until it becomes translucent. Add tomatoes and cook them until it becomes mushy
  4. Add tomato and onion paste. I've taken onion 1 no and tomato 1 no to make fine onion tomato paste. Mix well
  5. Add turmeric powder, coriander powder,chilli powder and garam masala. Mix well.once it starts releasing oil. Add 2 tbsp of thick curd
  6. Lastly add palak puree. Palak is boiled and made it as puree in mixer. Cook it for about 5 -7 minutes. If the Gravy is too thick add little water as per required consistency
  7. Put all the paneer cubes in gravy. Let it cook for about another 5 minutes.
  8. Lastly add fresh cream and serve with Rotis/ chapathis /Naan
