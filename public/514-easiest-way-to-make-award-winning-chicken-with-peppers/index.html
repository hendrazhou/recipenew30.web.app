The ingredients needed to make Chicken with Peppers:
  1. Take 2 lbs. chicken drumstick (6 drumsticks)
  2. Prepare 1 lb. Mini sweet peppers/ or some other peppers
  3. Take 4 onions cut into quarters
  4. Get 5 cloves garlic / chopped
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp tomato paste
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp honey
  7. Take Red pepper flakes
  8. Get 2 ounces (1/3 cup) frying oil
  9. Get 1 cup parsley / chopped
  10. Get to taste Salt
