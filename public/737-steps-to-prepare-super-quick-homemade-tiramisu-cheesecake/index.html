The ingredients needed to make Tiramisu Cheesecake:
  1. Get A
  2. Prepare 15 piece egg yolks
  3. Make ready 5 piece egg whites
  4. Prepare 180 grams granulated sugar
  5. Make ready 1 tbsp SP
  6. Make ready B (sifted)
  7. Make ready 60 grams cake flour
  8. Make ready 50 grams cornstarch
  9. Get 50 grams tiramisu flour
  10. Get 1 tbsp milk powder
  11. Get C (mix)
  12. Take 100 grams butter
  13. Make ready 50 grams margarine
  14. Take 1 tsp baking rhum
  15. Take D (cream cheese)
  16. Prepare 600 grams butter cream
  17. Prepare 300 grams cream cheese
  18. Make ready 400 grams white cocoa, melted
  19. Make ready 50 grams tiramisu powder
  20. Take 50 grams whole milk
  21. Take E (spray mixture)
  22. Make ready 150 ml simple syrup
  23. Take 50 ml black forest rhum essence
  24. Take 1 tbsp coffee rhum
Steps to make Tiramisu Cheesecake:
  1. Mix A until smooth
  2. Pour B slowly, keep mixing until incorporated
  3. Pour C, mix with spatula slowly
  4. Divide into 3 round baking pans of 26cm diameter. Bake in 200 celsius for 15 minutes
  5. D: mix cream cheese until smooth, pour other ingredients, mix evenly
  6. Spread cream cheese over the cake, garnish with cocoa powder, strawberry or chocolate bar
