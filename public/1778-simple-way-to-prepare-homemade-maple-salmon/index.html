The ingredients needed to make Maple Salmon:
  1. Get 1/4 cup maple syrup
  2. Take 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  3. Prepare 1 clove garlic, minced
  4. Get 1/4 teaspoon garlic salt
  5. Take 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
  6. Prepare 1 pound salmon

Instructions to make Maple Salmon:
  1. In a small bowl, mix the maple syrup, soy sauce, garlic, garlic salt and pepper
  2. Place salmon in a shallow glass baking dish, and coat with the maple syrup mixture. Cover the dish, and marinate salmon in the refrigerator 30 minutes, turning once.
  3. Preheat oven to 400°
  4. Bake salmon uncovered 20 minutes or until easily flaked with a fork.
