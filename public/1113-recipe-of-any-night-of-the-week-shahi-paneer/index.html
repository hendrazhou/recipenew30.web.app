The ingredients needed to make Shahi paneer:
  1. Take 3 chopped onions
  2. Make ready 2 diced capsicum
  3. Get 1 cup tomato puree
  4. Take 1 teaspoon salt
  5. Get 2 bay leaves
  6. Get 1 black elachi
  7. Take 3 cinnamon sticks
  8. Prepare 2 chopped green chillies
  9. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder
  10. Take 1 teaspoon Garam masala
  11. Prepare 1 tbsp ginger paste
  12. Take 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  13. Take 2 teaspoons oil
  14. Get 200 gms diced paneer
  15. Get 1/4 cup amul cream
  16. Prepare as required Kasuri methi for garnish
  17. Get 1/2 cup water
  18. Make ready 1 teaspoon dhania powder
  19. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder

Instructions to make Shahi paneer:
  1. Take a pan and add oil to it. Let it get hot and then add cumin seeds in it. Cook it for 2 min and add green chilli and ginger paste to it. Cook it for another 2 mins.
  2. Now add onion to it and cook it for 5 min on medium flame. Now add capsicum in it. Cook it till capsicum softens a little.
  3. Now add red chilli powder, dhania powder, garam masala, bay leaves, cinnamon sticks, turmeric powder in it and cook it for 2 mins.
  4. Now add tomato puree in it and cook it till it lefts oil. Now add salt to it. Cook it for another min and add water in it. Cook it for 5 min and add cream to it. Mix it well and let it cook on low flame for 10 mins.
  5. Add paneer in it and top up with kasuri methi.
  6. Our yummy tasty shahi paneer is ready.
