The ingredients needed to make Sausage and peppers:
  1. Prepare Olive oil
  2. Take 1 stick butter
  3. Prepare 2 large white onions
  4. Make ready 6 large garlic cloves
  5. Make ready 5 pounds Italian sausage
  6. Get 2 large green peppers and 1. red pepper
  7. Get 1 large can plum tomatoes
  8. Prepare to taste Fresh basil
  9. Get to taste Dried oregano
  10. Get a few Bay leaves
  11. Take to taste White pepper and salt
  12. Make ready Chicken broth (enough to cover)

Steps to make Sausage and peppers:
  1. Brown onions and garlic with olive oil and butter in a large soup pot.
  2. Slice your sausage on an angle about 1/2 inch thick. Let saute.
  3. Add peppers, tomatoes in can (squish them up first), basil, oregano and bay leaves. Add enough chick broth to cover.
  4. Let cook with lid on for about an hour. Taste, add white pepper and salt.
  5. Take lid half off and let cook till all taste comes together. Add more fresh basil to finish.
