The ingredients needed to make Tomato-Bell Pepper Rice:
  1. Get 1.5 C long grain rice
  2. Make ready 3 C water
  3. Make ready 3 T butter
  4. Make ready 2 cloves garlic; minced
  5. Get 1 green bell pepper; julienne & cut on bias
  6. Take 1 tomato bouillon cube
  7. Make ready 1/2 small red onion; minced
  8. Prepare 1 t ancho chile powder
  9. Take 1/2 t paprika
  10. Get 1 large pinch kosher salt & black pepper

Steps to make Tomato-Bell Pepper Rice:
  1. Bring water to a simmer.
  2. Heat 3 T butter in a tall sauce pot. Add bouillon cube and crush with wooden spoon. Stir until dissolved.
  3. Add rice. Season with salt, pepper, ancho chile, and paprika. Stir to coat each grain of rice with butter. Cook on medium-high heat for 30 seconds.
  4. Add bell pepper and onion. Stir. Cook 30 seconds.
  5. Add water. Stir. Bring back to a simmer. Cover. Simmer for 15 minutes. Remove from heat. Do not remove cover. Let steam 5 minutes or until ready 2 serve.
  6. Add garlic. Stir. Cook until fragrant, about 20-30 seconds.
