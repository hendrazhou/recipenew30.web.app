Instructions to make Delicious Lemon Pepper Pork Chops:
  1. Preheat oven to 375.
  2. In baking dish, evenly spread1 tspn of oil then add 1 tblsn of butter. Season chops with lemon pepper & cayenne, place in dish.
  3. Top chops with 1 tspn of oil and 1 tblspn butter. Top with worchester, add sliced onions.
  4. Cover well with foil. Make sure the foil is not loose. Bake for 45-50 min.
  5. Serve with rice, baked potato, or whatever. Goes well with any side favorite. Enjoy!

  7. When the chops are done, I make gravy for those folks that love sauce. I heat a pan on med heat for about 5 min. Add 2 tbspn of oil and 2 tablespoons of flour.
