The ingredients needed to make Fancy french toast:
  1. Prepare 8 piece Texas toast
  2. Prepare 3 eggs
  3. Take 1/4 cup eggnog/cream or milk
  4. Make ready 1/8 tsp salt
  5. Prepare 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp sugar
  7. Get 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  8. Prepare 1 cooking spray
  9. Get 1 tbsp butter
  10. Get 1 powdered sugar and syrup (optional)
