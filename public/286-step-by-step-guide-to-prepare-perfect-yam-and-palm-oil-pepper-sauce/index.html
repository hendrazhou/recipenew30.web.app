Steps to make Yam and palm oil pepper sauce:
  1. Slice the yam and boil till soft, then set aside
  2. Blend the shobo, garlic and ginger together
  3. Preheat the palm oil, add the blended pepper paste into the hot oil, fry for 3mins, slice your spring onions and add, add in your Maggi and salt, fry for another 1min. Your sauce is ready
