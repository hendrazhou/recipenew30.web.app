The ingredients needed to make Hickory Smoked Lemon Pepper Chicken Salad:
  1. Get 4 Tablespoons FRESH Chopped Parsley
  2. Take 2 Tablespoons FRESH Chopped Chives
  3. Take 2 Cups Hickory Smoked Lemon Pepper Chicken
  4. Get 2 Hard Boiled Eggs (chopped)
  5. Prepare 3-4 Tablespoons Mayonnaise
  6. Make ready 1 Tablespoon Dijon Mustard
  7. Get 3 Tablespoons Sweet Pickle Relish
  8. Prepare 1 \2 Teaspoon Lemon Pepper Seasoning
  9. Make ready 1 \2 Teaspoon Celery Salt
  10. Make ready 1 \2 Teaspoon Smoked Paprika
