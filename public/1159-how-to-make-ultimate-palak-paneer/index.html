The ingredients needed to make Palak Paneer:
  1. Prepare 250 gms spinach
  2. Take 200 gms paneer
  3. Get 1 bay leaf (tej patta)
  4. Take 1 tbsp garlic finely chopped
  5. Take 1 tbsp ginger finely chopped
  6. Make ready 1 medium onion finely chopped
  7. Make ready 1-2 green chillies finely chopped
  8. Take 2 tomatoes pureed
  9. Make ready 1 tsp red chillies powder
  10. Get 1 tsp cumin powder
  11. Get 1 tsp coriander powder
  12. Prepare 1 tsp garam masala powder
  13. Get 2 tsp crushed kasoori methi
  14. Get 2 tbsp oil
  15. Prepare to taste Salt

Steps to make Palak Paneer:
  1. Clean and wash the spinach. Boil water in a pan. Blanch the spinach until it changes color. Remove it to a bowl with ice cold water so that it retains its color.
  2. Squeeze out the water from the spinach. Puree it into a smooth paste and keep aside.
  3. Cut the paneer into bite sized pieces. It can be any shape of your choice but cubes work best. You can also fry the paneer but I prefer the soft texture of the unfried one.
  4. Heat oil in a pan. Add the bay leaf.
  5. Add in the garlic. When it changes color, add the ginger and cook for a minute.
  6. Add the chopped onions and the green chillies. Saute until the onions are translucent.
  7. Add the tomato puree and saute for a minute or two.
  8. Now add the dry masalas - salt, red chillies powder, cumin powder and coriander powder. Mix well and saute until you can see the oil appearing on the sides.
  9. Add in the spinach puree. Mix well. You can add a bit of water if required.
  10. When it begins to bubble, add in the paneer pieces. Mix until they are well coated. Do not cover the pan or the spinach will lose its color.
  11. To finish, add the garam masala and kasoori methi. Mix well.
  12. Palak paneer is ready to serve.
