The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Waffles:
  1. Take 1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  2. Make ready 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  3. Take 1 tablespoon baking powder
  4. Make ready 1 tablespoon cocoa powder
  5. Get 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  6. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon salt
  7. Make ready 1 cup Blue Diamond Almond Breeze Chocolate almond milk
  8. Take 1 large egg, lightly beaten
  9. Take 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  10. Get Cooking spray
Steps to make Chocolate Waffles:
  1. Combine the first 6 ingredients in a large bowl with a whisk.
  2. In a separate bowl, combine the almond milk, oil and egg. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients; stir together until combined.
  3. Preheat waffle iron.
  4. Lightly spray heated waffle iron with cooking spray. Add 1/3 cup batter to each segment of iron, spreading batter to edges. Cook 4 minutes or until steaming stops and waffle is crisp and lightly browned.
