The ingredients needed to make Shahi paneer:
  1. Get 200 gm Paneer
  2. Take 100 gm Mawa
  3. Take 1/2 tsp Chilli powder
  4. Prepare 1/4tsp Haldi
  5. Get 1/2 tsp Dhaniya powder
  6. Get 1/4 tsp Jeera
  7. Take as per taste Salt
  8. Prepare 1/4tsp Dalchini powder
  9. Prepare 1 cup Tomato puyri
  10. Get 1 Onion paste
  11. Get as needed Oil
  12. Get 2 tbsp Fresh cream
