The ingredients needed to make Salt and Pepper Baked Chicken Wings:
  1. Prepare 1 kg chicken wings
  2. Get 2 Tbsp olive oil
  3. Prepare 1,5 tsp salt
  4. Make ready 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  5. Take 2 tsp minced parsley (or you can also use dried parsley)

Instructions to make Salt and Pepper Baked Chicken Wings:
  1. In a big bowl, add chicken wings. Season with olive oil, salt, and black pepper. Mix well.
  2. Place chicken wings on a baking pan lined with parchment paper.
  3. Bake in a preheated oven 225C/450F for 20-25 minutes until golden brown.
  4. Sprinkle with minced parsley.
