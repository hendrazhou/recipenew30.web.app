The ingredients needed to make Methi Paneer Masala:
  1. Get 150 gm paneer
  2. Make ready 1.5 cup fresh methi leaves
  3. Make ready 2 medium onion finely chopped
  4. Make ready 1/2 square cut small green pepper
  5. Make ready 1 small tomato finely chopped
  6. Get 1 tbsp minced garlic
  7. Prepare 1/2 inch ginger minced
  8. Make ready 3/4 cup stirred yogurt
  9. Take 8 cashew nut fine paste
  10. Get 1 tbsp Kashmiri red chilli powder
  11. Make ready 1/2 tbsp MDH deggi mirch powder
  12. Take To taste Salt
  13. Get 1 tbsp chopped green chilli
  14. Make ready 1 tbsp oil
  15. Get 2 tbsp salted butter
  16. Get 1 tsp cumin seeds
  17. Make ready 1 bay leaf
  18. Take 1 black cardamom
  19. Make ready 1 tsp roasted chickpea flour (besan)

Steps to make Methi Paneer Masala:
  1. Heat oil and butter in a pan. Once it heat up add cumin seeds let it splutter. Add bay leaf, black cardamom and asafoetida.
  2. Add onion and sauté until golden. Now add garlic ginger and green chilli. Cook for 2-3 mins then add both chilli powders. Add dash of water so chilli powder doesn’t get burnt.
  3. It’s time to add chopped tomato and cook until oil oozes. Add chopped fresh methi leaves and cook until and green pepper. Cook for 5-6 minutes or until methi is cooked.
  4. Add roasted chickpea flour and stir. Add yogurt, cashew nut puree and salt. Cook until oil oozes. Add paneer and close the lid to cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  5. Garnish with cilantro and serve hot with paratha.
