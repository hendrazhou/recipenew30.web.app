The ingredients needed to make chicken won tons:
  1. Get 1 packages cream cheese 8 oz
  2. Take 3 tbsp milk
  3. Take 1/2 tsp salt
  4. Get 1/4 tsp pepper
  5. Prepare 2 cup cooked chicken
  6. Make ready 2 tbsp chopped green onion
  7. Prepare 1 packages won ton wrappers(square ones)
  8. Get 1 butter cooking spray
