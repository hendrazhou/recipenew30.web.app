The ingredients needed to make Vegetable Paneer Tikka Grill:
  1. Get 2 lbs Cottage Cheese(paneer) Cubes
  2. Make ready 250 grams Onion
  3. Get 250 grams Parboiled Potato
  4. Make ready 250 grams Green Bell pepper
  5. Take 150 grams Red bell pepper
  6. Take 2 tbs Red chilli Powder
  7. Get 2 tbs Coriander Cumin Powder
