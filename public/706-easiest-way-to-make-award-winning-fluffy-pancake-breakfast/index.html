The ingredients needed to make Fluffy Pancake Breakfast:
  1. Make ready 11/2 cup flour
  2. Get 31/2 tsp baking powder
  3. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  4. Make ready 1/4 cup sugar
  5. Take 11/4 cup milk
  6. Prepare 2 egg
  7. Prepare 3 tbsp melted butter
  8. Take cooking spray or veg oil to grease the pan
Steps to make Fluffy Pancake Breakfast:
  1. Melt butter in microwave for less than a minute and set aside
  2. In a medium bowl, mix together flour, sugar, salt and baking powder and in another bowl stir milk and egg together and set them aside
  3. Create a well in the center of the flour mixture and pour the butter and milk mixture into the well and use a whisk to stir everything together until fully mixed
  4. Allow batter to rest while heating a lightly greased frying pan on a medium heat with either cooking spray or veg oil
  5. You can use a cooking spoon to pour in batter into the frying pan and cook each side for about 2 to 4 minutes until it turns golden brown
  6. Serve with mushroom scrambled eggs and baked beans
