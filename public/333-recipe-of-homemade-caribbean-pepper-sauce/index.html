The ingredients needed to make Caribbean Pepper Sauce:
  1. Get 20 scotch bonnet peppers
  2. Get 8 habanero peppers
  3. Take 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  4. Make ready 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  5. Prepare 1 large white onion
  6. Get 2 small carrots
  7. Prepare 2 tsp cumin
  8. Get 2 tsp salt
  9. Take 2 tsp black ground pepper
  10. Take 8 cloves garlic
  11. Take 3 limes juiced and zested
  12. Make ready 1 mango
  13. Get 2 cups pineapple w/ juice
  14. Prepare 1/4 cup brown sugar
  15. Take Optional
  16. Make ready 10 thai chili peppers
  17. Take 3 tbsp olive oil

Steps to make Caribbean Pepper Sauce:
  1. Chop and combine ALL ingredients.
  2. Place all ingredients food processor. Puree until smooth. However I like a little texture from the veggies 😋.
  3. Put in steel pot simmer for 1 hour, cover on low. Try not to smell it directly..you might sneeze.
  4. Rinse dishes in cold 💧 water.
  5. Find either glass or disposable thick container with firm sealable lid. You will need to two for this recipe. One container if you split the recipe in half.
  6. Let mixture cool and place on jar.
  7. Delicious with rice dishes, grilled veggies, orzo and roasted chicken. ENJOY Friends!
