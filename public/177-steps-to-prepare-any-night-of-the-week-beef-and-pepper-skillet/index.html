The ingredients needed to make Beef and Pepper Skillet:
  1. Get 1 lb ground beef
  2. Prepare 1 can rotel
  3. Prepare 1 can beef broth
  4. Make ready 1 tbsp chili powder
  5. Prepare 1/4 tsp salt
  6. Make ready 1/8 tsp garlic powder
  7. Get 2 cup instant brown rice
  8. Make ready 1 green pepper sliced
  9. Make ready 1 red pepper sliced
Instructions to make Beef and Pepper Skillet:
  1. In large skillet brown ground beef, drain
  2. Add rotel, broth, chili powder, and salt. Bring to boil.
  3. Stir in rice and peppers. Simmer covered 8-10 minutes until liquid is absorbed.
  4. Remove from heat and sprinkle with cheese if desired.
