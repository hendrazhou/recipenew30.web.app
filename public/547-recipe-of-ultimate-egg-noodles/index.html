The ingredients needed to make Egg Noodles:
  1. Get 6 eggs
  2. Get 75 ml milk
  3. Make ready 1 cooking spray

Egg noodles are easy to make, and will bring a welcome freshness to any pasta dish or soup. Usable for both Asian-inspired cuisines and Italian, these noodles are lightly customizable and quick. Steps to make Egg Noodles:
  1. whisk eggs and milk together
  2. heat large fry pan or wok and spray with cooking spray
  3. use a third of the mixture at a time. pour into pan and tilt the pan to spread the mix out thin
  4. once mix is cooked on one side (should only take a minute or so) flip and cook the other side. try to keep in one piece but dont worry if it breaks your going to cut it into noodles soon
  5. once both sides are cooked try rolling it up and place on chopping board and cut about a centimeter wide
