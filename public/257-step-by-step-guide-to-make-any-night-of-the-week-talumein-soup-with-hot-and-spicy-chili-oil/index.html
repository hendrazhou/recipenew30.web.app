The ingredients needed to make Talumein Soup With Hot and Spicy Chili Oil:
  1. Make ready For the Soup:
  2. Get 1 handful Instant Noodles
  3. Make ready 4 Boneless and Skinless Chicken Thighs (Chopped Finely)
  4. Make ready 4 Cups Chicken/Vegetable Broth
  5. Prepare 4 Cups Water
  6. Take 1 Medium Green bell pepper(Chopped Finely)
  7. Take 1 Bunch Green Onions
  8. Make ready 1/2 Inch Ginger (Minced)
  9. Take 4-6 Garlic Cloves (Minced)
  10. Get 4 Tbsp Cornstarch
  11. Get 1 Tbsp Dark Soya Sauce
  12. Prepare 1 Tbsp Vinegar
  13. Prepare To taste Salt
  14. Make ready For the Chili Oil:
  15. Make ready 1 Cup Cooking oil (Any except Sesame oil, Mustard Oil & Olive Oil) I used Canola Oil
  16. Get 2 Tsp Red Chili flakes (Heaped)
  17. Get 4 Dried whole red chilies (Broken into halves)
Instructions to make Talumein Soup With Hot and Spicy Chili Oil:
  1. (A) To make the Soup:
  2. Take a small measuring cup or bowl. Mix the Corn Starch with 1/4 cup of water to make a slurry. Set aside. - - Pour the Chicken Broth & Water into a large pot with lid.
  3. When it comes to a rolling boil, add the Chicken, Ginger, Garlic, Soya Sauce, Vinegar & Salt. - - Reduce heat to low & let it simmer covered for about 15 minutes.
  4. Slightly crush the Noodles to make it easier to eat. Add to the pot. Followed by the Mushrooms, Bell Peppers & Green Onions. - - Cook for 2-3 minutes over medium heat. - We want the veggies to be crunchy & the noodles al dente.
  5. Reduce heat to low. Add the slurry. - Keep stirring constantly to avoid lumps.
  6. Serve hot with Chili Oil &/Or Chili Sauce. - - Garnish with Green Onions if you wish to. - - Note: You may totally omit the water & use 8 cups/2 Litres of broth.
  7. (B) To make the Chili Oil:
  8. Heat the cooking oil in a small frying pan or wok over medium heat.
  9. Add the chili flakes & broken chilies. Stir. - Take off the heat as soon as the chilies begin to sizzle.
  10. Do not let the chilies burn, please. - Let the oil cool down completely. - Store in a clean & dry glass jar. - Use as required.
  11. This is quite a versatile condiment. Can be used in Soups/Porridges, Stir fried Meats/Veggies, Pasta, etc.
