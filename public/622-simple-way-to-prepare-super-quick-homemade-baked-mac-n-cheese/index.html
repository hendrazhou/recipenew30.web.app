The ingredients needed to make Baked Mac n cheese:
  1. Take 2 pks of velveeta mac n cheese
  2. Make ready 2 eggs
  3. Get 2 cup of milk
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp melted butter
  5. Take 2 cup sharp cheddar
  6. Take 1 bread crumbs
  7. Make ready 1 oil spray

Steps to make Baked Mac n cheese:
  1. Mix the eggs, butter and milk in a bowl. Once all well blended add the sharp cheddar to it and continue to mix. Leave on side.
  2. Cook the pasta shells half way then drain. They would cook more in the oven. Don't want it to be dry.
  3. Once drained add the 2pks of velveeta cheese to the shells and mix.
  4. Spray down the pan with oil spray.
  5. Put the mix Mac n cheese in the pan and lay it out evenly.
  6. Grab the bowl mix and pour over the pan evenly. Move pan from left to right to make sure the liquid is spread all around
  7. Sprinkle bread crumbs on top
  8. Put in oven at 350° for 35min.
