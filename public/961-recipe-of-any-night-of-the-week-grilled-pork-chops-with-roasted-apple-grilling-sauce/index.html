The ingredients needed to make Grilled Pork Chops With Roasted Apple Grilling Sauce:
  1. Prepare 1 packages or 4 Butcher wrapped pork chops
  2. Take 4 small golden delicious apples cored and halved
  3. Take 1 Bottle roasted Apple grilling sauce -Aldis brand
  4. Get 1 If you don't have use 1 cup Apple juice, 1cup puréed apples, table spoon molasses, teaspoon brown sugar, table spoon Apple cider vinegar juice of one lemon and teaspoon alspice
  5. Get 1/2 tsp Webber steak and chop seasoning
Instructions to make Grilled Pork Chops With Roasted Apple Grilling Sauce:
  1. Wash pork chops and season with steak and chop seasoning fire up grill (heat grilling sauce in small sauce pan) when coals are white grill chops turning every 3 min until done may baste near end of cook time with apple grilling sauce
  2. Slice the apples in half spray cut half with butter cooking spray and place on grill after chops are removed
  3. Grill on both sides for seven minutes then remove apples place around dish with grill marks showing pour grilling sauce over apples and chops serve hot
