The ingredients needed to make Chicken stuffed bell peppers:
  1. Make ready 4 medium red pepper, cut in half, seeds removed
  2. Take 2 tsp Olive oil
  3. Prepare 1 medium onion, chopped
  4. Make ready 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  5. Prepare 4 chicken breasts, cut into small cubes
  6. Make ready 1 tsp chilli powder
  7. Make ready 1 tsp cumin
  8. Take 1/4 tsp Himalayan salt
  9. Get 1/4 tsp ground pepper
  10. Get 1 cup no sugar added tomato sauce
  11. Make ready 2 cups cooked brown rice
  12. Take 1 cup chickpeas, canned, drained
  13. Make ready 1 cup frozen corn kernels
  14. Make ready 5 tbsp fresh cilantro, or lemon thyme (for garnish)
  15. Make ready 1 tbsp lime juice
  16. Make ready 1 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese

Instructions to make Chicken stuffed bell peppers:
  1. Preheat oven 375°F. Place bell pepper in a glass dish, spray with olive oil and cook for 25 minutes
  2. Cook brown rice according to package
  3. In a large skillet, add olive oil, onions, garlic and cook for 5 minutes
  4. Add chicken, cumin, chilli powder, salt, pepper. Cook, stir frequently, for 5 minutes or until chicken is no longer pink.
  5. Add tomato sauce, beans, corn, lime juice, fresh herbs and rice. Reduce heat to med-low and cook another 3-5 minutes.
  6. Scoop chicken mixture into bell peppers, evenly. Add cheese, cover with foil and bake another 15-20 minutes. Remove foil, bake another 3 minutes. Or broil.
  7. Serve and sprinkle with fresh herbs !
