The ingredients needed to make Easy grilled salmon:
  1. Get 4 salmon fillets
  2. Get 1/4 cup olive oil
  3. Take 1 teaspoon dry basil
  4. Prepare 1 teaspoon dry thyme
  5. Make ready 1 teaspoons parsley
  6. Make ready 1/4 cup brown suger
  7. Prepare 1/4 cup soy sauce
  8. Prepare 1/2 teaspoons garlic salt
