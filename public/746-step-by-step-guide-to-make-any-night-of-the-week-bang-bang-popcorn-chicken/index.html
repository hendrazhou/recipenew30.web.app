The ingredients needed to make Bang Bang Popcorn Chicken:
  1. Get 1 lb Chicken breast or tenders cut into bite size pieces
  2. Take 1 cup Italian bread crumbs
  3. Take 1 cup Panko
  4. Make ready 1 Salt and garlic powder to taste
  5. Take 1 each eggs
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp Milk or water
  7. Prepare 5 tbsp mayonnaise
  8. Get 3 tbsp Sweet thai chili sauce
  9. Prepare 2 tbsp siracha
  10. Take 2 each slices scallions
  11. Prepare 1 olive oil spray
Instructions to make Bang Bang Popcorn Chicken:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line bakind sheet with foil and spray with olive oil. In one shallow bowl mix eggs and milk. In another shallow bowl mix bread crumbs, panko, salt and garlic powder.
  2. Dredge chicken pieces through egg mixture then through the bread crumb mixture and place in baking sheet. Repeat until all chicken is coated. Spray chicken with olive oil.
  3. Place chicken in oven and bake for 15 to 20 minutes or until all pieces reach internal temperature of 165°F.
  4. While chicken is baking, in a glass bowl add the remaining ingredients and stir until well mixed. Remove chicken from oven. Drizzle or dip chicken in sauce. Serve as a salad topper or eat alone, either way they are delicious.
