The ingredients needed to make Palak paneer:
  1. Get 100 gm paneer pieces
  2. Get 2 cup boil palak
  3. Make ready 2 onins chopped
  4. Prepare 3 tomato puree
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  6. Prepare 1 tsp jeera
  7. Prepare 1 tsp red chilli powder
  8. Take 1 tsp coriander powder
  9. Make ready 1/2 TSP turmeric
  10. Prepare 1 tsp garam masala
  11. Get to taste Salt
  12. Prepare 2 tbsp oil
  13. Take 2 tbsp cream
