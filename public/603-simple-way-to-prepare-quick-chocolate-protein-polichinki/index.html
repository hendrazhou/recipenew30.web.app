The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Protein Polichinki:
  1. Get 2 cup 100% Whole Wheat Flour
  2. Take 4 grams Stevia In The Raw
  3. Get 2 large Eggs
  4. Make ready 1 dash Salt
  5. Make ready 2 cup Skim Milk
  6. Take 1 Scoop - Chocolate Whey Protein (30g per scoop)
  7. Get 1 Spray - Olive Oil Cooking Spray
Instructions to make Chocolate Protein Polichinki:
  1. You will need a mixing bowl (preferably one with a lid if there will be leftovers), a frying pan, a spatula, and a spoon.
  2. Measure out each ingredient (except the cooking spray) into the mixing bowl, and using a mixer, combine them until fully mixed and smooth. The batter may seem a little liquidy.
  3. Spray the frying pan with the cooking spray, covering enough so that the polichinki will not stick and turn it on to low heat.
  4. Pour the batter (roughly 1/2 cup) into the lightly heated pan.
  5. Don't flip the cake until the side visible to you is almost cooked through.
  6. Flip the cake over and continue to cook for a minute or two until cooked through.
  7. Remove from pan and add your choice of toppings (fruit, cream cheese, syrup, etc).
  8. Refrigerate leftover.
