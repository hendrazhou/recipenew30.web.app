Instructions to make Cheesy cornbread in a cast iron pan:
  1. Preheat oven 450° Fahrenheit heat cast iron pan till hot spray with nonstick sprinkle ground black pepper on bottom of pan
  2. Mix all the rest of ingredients together but the butter, cubed cheese, and kosher salt save for later
  3. After mixing well pour into pan add cubed cheese on top bake in oven 20-25 minutes or till done check center with toothpicks stick in if it comes up clean then its done
  4. When done brush on butter on the top and sprinkle salt on top
  5. Hope you enjoy
