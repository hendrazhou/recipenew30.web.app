The ingredients needed to make Palak paneer:
  1. Make ready 1 bunch Palak or spinach (stems are removed)
  2. Prepare 1 medium onion
  3. Make ready 1 small ginger piece
  4. Make ready 4-5 small garlic cloves
  5. Prepare 200 g paneer cut in cubes
  6. Prepare 1 small cinnamon stick
  7. Prepare 1-2 green cardamoms
  8. Make ready 1 clove
  9. Prepare 1/2 tsp jeera/cumin
  10. Make ready 1 whole dry red chilli
  11. Get 1 tsp fresh cream for garnishing
  12. Make ready 1/2 turmeric powder (optional)
  13. Make ready 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  14. Prepare 1 tsp coriander powder
  15. Get 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  16. Prepare 1 pinch garam masala
  17. Take 1/2 tsp sugar
  18. Make ready 1 tsp tomato ketchup

  3. Heat a pan or wok, add 1 tsp oil. Add jeera, red chilli, cinnamon stick, cardamom and clove. Saute for few seconds. Add finely chopped onion, saute until lightly golden.
