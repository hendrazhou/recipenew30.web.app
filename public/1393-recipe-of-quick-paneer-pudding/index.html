The ingredients needed to make Paneer Pudding:
  1. Make ready 200 gms. Paneer
  2. Make ready 1/3 cup powdered sugar
  3. Make ready 1 & 1/2 cups evaporated / thickened milk
  4. Get 1 full egg
  5. Make ready 3 egg yolks
  6. Prepare pinch cardamom powder
  7. Get 1 tsp. rose / kewra powder
  8. Take pinch salt
  9. Get 1 tsp. evaporated milk to drizzle (opt)
  10. Get tutty fruity / chopped nuts to garnish

Steps to make Paneer Pudding:
  1. Blend the paneer, milk, rose water and cardamom powder. Beat the eggs and sugar separately and combine with the paneer mix.
  2. Pass it through a strainer and pour into a greased pudding bowl. Place in a pan filled with water and cover with a tight lid. Steam for 25-30 minutes on a low flame.
  3. Let it come to room temperature before refrigerating it for 3-4 hours. Pass a knife around the edges and demould it on a serving plate.
  4. Drizzle some evaporated milk and garnish as desired. Serve as an after meal dessert or enjoy whenever desired.
