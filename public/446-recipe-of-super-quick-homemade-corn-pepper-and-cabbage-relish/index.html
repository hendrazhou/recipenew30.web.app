The ingredients needed to make Corn Pepper and Cabbage Relish:
  1. Prepare 2-1/2 cup whole kernel corn
  2. Prepare 1 large orange bell pepper
  3. Make ready 1 large onion diced
  4. Get 1/3 teaspoon sesame oil
  5. Take 2-1/2 cup thinly sliced green cabbage
  6. Prepare 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  7. Take 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar unfiltered with the mother
  8. Take 1/4 teaspoon salt
  9. Get 1/2 teaspoon seasoned salt
  10. Get 1 teaspoon granulated garlic powder
  11. Make ready 1/4 cup chopped parsley
  12. Take 1/4 cup capers chopped
  13. Prepare 1/4 cup chopped pimento peppers
  14. Prepare 1/2 tablespoons seasoned salt
  15. Prepare To taste honey optional
Steps to make Corn Pepper and Cabbage Relish:
  1. Dice the peppers and onions put with the corn.
  2. Add to a very hot skillet and stir to mix add the salt, seasoned salt, garlic, both vinegars and simmer till the onion is tender. Slice the cabbage.
  3. Add the 1/2 tablespoons to the cabbage and rub vigorously
  4. Add seasoned cabbage to the corn mixture.
  5. Stir it together simmer 15 minutes
  6. Chop the capers and pimentos.
  7. Stir them in and simmer 15 minutes.
  8. You can serve in a bowl as a side or use as a condiment. I hope you enjoy!!
