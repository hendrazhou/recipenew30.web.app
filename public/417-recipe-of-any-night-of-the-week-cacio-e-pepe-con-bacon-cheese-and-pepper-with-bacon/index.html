The ingredients needed to make Cacio e Pepe Con Bacon, Cheese and Pepper with Bacon:
  1. Get Bacon——————-
  2. Take 1/4 pound Bacon
  3. Get 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  4. Prepare 1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
  5. Prepare 4 cloves garlic
  6. Make ready 3 tablespoon butter
  7. Prepare 3 tablespoon extra Virgin olive oil
  8. Get 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  9. Make ready Cacio e Pepe/ Cheese and Pepper —
  10. Prepare 1 pound cavatappi / corkscrew pasta
  11. Take 2 quarts water
  12. Get 2 tablespoons fresh ground black pepper
  13. Prepare 2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  14. Make ready 1 tablespoon salt for boiling pasta
  15. Get 2 tablespoons chopped parsley

Steps to make Cacio e Pepe Con Bacon, Cheese and Pepper with Bacon:
  1. Cut the bacon into pieces.
  2. Get the water boiling. Add the pasta and heat the bacon and butter in a pan.
  3. Slice the garlic and add to the bacon drippings and butter mixture. Add the oregano and thyme.
  4. When the the garlic is browned a bit add salt and add the bacon back to the pan.
  5. When the pasta is just done, add the pasta straight from the pot using a dipper.
  6. Add the cheese and butter. Stir in a couple of ladles full of pasta water. Allow it to get creamy don't let the cheese clump up stir a lot.
  7. Add more pepper and cheese if you like. Serve immediately. I hope you enjoy!!
