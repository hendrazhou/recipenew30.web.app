The ingredients needed to make Fry Bread batch 9:
  1. Make ready 1 cup all purpose flour
  2. Take 1 cup self-rising buttermilk cornbread mix
  3. Take 1 cup lactose free milk 1%
  4. Get 2 large eggs
  5. Get 2 tablespoons sugar
  6. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  7. Take As needed nonstick spray
Instructions to make Fry Bread batch 9:
  1. Mix all the ingredients together it will be stiff.
  2. Spray the skillet with nonstick spray. Heat and add a desert spoonful sized dollop.
  3. Turn as needed cook till done about 3-4 minutes each.
  4. Serve with what you are eating or by itself. I hope you enjoy!!! I served mine with multityped of pasta corkscrew and rigatoni, smoked sausage, and Spaghetti sauced, goulash. With grated parmesan cheese.
