The ingredients needed to make Paneer Lababdar:
  1. Prepare 4 tsp butter
  2. Prepare 2 chopped onions
  3. Get 4-5 chopped tomatoes
  4. Take 2 Green chillies, chopped
  5. Make ready 2 bay leaves
  6. Get 10-12 Cashew nuts
  7. Prepare 2 tsp melon seeds
  8. Get 1 tsp salt
  9. Take 1 tsp degi mirch
  10. Prepare 1 tsp kasuri methi
  11. Prepare 1.5 tsp sugar
  12. Get 1 tbsp crumbled paneer
  13. Take 250-300 gms pieces of paneer
  14. Get as required Chopped coriander leaves
  15. Prepare 1 tablespoon malai or cream
Steps to make Paneer Lababdar:
  1. Heat a pan, add butter, chopped onion, tomatoes, bay leaves, green chilli, cashew, melon seeds add salt and cook.
  2. Cover and cook for 10 min.Then cool and grind it into smooth paste.
  3. Then heat a pan, add butter, degi mirch,add puree in it. Add kasuri methi, sugar and cook. Add some water and crumbled panner in it and cook it. Now add pieces of paneer in it.add 1 tsp malai in it.
  4. Cook for 5 mins, garnish with coriander and cream or malai. serve with Naan or parantha.
