The ingredients needed to make Paneer Bhurji:
  1. Take 200 gm Paneer / Indian cottage cheese
  2. Prepare 1 onion chopped
  3. Make ready 1 tomato chopped
  4. Get 1/2 capsicum chopped
  5. Take 1/2 spoon ginger garlic paste
  6. Prepare 1 spoon termiric powder
  7. Take 1/3 spoon red chilli powder
  8. Get 1/3 garam masala powder
  9. Get as per taste Salt
  10. Take 2 Little chopped green chili
  11. Take as needed Cooking oil
  12. Make ready as required Fresh Coriander leaves

Instructions to make Paneer Bhurji:
  1. Crumble paneer with your hands
  2. Heat up oil in pan add chopped onions fry till light brown add ginger garlic paste saute add green chilli add capsicum saute add chopped tomatoes mix all saute few minutes.
  3. Now add all dry spices mix well add crumble paneer mix well add garam masala powder mix all together cover for 2 min.
  4. Your easy and tasty paneer is ready. Serve with chapati naan bread or as side dish. Enjoy. sprinkle some fresh Coriander leaves.
