The ingredients needed to make Best Breaded Pork Chops You'll Ever Have:
  1. Get 6 Center cut boneless pork chops
  2. Get 1 Pepper
  3. Prepare 1 Salt
  4. Make ready 1 large egg
  5. Make ready 1/2 cup Panko breadcrumbs
  6. Make ready 1/3 cup Italian breadcrumbs
  7. Prepare 2 tbsp Parmesan cheese
  8. Prepare 1 1/4 tsp Sweet paperika
  9. Prepare 1/2 tsp Garlic powder
  10. Prepare 1/2 tsp Onion powder
  11. Take 1/4 tsp Chili powder
Steps to make Best Breaded Pork Chops You'll Ever Have:
  1. Preheat oven to 425°F
  2. Grease a large enough baking dish
  3. Mix all of the ingredients EXCEPT the egg in a bowl
  4. Break the egg in a bowl and wisk it up
  5. Salt and pepper both sides of the chops
  6. Dip the chop into the egg then into the breadcrumb mixture
  7. Place each breaded chop in the dish
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes
  9. Plate and enjoy!
