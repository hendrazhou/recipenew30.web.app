The ingredients needed to make Mayo muffins:
  1. Prepare 2 cups self raising flour
  2. Prepare 1 cup milk
  3. Prepare 6 Tbsp mayonnaise
  4. Get Cooking spray

Steps to make Mayo muffins:
  1. Pre heat oven to 180°c
  2. In a bowl, mix all the ingredients together.
  3. Spray muffin tin with cooking spray. Can also use butter or margarine.
  4. Spoon out the batter into muffin pan.
  5. Bake for about 25minutes, until golden brown.
