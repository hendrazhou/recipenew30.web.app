The ingredients needed to make Sticky salmon:
  1. Take 4 salmon fillets
  2. Get 1 red pepper
  3. Take 1 green pepper
  4. Get Spoonful siracha sauce
  5. Get Spoonful soy sauce
  6. Make ready Spoonful fish sauce
  7. Make ready Sesame seeds
  8. Prepare Oil for pan frying
  9. Prepare Spring onions for garnish

Steps to make Sticky salmon:
  1. Prepare the veg. Make the sauce by mixing the ingredients together. Marinate the fish but save a tiny bit for the veg
  2. Heat oil in two pans. Throw in the peppers and sauce. Cook for 5 mins, leaving a bit of crunch to the veg
  3. At the same time heat the pan for the fish nice and hot. Cook fish skin side down. Leave it to crisp up.
  4. When nice n crispy, flip over and cook on the other side for a few mins. Then flip back and add some sesame seeds. Serve on top of rice, garnished with spring onions
