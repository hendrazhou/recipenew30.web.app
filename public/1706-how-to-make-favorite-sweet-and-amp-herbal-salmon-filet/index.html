The ingredients needed to make Sweet & Herbal Salmon Filet:
  1. Take 2 salmon filets
  2. Make ready 1.5 teaspoons brown sugar
  3. Make ready 0.5 tablespoons thyme
  4. Prepare 0.5 Tablespoons oregano
  5. Prepare 1.5 teaspoons paprika
  6. Prepare 2 gloves of smashed garlic
  7. Make ready 2-3 tablespoons olive oil
