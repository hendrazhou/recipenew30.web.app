The ingredients needed to make Potato Bites - Quick & Easy Snack Recipe - Potato Snacks:
  1. Take For Dough = Mashed Boiled Potatoes = 3-4 Medium
  2. Make ready 1 tea spoon Black Pepper
  3. Make ready 1 tea spoon salt
  4. Make ready 1 tea spoon Paprika Powder
  5. Get 1 tea spoon Chili Flakes

  7. Make ready 1 table spoon All purpose flour
  8. Get for coating
  9. Prepare 2 tbs All purpose flour
  10. Take 2 tbs Corn Flour
  11. Prepare water as required
  12. Make ready 1/2 tea spoon black pepper
  13. Make ready 1/2 tea spoon salt

Steps to make Potato Bites - Quick & Easy Snack Recipe - Potato Snacks:
  1. Mashed All Potatoes and mix all ingredients in it, and make dough
  2. Now make its shapes, you can make different types of shapes
  3. Now make Thick Batter. Take 2 tbs all purpose flour, 2 tbs corn flour and half tea spoon salt and half tea spoon black pepper, mix them well and add required water, make thick batter for coating.
  4. Now coat them, firstly adding in batter and then coat in bread crumbs
  5. Now fry them at medium flame, Now crispy bites are ready to eat.
