The ingredients needed to make Cranberry-Basil Pork Chops:
  1. Get 2 boneless pork chops
  2. Prepare 4 basil leaves; minced
  3. Take 2 T cranberry-honey mustard
  4. Make ready 3/4 t onion powder
  5. Get 3/4 t garlic powder
  6. Make ready as needed walnut oil
  7. Prepare as needed kosher salt & black pepper
Steps to make Cranberry-Basil Pork Chops:
  1. Toss chops with enough oil to coat.
  2. Season with salt, pepper, onion powder, and garlic powder.
  3. Bake at 400º for approximately 15 minutes or until pork reaches desired doneness. Spread cranberry-honey mustard atop chops during last 2 minutes of cooking process.
  4. Garnish with basil.
