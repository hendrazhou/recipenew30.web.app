The ingredients needed to make Evening snacks:
  1. Make ready 250 gm Paneer
  2. Take 4 Boiled potatoes
  3. Make ready 1 Chopped onion
  4. Get 1 tsp Chopped garlic
  5. Prepare 1 tsp Chopped green chillies
  6. Prepare 1 tsp Chopped ginger
  7. Make ready to taste Salt
  8. Take 1 tsp Jeera Powder
  9. Take 1 tbsp Green coriander leaves

Steps to make Evening snacks:
  1. Firstly take a bowl. add boiled mashed potatoes. add garlic, onion, ginger, green chillies, coriander leaves, salt,jeera Powder. mix it well. Keep aside.
  2. Now cut paneer in cubes. take small portion of potatoes mixture. make a round shape with help of palm. put Paneer cube on center. cover it all around with potatoes. make all cutlets like this.
  3. Now heat oil. fry all cutlets until golden colour..fry it for 10 minutes. ready to serve with sauce.😊😋
