The ingredients needed to make Homemade beef with black pepper and onion sauce:
  1. Get 500 g beef, cut into small cubes
  2. Get 1 onion
  3. Take 4 cloves shallots
  4. Get 5 cloves garlic
  5. Take 3 tbsp sweet soy sauce
  6. Prepare 1 tsp light soy sauce
  7. Make ready 1 tsp ground/coarse black pepper
  8. Take 1 tsp salt
  9. Take 3 tbsp ketchup
  10. Make ready 150 ml water

Instructions to make Homemade beef with black pepper and onion sauce:
  1. Finely slice the onion, shallots, and garlic
  2. Put the oil on a pan over a medium heat, and add the onion, shallots and garlic to the pan. Stir occasionally until golden brown
  3. Add the beef to the pan, season with salt and pepper. Stir until the meat is cooked thoroughly
  4. Add the sweet soy sayce, light soy sauce, more pepper, more salt, and water. Mix well with the meat. Cover the pan with a lid and wait for 10 minutes
  5. After 10 minutes, open the lid and add the ketchup, taste and adjust the seasoning as needed. The close the lid again, wait for another 5 minutes
  6. Finally open the lid and stir it again. And voillaaaa, it’s ready to serve. It pairs well with a hot steamed rice!
