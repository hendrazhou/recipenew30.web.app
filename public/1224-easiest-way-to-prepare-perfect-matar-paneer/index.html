The ingredients needed to make Matar paneer:
  1. Make ready 250 gms paneer
  2. Take 1 cup peas(frozen/fresh)
  3. Take 2 finely chopped onion
  4. Prepare 2 tsp ginger garlic paste
  5. Get 1 tsp coriander powder
  6. Take 1 tsp cumin powder
  7. Make ready 1 tsp red chilli powder
  8. Get 1 tsp Kashmir red chilli powder
  9. Take 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  10. Get 1 tsp sugar
  11. Prepare 4 tbsp cooking oil
  12. Make ready 1/2 cup curd/dahi
  13. Take 1 tbsp kasuri methi
  14. Prepare to taste Salt
  15. Make ready 1 tsp garam masala powder
  16. Make ready 1 tejpata
  17. Get 1 tsp cumin seeds
  18. Take 2 cardamoms
  19. Make ready 1 cup powder

Steps to make Matar paneer:
  1. Heat oil in a pan,add tejpata,cardamom and cumin seeds.
  2. Add turmeric powder,red chilli powder and chopped onion.
  3. Cook for 2-3 mins on medium flame.
  4. Then add ginger garlic paste and cook for a minute
  5. Add chopped tomatoes and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Then lower the flame add curd ana cook for 5mins.
  7. In the cooked mixture add1glass of water,sugar and salt.
  8. Add green peas ana let all the mixture boil.
  9. After that add paneer and again let it cook for 5mins.
  10. Add garam masala and kasuri methi powder and turn off the flame.
  11. Gravy Matar paneer is ready to serve.
