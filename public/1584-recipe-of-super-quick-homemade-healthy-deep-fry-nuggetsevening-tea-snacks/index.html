The ingredients needed to make Healthy Deep fry Nuggets.(evening tea snacks):
  1. Get 4 boiled potatoes
  2. Take 1 chopped onion
  3. Take 1 chopped tomato
  4. Make ready 1 chopped green chilli
  5. Make ready 1 spoon suji
  6. Prepare 1 spoon wheat flour
  7. Prepare 1 pinch red chilli powder
  8. Prepare 1tbsp garam masala
  9. Make ready 1 pinch black pepper powder
  10. Make ready 1 packet maggi masala
  11. Get to taste salt according
  12. Prepare As required chopped green peas
  13. Prepare 1 tbsp garlic ginger paste
  14. Take as needed oil for deep fry
Steps to make Healthy Deep fry Nuggets.(evening tea snacks):
  1. Take a bowl put into potato and mash it.
  2. After that put into onion,tomato,green chilli,ginger garlic paste,green peas and mix well.
  3. After that put into suji and wheat flour, salt, garam masala, red chilli powder, black pepper powder, Maggi masala and mix well again.
  4. And the other side take a pan and heat the oil for deep fry.
  5. When the oil is heated and mixture is ready,make small balls with mixture and deep fry into oil.
  6. When it change into light brown colour take out from pan and serve it with chilli or tomato sauce.
