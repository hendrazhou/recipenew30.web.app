The ingredients needed to make Salmon Pandan:
  1. Take Pandan Leafs (this is a type of herb)
  2. Prepare Salmon (sliced)
  3. Prepare Sesameseed oil
  4. Prepare Lemon
  5. Get Dashi
  6. Take Water
Instructions to make Salmon Pandan:
  1. Cut the young bits of Pandan, add squeezed lemon and water
  2. Fry Salmon slice in pan with sesame seed oil
  3. Add pandan sauce, now soft good to taste
  4. Add dashi and fry well
  5. Pick up fried Salmon without the sauce, serve with rice - seaweed
