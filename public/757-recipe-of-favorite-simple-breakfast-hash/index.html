The ingredients needed to make Simple breakfast hash:
  1. Take 1/2 bag. Ore Ida tater tots
  2. Prepare 5 eggs beaten
  3. Take 1/2 cup 2% milk
  4. Get 4 sliced bacon; crumbled
  5. Get 1/4 cup Jimmy dean's crumbled sausage
  6. Take 1/4 cup Kroger diced ham
  7. Take 1/2 onion; diced
  8. Take 1/4 green pepper; diced
  9. Take 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  10. Prepare 1 Tsp. Onion Powder

  12. Take 1/2 Tsp. Celery salt
  13. Prepare 1/2 Tsp. Slap-ya-mama; Cajun seasoning

Steps to make Simple breakfast hash:
  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Spray or coat the bottom of a cast iron skillet or baking pan.
  3. Later bottom of skillet or pan with tater tots.
  4. Next add meat and onions on top.
  5. In a bowl brat together eggs, milk, and seasonings.
  6. Pour egg mixture over meat and tater tots.
  7. Sprinkle with cheese and bake for 30 to 35 minutes.
  8. Remove and let cool down a little.
  9. Take a knife and run it along the edge of pan and has to loosen from sides.
  10. Serve and enjoy!
