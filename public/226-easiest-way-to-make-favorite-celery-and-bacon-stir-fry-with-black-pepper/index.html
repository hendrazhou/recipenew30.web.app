The ingredients needed to make Celery and Bacon Stir-fry with Black Pepper:
  1. Take 1/2 stick Celery (and leaves)
  2. Prepare 1 slice Bacon
  3. Prepare 1 tsp Shiro-dashi
  4. Get 1 Black pepper
  5. Take 1 Garlic powder

Steps to make Celery and Bacon Stir-fry with Black Pepper:
  1. Slice the celery into 5 mm thick pieces, and the bacon into 1 cm strips.
  2. Coat a frying pan with olive oil and fry the bacon until browned. Add the celery and fry until it becomes tender.
  3. Lower the heat, season with the shiro-dashi, then sprinkle in the black pepper and garlic powder. Adjust the amounts to your tastes. Lightly fry, and it's done.
