The ingredients needed to make Paneer paratha:
  1. Get 100 gm paneer
  2. Take 1 medium sized onion
  3. Take 1/2 cup wheat dough
  4. Prepare to taste Salt
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp Red chilli or chilli flakes
  6. Take 1/2 tsp Oregano
  7. Make ready 1/4 tsp Black pepper
  8. Make ready as needed oil

Steps to make Paneer paratha:
  1. Mash paneer…finely chop onion..add salt and spices.mix them to make stuffing
  2. Take dough.and roll it a little
  3. Then keep the stuffing over it…and make it either of two ways….if your paneer parantha break while rolling then use 1st method.
  4. Seal it properly and roll it again
  5. Put on hot tawa.. and keep on cooking and flipping applying oil.
  6. Paratha is ready to serve
