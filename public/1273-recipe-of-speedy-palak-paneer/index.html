The ingredients needed to make Palak paneer:
  1. Make ready 3 cups baby spinach
  2. Prepare 1-2 green chillies
  3. Make ready 4 cloves garlic
  4. Prepare 1 large piece ginger
  5. Prepare 3 tablespoons olive oil
  6. Take 1 cinnamon stick
  7. Get 1 piece bay leave
  8. Prepare 2 cardamom pods
  9. Make ready 1 teaspoon garlic, minced
  10. Take 1/4 cup onion, finely chopped
  11. Prepare 3-4 tomatoes, finely chopped
  12. Take 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  13. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon Garam masala powder
  14. Get 2 tablespoons kasuri methi (Optional)
  15. Prepare 2 tablespoons heavy cream
  16. Take 4 slc of happy cow cheese
  17. Take 2 cubes cheese
  18. Take to taste Salt
