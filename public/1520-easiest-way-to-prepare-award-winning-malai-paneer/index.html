The ingredients needed to make Malai Paneer:
  1. Get 1 cup Paneer pieces
  2. Make ready 1 cup Milk
  3. Make ready 2 teaspoon cashews powder
  4. Take 2 teaspoon milk powder
  5. Make ready 2teaspoon fresh cream
  6. Prepare as taste Rock salt
  7. Take 1/4 teaspoon black pepper powder
  8. Get 1/4 teaspoon cumin powder
  9. Take 2 pieces green chili
  10. Make ready 1 teaspoon chopped coriander

Instructions to make Malai Paneer:
  1. Heat the milk in a pan and add the cashew powder
  2. Then mix the paneer pieces, salt and pepper and cook it a little.
  3. Then put the milk powder, cumin powder and cook it
  4. Now mix cream and serve it with green chili and coriander
