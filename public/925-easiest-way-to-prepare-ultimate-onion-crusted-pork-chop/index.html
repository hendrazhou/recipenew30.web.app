The ingredients needed to make Onion Crusted Pork Chop:
  1. Get 3 thick pork chops
  2. Make ready 2 C fried onions
  3. Take 2 eggs
  4. Take 2/3 C flour
  5. Prepare 1/4 C milk
  6. Get 1 T Italian seasoning
  7. Take 1/4 C grated romano cheese
  8. Take 1 pinch kosher salt and black pepper
Instructions to make Onion Crusted Pork Chop:
  1. Set up 3 step frying station. Flour, egg wash, then fried onions. Crushed the fried onions with your hands, then add seasoning and mix to combine. Briefly whisk milk into eggs until smooth to make your egg wash.
  2. Use your left hand to dredge each pork chop in flour and shake off excess. Use your right hand as your 'wet hand' and dip the chop in the egg wash. Drop into third mixing bowl full of crushed, fried onions and use your left hand to press onion mixture firmly around every surface of chop. Place onto baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Sprinkle cheese over each chop.
  3. Bake at 350° for approximately 30 minutes or until pork reaches desired doneness.
