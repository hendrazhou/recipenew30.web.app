The ingredients needed to make Make And Take Maple-Apple Pancakes:
  1. Make ready 2 cup Flour
  2. Prepare 2 tbsp Brown sugar
  3. Prepare 2 tsp Baking soda
  4. Prepare 2 tsp Baking powder
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp Salt
  6. Make ready 1 cup Applesauce (see my applesauce recipe)
  7. Get 1 cup Milk
  8. Prepare 1 tbsp Vanilla
  9. Get 2 medium Eggs
  10. Get 16 tbsp Maple syrup
  11. Prepare 1 Cooking spray, bacon drippings or coconut oil
Steps to make Make And Take Maple-Apple Pancakes:
  1. Heat griddle on medium high.
  2. In a large mixing bowl combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda and salt.
  3. In a 2 cup measuring glass whisk the applesauce, milk and vanilla.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry. Add the eggs and whisk everything together.
  5. Oil the griddle.
  6. Pour about 1/4 a cup of batter for each pancake onto the griddle. You want the batter a bit on the runny side so the maple doesn't sit on one side of the cake causing it to stick once you flip it over.
  7. Quickly drizzle 1 tablespoon of maple syrup onto each uncooked pa cake. Avoid the edges and try not to get too much in one place or the maple will harden on your grill.
  8. Cook for about 5 minutes or until the edges look dry and the entire pancake is bubbly.
  9. Once you have flipped the pancakes, allow to cook a few minutes more before removing from the grill.
  10. Transfer pancakes to a plate or wax paper and repeat process.
