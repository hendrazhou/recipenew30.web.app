The ingredients needed to make Paneer Tikka:
  1. Get Marination
  2. Make ready 300 gm paneer
  3. Take 1/2 cup hung curd
  4. Take 2 tbsp Roasted besan
  5. Make ready 2 tbsp Soaked musk melon& soaked cashew paste
  6. Take 1 tbsp turmeric
  7. Prepare 2 tbsp kashmiri red chilli
  8. Get 2 tbsp ginger and chili paste
  9. Take 1 tsp Roasted cumin powder
  10. Prepare 1 tbsp coriander powder
  11. Prepare 1&1/2 tsp garam masala
  12. Make ready 2 small size lemon juice
  13. Prepare to taste Salt according
  14. Get 1&1/2 tsp mustard oil

Steps to make Paneer Tikka:
  1. Take a pan. Add besan and slightly roast in medium flame
  2. Take a bowl add hung curd, salt, chily powder, coriander powder, ginger and chily paste, garam masala, roasted cumin powder, garam masala, mustard oil, muskmelon &cashew paste and roasted besan and turmeric mix all together
  3. Now cut paneer cut into big piece and add to the paste and give a gentle stir and in keep in fridge for half an hour until paneer marinate properly
  4. Soak skewer for half an hour and arrange paneer pieces
  5. Pre heat grill pan and apply butter and grill paneer in medium flame from al sides
