The ingredients needed to make Salmon loaf:
  1. Take 1 pound can salmon
  2. Get 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  3. Get 1/8 teaspoon pepper
  4. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon salt
  5. Take 2 egg yolks
  6. Make ready 1 teaspoon chopped parsley
  7. Make ready 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  8. Take 1/2 cup hot milk
  9. Prepare 2 egg whites
  10. Make ready 2 tablespoons melted butter
