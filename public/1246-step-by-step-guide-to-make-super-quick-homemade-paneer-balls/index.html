The ingredients needed to make Paneer balls:
  1. Prepare 100 gms paneer
  2. Prepare 2 boiled and mashed potatoes
  3. Get 1 onion chopped
  4. Prepare 2 green chillies chopped
  5. Prepare 1 tsp red chilli powder
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp chat masala
  7. Get to taste Salt
  8. Make ready 2 tbsp cornflour
  9. Prepare 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  10. Make ready as required Oil for deep frying

Steps to make Paneer balls:
  1. Lets mix all the ingredients. Take crumbled paneer in a large bowl.
  2. Add mashed potatoes and onion,green chilli and all spices.
  3. Mix all ingredients nicely.
  4. Take a small portion of mixture and roll it gently between the palms and form a shape of ball.
  5. Make a thin batter using cornflour.
  6. Take one ball at a time and dip them into batter.
  7. Coat the balls with bread crumbs.
  8. Heat oil and deep fry the balls at low to medium flame.
  9. Fry untill they become golden brown.
  10. When done take out in a plate.
  11. Paneer balls are crunchy outside and soft inside.
