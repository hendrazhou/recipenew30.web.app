The ingredients needed to make Palak Paneer:
  1. Take 500 gm Palak leaves
  2. Make ready 250 gm Cottage cheese
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp Oil
  4. Get 1/2 spoon Cumin seeds
  5. Get 3 Chopped onions
  6. Make ready 4 tomato Tomato puree
  7. Prepare 1 spoon Salt
  8. Make ready 1/2 spoon turmeric powder
  9. Take 1/2 spoon Red chilli powder
  10. Get 1/2 spoon Garam masala

Instructions to make Palak Paneer:
  1. Wash the palak leaves and boil it.
  2. Blend the boiled palak with blender. Keep it aside.
  3. Take a vessel on the gas stove. Put oil in it, warm it and fry cheese.
  4. Take out the cheese and in the same oil, add cumin seeds.
  5. Add onions in it and cook it till Golden brown.
  6. Add tomato puree, salt, turmeric and chilli powder. Mix well. Cook for 5 minutes.
  7. Add garam masala in it and mix well. Then add fried cheese pieces in it.
  8. Mix well and cook for 5 minutes.
  9. Palak paneer is ready.
