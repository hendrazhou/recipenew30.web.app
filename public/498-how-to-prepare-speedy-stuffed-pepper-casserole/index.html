The ingredients needed to make Stuffed Pepper Casserole:
  1. Take -stuffed peppers——
  2. Prepare 4 large green bell peppers
  3. Prepare 2 pounds ground meat, I used beef
  4. Take 1 small onion finely chopped
  5. Get 1 tablespoon granulated garlic powder
  6. Prepare 1-1/2 teaspoons smoked paprika
  7. Get 1 teaspoon seasoned salt
  8. Take 1/2 teaspoon seasoned salt
  9. Take 1-1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  10. Get 1/2 cup crumbled cheddar cheese, I used colby
  11. Prepare –Sauce——
  12. Make ready 1/3 cup raisins
  13. Take 29 ounces crushed tomatoes
  14. Make ready 1 teaspoon salt
  15. Prepare 1/3 cup maple syrup
  16. Prepare 1-1/2 cups crumbled cheddar cheese I used colby
  17. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon basil chopped
  18. Get 1 teaspoon oregano
  19. Get 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric
  20. Take 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  21. Make ready —–Optional——
  22. Take to taste sour cream
Instructions to make Stuffed Pepper Casserole:
  1. Preheat oven 350 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Cut the tops off the peppers, remove the steams and seeds
  3. Mix the meat and spices together
  4. Pack the meat into each pepper
  5. Place the top of peppers back on
  6. The leftover meat brown in a pan.
  7. Add spices of the sauce.
  8. Add tomatoes and raisins simmer 10 minutes add maple syrup and stir in
  9. Add the peppers in the sauce and bake in oven for 1 hour
  10. Add cheese to tops of peppers where the steam was.
  11. Take out of oven
  12. Cut the peppers down the middle and place in a pan
  13. Pour sauce over the top
  14. Add the rest of the cheese
  15. Put into oven for 30 minutes
  16. Serve add sour cream if you like. I hope you enjoy!
