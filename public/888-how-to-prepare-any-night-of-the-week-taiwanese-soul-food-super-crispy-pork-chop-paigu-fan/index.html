The ingredients needed to make Taiwanese soul food super crispy pork chop (paigu fan):
  1. Prepare 4 pieces pork chops
  2. Take 70 ml soy sauce (for marinated)
  3. Take 1/2 tbsp white pepper (for marinated)
  4. Make ready black pepper a lot (for marinated)
  5. Prepare 1/4 tbsp cinnamon powder (for marinated)
  6. Make ready 5 spices powder 1/4 tsp (for marinated)
  7. Get 2 tsp chinese oyster sauce (for marinated)
  8. Prepare 3 tbsp sugar (for marinated)
  9. Make ready 100 ml water (for marinated)
  10. Prepare 100 ml cooking wine (for marinated)
  11. Take 3-4 eggs
  12. Prepare 8 tbsp tapioca starch
  13. Get 8 tbsp potato starch

Steps to make Taiwanese soul food super crispy pork chop (paigu fan):
  1. Few quick cuts to keep the chops from curling
  2. Hammer the pork chops to make it tender
  3. Place the meat into ziploc then add all materials for marinated
  4. Stay in fridge overnight
  5. Next day, coat the meat with egg, tapioca starch and potato starch
  6. Bake with 220°c(428°F) per side for 10 min, total 20 min
  7. Pan fry to make it extra crispy!!!
