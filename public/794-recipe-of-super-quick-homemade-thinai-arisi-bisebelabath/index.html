Steps to make Thinai arisi bisebelabath:
  1. Soak the foxtail millet overnight or up to 6 hrs so that it will cook well.
  2. Wash and soak the toor dhal for 20 minutes and meanwhile take the tamarind and soak it for 30 minutes.
  3. Now In a pressure pan or cooker, add 4 tbsp of oil and add the items mentioned under tempering or else you can skip this step and do it atlast.
  4. Add the onion,garlic,green chilies and saute till golden brown.
  5. Then add tomatoes and saute till mushy add some salt so that it will cook earlier.
  6. Now add all the veggies you used saute for 10 mins on medium flame.
  7. Add the tamarind pulp and then add the water. For millets we usually use 3 cups of water. Here we used dhal so that we are adding extra water to get the consistency.
  8. Now add sambar powder, kulambu chilli powder and rasam powder (we use rasam powder because it will give nice aroma if you don't like skip it.).
  9. Check the salt and spicy adjust to your need.
  10. Cover the cooker and pressure cook for 3 whistle on a medium high flame.Turn off the stove.
  11. Tadaaa.. The Healthy foxtail millet bisbelabath receipe is ready.
  12. Serve it with fryums & pickle.
