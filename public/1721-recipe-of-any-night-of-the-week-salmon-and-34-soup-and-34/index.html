The ingredients needed to make Salmon "Soup":
  1. Take 1 kg salmon (head, tail - included)
  2. Prepare 1 carrot
  3. Prepare 1 parsnip
  4. Make ready 1 red bell pepper
  5. Take 1 green bell pepper
  6. Make ready 2 onions
  7. Prepare 3 celery sticks
  8. Get 1 bunch parsley
  9. Prepare 1 bunch lovage
  10. Make ready 1 lemon
  11. Take 1 tin mackerel fillets in tomato sauce
  12. Make ready Salt and pepper

Instructions to make Salmon "Soup":

  2. When all vegetables are half way cooked add the salmon and cook for another 10 min
  3. Add the mackerel tin with the tomato sauce including
  4. Squeeze the lemon juice and season with salt and pepper
