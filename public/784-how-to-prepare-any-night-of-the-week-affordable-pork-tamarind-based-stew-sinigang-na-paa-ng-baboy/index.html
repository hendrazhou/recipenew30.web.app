The ingredients needed to make Affordable Pork Tamarind-based Stew (Sinigang na Paa ng Baboy):
  1. Prepare 1 kg pork knuckles
  2. Prepare 1 tsp tamarind based powder pack
  3. Prepare 1 liter water
  4. Take 1 bunch string beans
  5. Get 5 small okra
  6. Prepare 1 cup raddish - sliced
  7. Prepare 1 medium onion
  8. Take 3 small tomatoes
  9. Make ready 3 small chili picantes
  10. Take 1 tbsp salt
Steps to make Affordable Pork Tamarind-based Stew (Sinigang na Paa ng Baboy):
  1. Boil pork knuckles in an hour. Set aside with the broth.
  2. Heat 2 cups of water, add chopped onions and tomato. Also add salt to taste.
  3. Add boiled pork knuckles and the broth. Simmer for 5 mimutes.
  4. Add tamarind powder pack.
  5. Add chili picante or pansigang na sili and raddish until it boils.
  6. Add okra and string beans and simmer for 1 minute.
  7. Serve while hot.
  8. This is popularly known in the Philippines as Sinigang na Paa ng Baboy.
