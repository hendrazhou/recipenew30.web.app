The ingredients needed to make Quick Breakfast – Egg Mini's:
  1. Make ready 1/3 cup milk
  2. Take 6 each eggs
  3. Take 1/4 cup chopped onion
  4. Make ready 1/4 cup bell pepper and/or red pepper
  5. Make ready 1 dash salt
  6. Make ready 1 can cooking spray
  7. Take 1 each muffin pan
  8. Get 1/2 cup of shredded sharp cheese (optional)
Steps to make Quick Breakfast – Egg Mini's:
  1. preheat oven to 375
  2. mix all ingredients above on a mixing bowl.
  3. spray the muffin pan with cooking spray and pour mixture in each cup.
  4. cook for 15-20 minutes until eggs are fluffy and done.
  5. scoop each egg mini out and serve with fruit or garnish your regular breakfast. ENJOY!
