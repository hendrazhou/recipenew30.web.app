The ingredients needed to make Red pepper, mushroom cajun chicken thighs:
  1. Take 4 chicken thighs, skin off
  2. Make ready 1 red pepper, sliced
  3. Prepare 12 mushrooms, sliced
  4. Get 1 small onion, sliced
  5. Take 1/4 C cajon seasoning (I use Joes' recipe)
  6. Prepare 2 T butter
  7. Get 1-2 T oil
  8. Make ready 1/2-3/4 C coconut milk
  9. Make ready Shredded parmesan cheese
  10. Get Thyme, a few sprigs (pull out when serving)
  11. Get 1/8 tsp garlic powder
  12. Make ready 1/8 tsp pepper
  13. Prepare 1/4 tsp salt
  14. Take 2 C cooked rice

Steps to make Red pepper, mushroom cajun chicken thighs:
  1. Prepare rice according to package. Set aside. Coat the chicken well with the cajon rub. Use an iron skillet on medium heat to melt the butter and oil. Cook the chicken for 6 minutes each side.
