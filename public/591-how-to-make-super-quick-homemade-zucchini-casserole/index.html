The ingredients needed to make Zucchini Casserole:
  1. Make ready 5 large zucchini, peeled and cut into coins
  2. Take 1 large tomato, sliced
  3. Take 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  4. Prepare 1/3 cup Parmesan cheese
  5. Prepare 2/3 cup Bisquick mix
  6. Prepare 3/4 cup whole milk
  7. Make ready 2 eggs
  8. Prepare 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  9. Take Canola oil cooking spray

Instructions to make Zucchini Casserole:
  1. Spray canola oil cooking spray into a large casserole dish……
  2. Layer zucchini and tomato slices into casserole dish……
  3. Now add your shredded cheddar cheese and Parmesan cheese……
  4. In a bowl mix with a small whisk or a fork your bisquick mix,milk, eggs and black pepper…..
  5. Pour Bisquick mixture over tomato and zucchini in casserole dish…..
  6. Place in a preheated 400 degree oven for 35-40 minutes, uncovered…..
  7. Let rest for 10 minutes…..
  8. Serve and enjoy 😉!
