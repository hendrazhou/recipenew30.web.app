The ingredients needed to make Potato and Chicken  Ball with Black Pepper:
  1. Prepare 3 Boiled Potatoes
  2. Prepare 1 /2 CUP Chicken Shredded
  3. Take 1 tsp Salt
  4. Make ready 1/2 tsp Black Pepper
  5. Get 1/2 tsp Corn Flour
  6. Make ready Leaves Coriander
  7. Make ready For Fry:
  8. Take Maida
  9. Take Egg
  10. Prepare Bread Crumbs
  11. Take Oil
Instructions to make Potato and Chicken  Ball with Black Pepper:
  1. Boiled potato ko mash kr ly is m chicken dal kr achi tarah ix kr ly.
  2. Ab is k under salt,black pepper,coriander leaves aur corn flour dal kr achi tarah mix kr ly.
  3. Ab is k chote chote ball bna ly.
  4. Is k uper ab corn flour lagay phr anday m dip kry phir bread crumbs laga kr fry kr ly.
  5. Mazedar balls tayar h isy ab serve kr ly
