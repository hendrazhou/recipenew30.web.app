Steps to make Salmon flower:
  1. Mix the dry gelatine dust with a little cold water, then heat it in the microwave,stir it.
  2. Cut the other ingredients into small pieces and mix it with the gelatine and the mascarpone
  3. Put the mixture in the flower shaped silicones and put it in the fridge.
