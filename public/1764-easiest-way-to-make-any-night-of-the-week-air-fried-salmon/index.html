The ingredients needed to make Air fried Salmon:
  1. Prepare 1 kg Salmon
  2. Prepare 1/4 cup Lemon juice
  3. Get Salt as per taste
  4. Take 1 tsp Red chilli powder
  5. Make ready 1 tsp Crushed red chilli
  6. Make ready 1 tsp Black pepper
  7. Take 1 tsp Garlic powder
  8. Prepare 1 tsp Onion powder
  9. Get 1 tsp Coriander powder
  10. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp Achar ghosht masalah(optional)

Instructions to make Air fried Salmon:
  1. Marinate salmon with all spices and keep in refrigerator for 1 hour.
  2. In air fryer, air-fry each side for 10 minutes.
  3. Delicious salmon is ready. Serve with salad and bread.
