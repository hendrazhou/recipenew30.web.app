The ingredients needed to make Sautéed Shishito Peppers:
  1. Get 1/2 lbs Shishito Peppers
  2. Prepare Olive Oil
  3. Prepare Sesame Oil
  4. Make ready Lemon Juice
  5. Take Sea Salt (coarse)
Steps to make Sautéed Shishito Peppers:
  1. If rinsing your peppers, make sure they are fully dried.
  2. Heat a little olive oil and sesame oil in a wide sauté pan until it is good and hot but not smoking.
  3. Add the peppers and cook them over medium, tossing and turning them frequently until they blister (about 10-15 minutes). They shouldn't char except in places.
  4. Toss them with some fresh lemon or lime juice. And sprinkle with coarse sea salt.
