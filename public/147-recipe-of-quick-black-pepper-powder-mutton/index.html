The ingredients needed to make Black Pepper Powder Mutton:
  1. Get 500 gms mutton
  2. Prepare 500 gms curd
  3. Make ready 1 tsp haldi powder
  4. Take 1 tsp Kashmiri chilli powder
  5. Make ready Few whole garam masala
  6. Prepare Few whole peppercorns
  7. Make ready 1 tbsp white oil
  8. Make ready to taste Salt and sugar
  9. Make ready 1/2 cup water
  10. Get 1 tbsp ghee
  11. Get 2 tbsp black pepper powder
  12. Take Few whole potatoes
Steps to make Black Pepper Powder Mutton:
  1. Take small whole potatoes, and fry them in white oil.
  2. Now pressure the mutton in cooker for 15 minutes or till done.When cool, put the potatoes, and pressure again for 2 to 3 whistles.
  3. Now, check the seasoning, if required add sugar and salt according to ur taste. Add the black pepper powder, and ghee.
  4. Keep the mutton for 1/2 an hr for marination, after mixing with all the ingredients.
  5. Stir properly. Now the mutton is ready. Serve hot, with plain rice.
  6. Ghee and black pepper powder, should be put only at the end.After that it should not be boiled again.
