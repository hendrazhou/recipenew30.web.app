The ingredients needed to make Cheeseburger Pie:
  1. Prepare 1 lb ground beef
  2. Take 1 cup onion, chopped
  3. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  4. Prepare 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup Original Bisquick mix
  6. Take 1 cup Milk
  7. Make ready 2 eggs
  8. Get can cooking spray
  9. Make ready Cajun seasoning
  10. Take 1/4 tsp garlic powder
Instructions to make Cheeseburger Pie:
  1. Preheat oven to 400° F. Spray a 9-inch glass pie plate with cooking spray or use a silicon pie plate.
  2. In a 10-inch skillet, cook beef and onion over medium heat. Stir occasionally. Drain. Add salt, garlic powder and Cajun seasoning. Spread in pie plate. Sprinkle with cheese.
  3. In a small bowl stir the Original Bisquick mix, milk, and eggs with a wire wish until blended. Pour into pie plate.
  4. Bake about 20-25 minutes or until a knife inserted into the center comes out clean.
  5. You can add to your slice ketchup, BBQ sauce, bacon or anything else you would normally top your cheeseburger with.
