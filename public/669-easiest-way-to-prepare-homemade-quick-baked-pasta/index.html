Instructions to make Quick Baked Pasta üçù:
  1. Boil your pasta until slightly undercooked……
  2. Drain pasta and pour into a baking pan that has been sprayed with olive oil cooking spray…..
  3. Add to cooked pasta your favorite pasta sauce, chunky salsa, cream of mushroom soup, black pepper and garlic powder, mix all well……
  4. Place your shredded mozzarella cheese all over your pasta……
  5. Cover and cook at 350 degrees for 45 minutes…..
  6. Uncover after 45 minutes and cook until mozzarella cheese is slightly browned on top, about 5-10 minutes more……
  7. Serve and enjoy üòâ!
