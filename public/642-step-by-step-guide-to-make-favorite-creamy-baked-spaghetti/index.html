The ingredients needed to make Creamy Baked Spaghetti 🍝:
  1. Get 1 box spaghetti
  2. Take Salt for boiling water
  3. Get 1 can (10.5 ounce) cream of mushroom soup
  4. Take 1 can (10.5 ounce) cream of chicken soup
  5. Make ready 2 soup cans milk
  6. Prepare 1 bar (8 ounce) cream cheese, softened
  7. Make ready 1 tablespoon onion powder
  8. Prepare 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  9. Get 6 cloves garlic, minced
  10. Prepare Butter cooking spray
  11. Get Pepper jack cheese (deli sliced)
Instructions to make Creamy Baked Spaghetti 🍝:
  1. In a small pot heat up cream of mushroom soup, cream of chicken soup, milk, cream cheese, onion powder, black pepper and garlic, heat until cream cheese is melted into mixture……
  2. In a large pot boil spaghetti until al dente, drain well…….Don’t forget to salt your water!
  3. When cream mixture has been fully heated, pour mixture into drained spaghetti, mix all well……
  4. Pour entire mixture into a large baking pan that has been sprayed with butter cooking spray……
  5. Place several slices of the pepperjack cheese on top of spaghetti mixture, place just enough to cover your spaghetti……
  6. Place in a preheated 400 degree oven for 15- 20 minutes, uncovered…..
  7. Serve and enjoy 😉!
