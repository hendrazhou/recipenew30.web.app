The ingredients needed to make Asiago chicken sausage and peppers with rice skillet:
  1. Prepare 1 pack Costco chicken Asiago garlic chicken (it’s precooked but I brown it in the oven)
  2. Get 1 large onion chopped
  3. Make ready 1 red pepper and 1 yellow pepper chopped
  4. Make ready 1 tbsp minced garlic
  5. Get 4 cups chicken broth
  6. Prepare 1 3/4 cups par-boiled rice
  7. Make ready 1 small can tomato paste
  8. Get 1/3 cup lite cream
  9. Make ready to taste Salt and pepper
  10. Get Adobo seasoning
  11. Make ready Garlic powder
  12. Take 1/2 bag low fat shredded cheese (optional cause the sausage had cheese in already)

Instructions to make Asiago chicken sausage and peppers with rice skillet:
  1. Preheat oven to 400°. Spray a deep oven safe pot with Pam and brown the chicken sausage in it for 30 min
  2. Let it cool then place in fridge cause if you cut even at room temp the CHEESE WILL OOZE OUT!
  3. Chop up onions and garlic and place in pot that has the juices from the sausage in it. Add seasonings. Sauté til clear then add garlic and broth. Bring to a boil
  4. Add rice and reduce to a simmer. Once rice is almost done add paste and sausage and lite cream. Add cheese if you want too.
