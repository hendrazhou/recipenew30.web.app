The ingredients needed to make Balsamic Pork Chops:
  1. Take 3 thick pork chops
  2. Get 1 tbsp Italian seasoning
  3. Take 1 tbsp garlic powder
  4. Prepare 2 tsp onion powder
  5. Prepare 1 tsp dried minced onions
  6. Take 1 tsp ground celery seed
  7. Get 1 tsp dried lemon peel
  8. Make ready 1 cup balsamic vinegar
  9. Take 1 large pinch sugar
  10. Prepare 1 pinch kosher salt and black pepper
  11. Make ready 1 EVOO, as needed
Steps to make Balsamic Pork Chops:
  1. Combine vinegar and sugar in a small sauce pan. Reduce by 3/4
  2. Coat chops with enough oil to cover. Season. Bake at 350° for approximately 25 minutes or until pork reaches desired doneness. Pour vinegar reduction over chops during last 5 minutes of cooking.
  3. Variations; Red or white wiine vinegar, red or white wine marinade or reduction, fresh garlic, rosemary, lemon zest, thyme, oregano, marjoram, parsley, herbes de provence, shallots
