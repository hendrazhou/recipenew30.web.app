The ingredients needed to make Seared Scampi Salmon:
  1. Get 1/2 Tablespoon Black Pepper
  2. Prepare 1/2 Tablespoon Kosher Salt
  3. Take 1 Tablespoon Ground Paprika
  4. Prepare 1 Tablespoon olive oil (or canola oil)
  5. Make ready 6 Tablespoon Salted Butter
  6. Prepare 3 Tablespoon White Wine (Chardonnay)
  7. Take 1 Tablespoon Garlic Powder
  8. Take 4 garlic cloves minced
  9. Prepare 3 Tablespoon Parsley chopped
  10. Get 3 Tablespoon Fresh Dill
  11. Prepare 1 Whole Lemon Squeezed
  12. Prepare 1 Whole Tomato diced
  13. Get 3 Tablespoon Parmesan Cheese
  14. Get 1 Teaspoon Cayenne Pepper
  15. Take 1 Cup Heavy Cream
Instructions to make Seared Scampi Salmon:
  1. Season Salmon Garlic Powder, Salt,Black Pepper, Cayanne Pepper
  2. Heat the oil in a 12-inch nonstick skillet over medium-high heat until hot and shimmering. Cook the salmon, skin side up, until golden and crisp, about 4 minutes.
  3. Use another skillet pot or clean the first pot and use again.heat oil over med/high heat. Once hot, add the garlic and butter, saute over medium heat for 1 minute until fragrant, stirring frequently so the garlic doesn't burn.
  4. Pour in the white wine and cook for 3-4 minutes. Keep whisking so it does not burn
  5. Add heavy cream then parsley, Dill, Tomato, and Squeeze Lemon.
  6. Add Parmesan cheese, 1 Tablespoon Paprika, 1 Teaspoon of Cayenne pepper. Whisk and taste for perfection. Then serve with Salmon
