The ingredients needed to make Bacon cheesy breaded pork chops:
  1. Get 6 pork chops
  2. Make ready 1 1/2 cups bread crumbs
  3. Make ready 1 cup Mozzarella cheese
  4. Get 3 cloves garlic
  5. Take 6 Tbsp butter
  6. Take 1/2 cup bacon bits
  7. Prepare 1 tsp onion powder
  8. Prepare 1 tsp lemon pepper
  9. Make ready 2/3 cup Worcestershire
  10. Make ready 1 Tbsp A1 sauce

Steps to make Bacon cheesy breaded pork chops:
  1. Tenderize pork chops on cutting board. Combine A1 and Worcestershire in shallow pan for coating pork chops. Set oven to 350°F
  2. Combine mozzarella cheese, bacon bits, bread crumbs, and seasoning in a shallow dish.
  3. Coat pork chops in A1 mixture then in bread crumb mixture. Seer on both sides for 1-2 mins. Then place on cookie sheet.
  4. Cook pork chops for 25-30 mins in oven.
  5. Let rest for 5 mins
  6. Dig in!
