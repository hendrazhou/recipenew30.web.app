The ingredients needed to make Paneer makhani:
  1. Take 1 cup Paneer cubes
  2. Get 4 Tomatoes
  3. Take 1 Onion
  4. Take handful Cashews
  5. Prepare 5 Garlic cloves
  6. Take 1 tsp Ginger chopped
  7. Prepare 1 Bay leaf
  8. Make ready 2 cloves
  9. Get 2 Cardamom
  10. Take 1 Black cardamom
  11. Get 1 inch Cinnamon stick
  12. Make ready 3 Black pepper whole
  13. Take 2 Red chilli
  14. Prepare 1 tsp Red chilli powder
  15. Prepare 1 Green chilli slitted
  16. Make ready to taste Salt
  17. Take 4 tbsp Butter
  18. Get as needed Cream for garnishing
  19. Take 1 tsp Ginger powder
  20. Make ready 1 tsp Honey
