The ingredients needed to make Methi Paneer Matar Malai:
  1. Make ready 1 cup methi finely chopped
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup boiled peas
  3. Get 1 cup cubed paneer
  4. Prepare 1 cup heavy cream
  5. Prepare 3 onions sliced
  6. Prepare 2 green chillies chopped
  7. Get 7-8 garlic cloves
  8. Prepare 1 " ginger piece
  9. Get 7-8 cashew nuts
  10. Get 1 tomato pureed
  11. Prepare 2 tsp kashmiri red chilly powder
  12. Prepare 1 tbsp kitchen king Masala
  13. Make ready to taste Salt
  14. Prepare 1/4 tsp garam masala powder
  15. Take 1 tsp kasuri methi
  16. Get 1 tbsp ghee or butter
  17. Get 2 tbsp ot sugar

Steps to make Methi Paneer Matar Malai:
  1. Take a heavy bottom pan add sliced onions, cashews, garlic, green chillies and 1 glass of water boil for 12 to 15 mins.
  2. Once boiled cool and blend with tomatoes to fine paste.
  3. Boil methi with lots of water and salt for 10 mins, drain n keep aside.
  4. In heavy bottom pan, add ghee, add 1tsp jeera, onion tomato paste and all dry masala's and cook on medium flame for 12 to 15 mins.
  5. Once the masala is cooked well add methi and peas with 1 cup of water and cook for 3 to 4 mins
  6. Next add sugar, kasuri methi, cream and paneer, give a good mix cover and cook for a min, turn off the flame
  7. Serve hot with naan or roti.
