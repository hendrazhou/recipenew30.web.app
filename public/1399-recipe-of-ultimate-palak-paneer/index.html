  1. First chop all the onion tomato ginger garlic clean and crushed and clean and cut the palak.
  2. In a pan heat the water and Blanche the palak for 1 minutes and strain the water and cool it take the paneer and cut in the cubes homemade paneer.
  3. In a kadai heat 1 tbsp butter and add jeera and onion and tomato ginger garlic green chilli fry for 5 minutes then add 2 tablespoon curd and add palak and fry for 2 minutes, and off the flame and cool it's and grind it.
  4. In a kadai add butter heat it then add palak Paste and fry for 5 minutes and add coriander powder chilli powder and mix it well and cube fresh paneer mix slowly e and cook for 5 minutes and off the flame.
