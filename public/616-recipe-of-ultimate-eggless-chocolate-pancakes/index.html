The ingredients needed to make Eggless Chocolate Pancakes:
  1. Make ready 1/2 cup plain flour
  2. Prepare 1 tbsp cocoa powder
  3. Get 1 tbsp melted butter
  4. Take 2 tbsps condensed milk
  5. Get 1/4 cup fresh cream
  6. Prepare 1/4 cup water
  7. Make ready 1/4 tsp baking powder
  8. Take Half a pinch baking soda
  9. Get 1 pinch salt
  10. Prepare 1/4 tsp vanilla extract
  11. Get Cooking oil spray
Steps to make Eggless Chocolate Pancakes:
  1. In a bowl, sieve flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda and salt. Mix well and set aside.
  2. In another bowl, add melted butter, condensed milk, fresh cream and water. Combine well.
  3. To the wet mixture, fold in the flour mixture and combine well. Let it rest for 5 minutes.
  4. Heat a pan on medium heat. Spray some oil to it and then pour the batter into it. Cook for around 3-4 minutes till bubbles form on the surface and the surface is no longer shiny.
  5. Then flip it over.
  6. Cover the pan and cook for 3-4 minutes. Take the pancake off the pan and it's ready to serve.
