The ingredients needed to make Paneer Musallam:
  1. Prepare 250 gms paneer
  2. Prepare 1 boiled mashed potato
  3. Prepare 3 tsp chopped nuts(cashew+ raisins)
  4. Take 1+1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  5. Take 2 chopped onions
  6. Prepare 3 tomato pureed
  7. Prepare 2 green chillies ,slit
  8. Get 3 tsp cream
  9. Make ready 1 tsp kasoori methi
  10. Take 1/4th +1/2 tsp haldi
  11. Make ready 1/4th + 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  12. Make ready 1/4th +1/2 tsp garam masala
  13. Take 1/4th +1/2 tsp sukha dhaniya powder
  14. Take as required Salt
Steps to make Paneer Musallam:
  1. Heat oil, put 1tsp ginger garlic paste with green chilli followed by nuts, salt and all spices.
  2. Lastly add mashed potato. Mix well and keep aside
  3. Apply this potato mixture in paneer pieces and press with hands nicely. Toss with some more haldi and chilli powder from both sides and grill each piece in butter.
  4. Heat oil again, add ginger garlic paste. Saute till pink, add chopped onions and cook till light brown. Add tomato puree followed by all spices, kasoori methi and cream.
  5. Serve delicious yummy dish with nan or fulka.
