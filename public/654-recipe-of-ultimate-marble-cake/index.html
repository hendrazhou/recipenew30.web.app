The ingredients needed to make Marble Cake:
  1. Take 1 cup white sugar
  2. Prepare 2 cups flour
  3. Get 1 cup oil
  4. Take 1 cup milk
  5. Get 2 eggs
  6. Get 2 teaspoons baking powder
  7. Prepare 1 teaspoon vinegar
  8. Make ready 2 teaspoons vanilla
  9. Make ready 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  10. Prepare 1 pinch of salt
  11. Prepare cooking oil spray
Steps to make Marble Cake:
  1. Beat the eggs with an electric mixer for 1 minute.
  2. Add vanilla and vinegar to the eggs and beat again.
  3. Add oil, milk and sugar; beat for another minute.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix together the flour, baking powder and salt.
  5. Make a whole in the middle of the dry ingredients and add the liquid mixture to it. Beat for 1 minute.
  6. Separate the batter in half; now you have 2 bowls with half the batter in each.
  7. In one bowl, add cocoa powder and mix till it’s “chocolaty”.
  8. Spray a pan with cooking spray, add half the white batter to the pan, then add all the chocolate batter then add the rest of the white batter on top.
  9. Move a knife in between the layers so that you have a marble effect.
  10. Preheat oven on medium heat (275 degrees) and bake the cake for 25 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
