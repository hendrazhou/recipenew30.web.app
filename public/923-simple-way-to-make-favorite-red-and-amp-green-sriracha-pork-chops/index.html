  5. Take 1 t garlic powder
  6. Get 1/2 t onion powder
  7. Make ready 1/2 t dried minced onions
  8. Make ready 1 large pinch kosher salt & black pepper

Steps to make Red & Green Sriracha Pork Chops:
  1. Cover pork chops with enough oil to coat. Season. Bake at 350° for approximately 25 minutes or until pork reaches desired doneness.
  2. Spread 1 T of Musashi Midori on each chop after removing from the oven.
