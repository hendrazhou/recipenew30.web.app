The ingredients needed to make Air fried Salmon:
  1. Make ready 1 kg Salmon
  2. Get 1/4 cup Lemon juice
  3. Prepare Salt as per taste
  4. Get 1 tsp Red chilli powder
  5. Make ready 1 tsp Crushed red chilli
  6. Get 1 tsp Black pepper
  7. Prepare 1 tsp Garlic powder
  8. Make ready 1 tsp Onion powder
  9. Get 1 tsp Coriander powder
  10. Get 1 1/2 tsp Achar ghosht masalah(optional)

Instructions to make Air fried Salmon:

  2. In air fryer, air-fry each side for 10 minutes.
  3. Delicious salmon is ready. Serve with salad and bread.
