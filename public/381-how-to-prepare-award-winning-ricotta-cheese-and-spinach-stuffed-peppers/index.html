The ingredients needed to make Ricotta Cheese and Spinach Stuffed Peppers:
  1. Take 3 medium or large green, red or yellow bell peppers
  2. Take 1 lb lean ground turkey or beef
  3. Prepare 15 oz tub ricotta cheese
  4. Take 1 1/2 cup spinach leaves
  5. Make ready 2-3 garlic cloves
  6. Get 1 medium onion
  7. Make ready 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning
  8. Get 14.5 oz fire roasted tomatoes
  9. Take 1 tsp chicken soup base
  10. Make ready 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  11. Make ready to taste Salt and pepper
  12. Take Parmesan cheese

Steps to make Ricotta Cheese and Spinach Stuffed Peppers:
  1. Pre heat oven 375 degrees
  2. In a large skillet, add your garlic, onion and brown your turkey or beef.
  3. Add your ricotta cheese and spinach, Italian seasoning, fire roasted tomatoes, soup base, Worcestershire sauce, salt and pepper. Give it a great stir and make sure your spinach is wilted.
  4. Now cut the tops off bell peppers and pull seeds out. Place on a baking sheet. Stuff your peppers full. Use the rest of the filling around your peppers. Sprinkle parmesan cheese on top.
  5. Cook for 30 minutes. Enjoy!
