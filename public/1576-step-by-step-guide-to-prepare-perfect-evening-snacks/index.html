The ingredients needed to make Evening snacks:
  1. Get 2 cups Paneer cubes
  2. Make ready 1 capsicum cut into cubes
  3. Get 1 Onion cut into cubes
  4. Get 2 tsp Soya sauce
  5. Take 1 tsp Vinegar
  6. Prepare 4 tsp Red Chilli sauce
  7. Take 2 tsp Green chilli chopped
  8. Take to taste Salt
  9. Prepare 1 tsp Black pepper
  10. Take 2 tsp Cornstarch
  11. Take 2 tbsp Oil
  12. Get 2 tsp Chopped garlic
  13. Prepare 1 tsp Chopped ginger
  14. Prepare 1 tbsp Coriander leaves chopped

Steps to make Evening snacks:
  1. Firstly take a bowl..add paneer cubes add 1tsp cornstarch, salt 1/4 tsp, black pepper 1/4 tsp mix it gently.
  2. Now heat a Pan add oil and add paneer cubes one by one,saute on high flame for 2-4 minutes.Now take out from pan. Keep aside.
  3. After that take another pan when heat the Pan add oil then add green chillies, garlic, ginger,cook it for 2 minutes then add onion, capsicum toss it gently then add soya sauce, vinegar, Red Chilli sauce,mix it well.
  4. After that make cornstarch slurry,put it in pan,mix well then add salt, black pepper,cook For 5 minutes,garnish with coriander leaves,ready to serve hot spicy chilli paneer.
