Instructions to make Black pepper chicken steak:
  1. Wash and clean the chicken breast then flatten it then hammer it.
  2. Now take a bowl then all the ingredients mix it then marinate the chicken breast and keep in fridge for 1 to 2 hours(if you have beef undercut meat then marinated for 3 to 4 hours).
  3. Heat the grill pan add butter and oil then add marinated chicken breast and grill it on high heat for 12 to 15 minutes. After 8 minutes flip the chicken breast from one side to another side and cook it until it becomes tender both sides.
  4. Now in the meantime to make the black pepper sauce heat the pan add butter and oil add onions sautÃ© for 2 minutes then add garlic and spices stir it well.
