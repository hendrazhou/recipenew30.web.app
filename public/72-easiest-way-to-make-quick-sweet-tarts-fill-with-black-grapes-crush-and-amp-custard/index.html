The ingredients needed to make Sweet tarts fill with black grapes crush,& custard:

  2. Make ready 2 cup all purpose flour
  3. Make ready 80 gm butter

  5. Make ready 1 pinch salt

  13. Make ready 1 Tsp milkmaid
  14. Make ready 2 Tsp custard
  15. Make ready 2 Tsp sugar
  16. Prepare 2 Tsp crushed dryfruits
  17. Take 1 Tsp vanilla extract

Instructions to make Sweet tarts fill with black grapes crush,& custard:
  1. Take a bowl put flour, salt, sugar,mix it together.
  2. Add chilled butter,mix it in flour,make it like a Crumbled.
  3. Knead it lightly with hands.
  4. Take tart molds.now take little portion of kneaded flour,put it in the molds & press it.fork it all sides.
  5. Now make all tarts same patterns.put some dried rajma in the tarts for blind baking.
  6. Keep it in preheated oven at 150 degree for 5 miniutes..
  7. After 5 miniutes remove the rajma & bake it again 5 miniutes.
  8. After 5 miniutes take it out from the oven.keep it a side.let it cool.
  9. Take a pan put sugar, dissolve it,add crushed grapes,cook it 2 miniutes in the high flames.
  10. Add lime & ginger juice.remove it from the flame.keep it a side.
  11. In another pan boil the milk.
  12. In a bowl put custard,add 4 tsp water or milk, dissolve it.
  13. Add it in the boiled milk.add sugar mix it.when it is thicken, remove it from the flame.
  14. Add Dryfruits,& vanilla mix it well.keep it a side for cooling.
  15. Now take out all tarts from the mold.
  16. Sprinkle some grapes crushed,put custard, again sprinkle crush.
  17. On the top put more grapes crush.
