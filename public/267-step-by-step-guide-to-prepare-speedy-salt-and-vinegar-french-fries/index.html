The ingredients needed to make Salt and Vinegar French Fries:
  1. Get 5 lb Russet Potatoes
  2. Get 3 cup White Vinegar
  3. Get 3 cup Water
  4. Take 1 Course Sea Salt (to your liking)
  5. Make ready 1 Black Pepper (to your liking)
  6. Make ready 3 tbsp Olive Oil
Instructions to make Salt and Vinegar French Fries:
  1. Cut potatoes into french fries. I used a french fry cutter, so easy!
  2. In a large pot, combine vinegar and water.
  3. Add fries and bring to a boil.
  4. Let cook until tender.
  5. Pre heat oven to 400.
  6. Let cool in vinegar and water. About 1/2 hour.
  7. Drain and toss in olive oil.
  8. Place fries on lightly greased cookie sheet.
  9. Add salt and pepper, bake until browned.
  10. Enjoy!
