The ingredients needed to make Hyderabadi Paneer:
  1. Prepare 1 cup diced paneer
  2. Make ready 1 handful coriander leaf
  3. Get Handful spinach
  4. Take 3 bay leaf
  5. Make ready 1 tsp cumin seed
  6. Take 1 tsp melon seed
  7. Get 1 big cadarmom
  8. Make ready 1 inch dalchini
  9. Make ready 2 long chopped onion
  10. Get 3 chopped tomato
  11. Get 1 tsp salt,
  12. Get 1/2 tsp Red chilli powder,garam masala,kitchen king masala
  13. Take 1/2 cup milk
  14. Prepare 2 tbsp oil
  15. Prepare 6-7 garlic clove
  16. Get 1 inch ginger
  17. Get 2 green chilli
  18. Make ready 1 tsp kasoori methi
Steps to make Hyderabadi Paneer:
  1. Collect all dry masalas
  2. Heat kadhai add 1 spn oil put bay leaf and cardamom and dal chini
  3. Now add onion saute it for 1 min now add tomato and green chilli and cover it fir 2 mins
  4. Now add coriander leaf and spinach mix it nicely.now add melon seed and cumin seeds cover it for baithe 5 mins. Off the flame cool it completely and grind it nicely
  5. Now heat kadhai add this paste saute it add masala and mix it add kasuri methi and mix it. Add paneer in it and mix it
