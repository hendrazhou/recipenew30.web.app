The ingredients needed to make Paneer Bhurji:
  1. Make ready 250 grams paneer (cottage cheese)
  2. Prepare 2 teaspoon chilli powder
  3. Prepare 1 onion chopped
  4. Make ready 1 tomato chopped
  5. Get 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  6. Take 20 ml oil
Steps to make Paneer Bhurji:
  1. In pan add oil and heat
  2. Add cumin seeds and onions. Cook for a while till onions glazed
  3. Add tomatoes and cook for another2 mins
  4. Add mashed paneer, chilli powder and stir.
  5. Add a pinch of salt and garnish with coriander leaves
