The ingredients needed to make Baked Pork Chops and Brown Rice:
  1. Make ready 3-4 bone in pork chops
  2. Prepare 4 cups brown rice
  3. Take 3 1/2 cups water
  4. Take 1 1/2 tsp rosemary
  5. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp onion powder
  6. Prepare to taste Salt and pepper
Instructions to make Baked Pork Chops and Brown Rice:
  1. Set oven to 375°
  2. Spray a baking dish with cooking spray, and put in your rice.
  3. Boil water and pour over rice.
  4. Place chops on top and season with onion powder, salt, pepper, and rosemary.
  5. Cover with foil and bake for 45 min to an hour.
