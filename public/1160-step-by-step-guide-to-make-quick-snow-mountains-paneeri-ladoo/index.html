The ingredients needed to make Snow mountains

(Paneeri ladoo):

  1. Make ready paneer-2cup
  2. Make ready 1 cup powdered sugar
  3. Make ready grated petha sweet- as per your taste
  4. Make ready roasted shredded coconut-1/2cup
  5. Make ready tooti-frooti- (1tbsp red) (1tbsp green) (u can stuff dry fruits)
  6. Prepare 1 cup shrdded coconut

Steps to make Snow mountains

(Paneeri ladoo):

  1. Mash paneer.
  2. Now add sugar, coconut, petha - mix well.
  3. Do flatten on palm. - stuff with tooti frooti.
  4. Make a shape of your choice.
  5. Coat well with roasted shredded coconut.
  6. Keep in fridge for 15-20 min. - ready. - enjoy❤️
