The ingredients needed to make Breakfast Omelette in a Pepper:
  1. Prepare 1-2 eggs
  2. Take 1/4 tsp oregano
  3. Make ready 1/4 tsp rosemary
  4. Prepare 1/4 tsp thyme
  5. Get Salt and Pepper
  6. Take 1 red bell pepper
Instructions to make Breakfast Omelette in a Pepper:
  1. Preheat the oven at 190 deg C.
  2. Mix all the ingredients except for the red pepper in a small bowl.
  3. Pour the mixture in the bell pepper and pop in the oven.
  4. Leave for around 20 to 30 mins depending on your oven. Then serve on a plate and bon appetit.
