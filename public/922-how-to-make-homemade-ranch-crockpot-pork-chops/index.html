The ingredients needed to make Ranch Crockpot Pork Chops:
  1. Make ready 5-6 boneless center cut pork chops
  2. Make ready 1 envelope ranch dressing seasoning
  3. Make ready 1 can cream of chicken
  4. Prepare 1 can cream of mushroom
  5. Get to taste salt & pepper
  6. Take cooking spray
  7. Get 2 tbsp dried minced onion

Steps to make Ranch Crockpot Pork Chops:
  1. Spray the bottom of your crockpot with cooking spray.
  2. Add pork chops to crockpot. Pour envelope of ranch seasoning evenly over the pork chops.
  3. In small bowl combine both soups & stir to mix together, then pour over pork chops in the crockpot as well.
  4. Sprinkle with dried minced onion & cover. Set on high for 4 hrs or on low for 6-8 hrs. Serve over mashed potatoes (optional) & enjoy!
