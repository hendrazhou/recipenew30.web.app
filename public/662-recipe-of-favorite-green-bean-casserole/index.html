The ingredients needed to make green bean casserole:
  1. Prepare 1 1/3 cup french's fried onions
  2. Get 4 can cream of mushroom soup
  3. Take 1/4 tsp black pepper
  4. Prepare 1 medium casserole dish
  5. Get 1 1/2 cup milk
  6. Prepare 4 can green beans (drained)
  7. Prepare 1 can cooking spray or foil

Instructions to make green bean casserole:
  1. preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. spray with nonstick cooking spray or line casserole dish with foil
  3. mix soup,milk,pepper,green beans,and fried onions together in casserole dish.
  4. place in the oven to cook for 30mins
  5. take out from the oven stir then place the remaining fried onions on top and cook for 5mins.
  6. take out and let it cool for 2-5mins then enjoy!
