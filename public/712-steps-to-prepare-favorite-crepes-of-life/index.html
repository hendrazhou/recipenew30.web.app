The ingredients needed to make crepes of life!!!:
  1. Prepare 2 cup flour
  2. Take 1 cup milk
  3. Get 5 egg yolks
  4. Get 1 tsp salt
  5. Prepare 4 tbsp butter, melted
  6. Make ready 1 cup water
  7. Make ready 1 butter spray
  8. Prepare for filling
  9. Prepare 1 cup chorizo sausages
  10. Make ready 2 medium onions (chopped)
  11. Take 1 cup chopped tomatoes
  12. Prepare 3 clove garlic
  13. Take 1 salt (to taste)
  14. Get 3 tbsp olive oil
  15. Take 1/2 cup chicken franks

Steps to make crepes of life!!!:
    1. Drizzle olive oil into a sauce pan, add onions and garlic, stir fry till tender.
    1. Using your fingers, squeeze out chorizo from skin, chop chicken Frank's and add both to onions. Stir fry for about 2 mins. Add tomatoes and salt . Stir and allow to cook for 5 mins, then turn off the heat
    1. For the crepes: Add eggs, milk, butter and water together in a bowl. Whisk together, then gradually add flour and salt. Mix till you have a smooth consistency.
    1. Grease pan with butter spray and allow to heat for so e seconds, pour 1/4 cup of batter into pan and tilt from side to side so it spreads out in the pan. Fry till the bottom is light brown then turn over to fry the other sides till it's light brown too.
    1. When crepes are ready, place each crepe on a flat surface, spoon a little filling to the edge closer to you, then roll and place on plate flap side down so the filling doesn't spill out.
