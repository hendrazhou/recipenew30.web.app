The ingredients needed to make Easy Teriyaki Pork Chops:
  1. Take 1/2 cup soy sauce
  2. Make ready 1 cup teriyaki sauce
  3. Make ready 1 cup water
  4. Take 1/2 teaspoon grated ginger
  5. Take 1/2 teaspoon garlic
  6. Take 1 chopped green onion stalk
  7. Make ready 4 pork chops

Steps to make Easy Teriyaki Pork Chops:
  1. Grate the ginger, chop the garlic, and chop the green onions.
  2. Combine all ingredients except for the pork chops in a medium bowl, mix.
  3. In a large skillet on medium heat, brown both sides of the pork chops in a little bit of oil. Pour teriyaki mixture over chops. Add more water if chops aren't completely covered.
  4. Bring to a boil. Lower tempature and simmer for an hour and twenty minutes, turning chops halfway through.
