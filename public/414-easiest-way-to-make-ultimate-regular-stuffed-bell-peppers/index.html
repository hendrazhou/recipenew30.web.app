The ingredients needed to make Regular Stuffed Bell Peppers:
  1. Prepare 4 green bell peppers
  2. Prepare 1 lb ground turkey or beef
  3. Prepare 1/3 cup chopped onion
  4. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  5. Make ready dash black pepper
  6. Take 1 can crushed tomatoes
  7. Make ready 1/2 cup water
  8. Take 1/2 cup uncooked long grain rice
  9. Make ready 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  10. Prepare 4 slices American cheese

Instructions to make Regular Stuffed Bell Peppers:
  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Cut off top off peppers and boil for 5 minutes
  3. Brown meat with onions.
  4. Add tomatoes, water and rice. Simmer until rice is tender.
  5. Add shredded cheese to mixture
  6. Stuff peppers and cook for 20 minutes.
  7. Top with slice of cheese if you want extra cheesy and continue to cook until melted
