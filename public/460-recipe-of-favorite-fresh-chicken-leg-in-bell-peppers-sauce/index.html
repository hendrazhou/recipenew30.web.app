Instructions to make Fresh chicken leg in bell peppers sauce:
  1. Season the chicken legs with 1/2 chicken cube and pinch of salt and cook for 15 minuets. Set aside
  2. Blend bell peppers with 1 chili and half of onion
  3. Heat oil in a sauce pan or wok
  4. Add onion to the oil
  5. Add chicken and add the blend mixture
  6. Season with salt and 1 chicken knorr cube
  7. Cover to cook for 7 minuets
  8. After 7 minuets check if season is needed
  9. If needed add more and continue to cook for another 2 minuets
  10. Done
