The ingredients needed to make Palak paneer:
  1. Make ready 1 kg palak
  2. Get 250 gms paneer
  3. Make ready 3 onions
  4. Take 5 tomatoes
  5. Get 1 tsp ginger finely cut
  6. Get 1 tsp garlic paste
  7. Take 1 cup milk
  8. Make ready 1 tsp cream
  9. Get 3 green chillies
  10. Take 1 tsp kitchen king
  11. Get to taste Salt
  12. Make ready as per taste red chilli powder

Steps to make Palak paneer:
  1. Wash palak and put it in a cooker. Give one pressure. Cool and churn it in grinder
  2. In a kadhai, add palak, milk, fresh cream, green chilies. Cover to cook on a slow flame for 15 mins.
  3. In other kadhai, add oil, ginger-garlic, onion, golden brown it. Add tomatoes and cook till it leaves oil.
  4. Add chilli powder, salt and kitchen king masala to it
  5. Add this tadka in palak. Add paneer pieces.
  6. Before serving heat 1 spoon ghee, add chilli powder and garnish palak with this tadka.
