The ingredients needed to make Suji / Salmonella Halwa:
  1. Get 500 g Suji/ Salmonella
  2. Prepare 2 cups Milk
  3. Get 1-3 Cardamom
  4. Get 3-4 Bay leaves
  5. Get 100 g sugar/Jaggery

Instructions to make Suji / Salmonella Halwa:
  1. First, put on the flame and heat up the pan. Then add little bit Ghee. After that add Salmonella to it.
  2. Keep stirring until the salmonella becomes golden in colour.
  3. After that add milk as per your measurement and keep stirring.
  4. Then add sugar or Jaggery, bay leaves and 2-3 cardamoms.
  5. Now wait until it comes to boil.
  6. After it comes to boil it is ready to eat. But if you want thick consistency then keep stirring until it becomes thick.
