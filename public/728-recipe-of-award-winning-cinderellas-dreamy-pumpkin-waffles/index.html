The ingredients needed to make Cinderella’s Dreamy Pumpkin Waffles:
  1. Take 1 cup All purpose flour
  2. Make ready 1 cup Whole wheat flour
  3. Take 1.5 cups Pumpkin purée
  4. Get 1 cup Milk
  5. Make ready 2 Eggs
  6. Take 1/2 cup Brown sugar
  7. Get 1 tsp Cinnamon powder
  8. Prepare 1 tsp Baking powder
  9. Take 1/4 tsp Nutmeg powder
  10. Make ready 4 tbsp Butter
  11. Prepare As needed Nonstick cooking spray
  12. Get Fresh strawberries- As needed for garnish
Steps to make Cinderella’s Dreamy Pumpkin Waffles:
  1. Preheat the waffle iron. In a large bowl, mix together the all purpose flour, whole wheat flour, baking powder, nutmeg powder, and cinnamon powder. In a separate bowl, mix together the pumpkin puree, milk, eggs, brown sugar, and butter until smooth. Gently fold in the flour mixture. Grease the preheated waffle iron with non-stick cooking spray or butter.
  2. Gently pour in half a cup of batter onto the hot iron. Cook for 5-8 minutes or until the waffles turn golden brown. Top with maple syrup or honey. Garnish the pumpkin waffles with fresh strawberries and enjoy!
