Steps to make Matar paneer:
  1. In a pan take 2 tbsp oil + cardamom seeds+ ginger garlic paste and sauté for a minute. Now add onion and sauté until the raw smell leaves. Now add tomato + cashews + turmeric and boil until it’s soft and mushy
  2. Now cool the mixture and blend the mixture
  3. Take 2 tbsp oil in the pan + bay leaf + cumin seeds + cinnamon stick + step 2 mixture. Add chilli powder + garam masala + coriander powder+jaggery and mix until the oil separates
  4. Now add capsicum + green peas + paneer + salt and let it boil for 10 minutes until the flavors are absorbed. You may want to adjust the thickness of gravy by adding water
  5. Now add curd + kasuri methi + 1 tsp cream and coriander leaves and give it a good mix. Serve it hot with roti or jeera rice
