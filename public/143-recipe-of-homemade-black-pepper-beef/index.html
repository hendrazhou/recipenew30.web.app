The ingredients needed to make Black Pepper Beef:
  1. Get 300 gr beef tenderloin, thinly sliced, marinade with 1 Tbsp oyster sauce and 1 tsp sweet soy sauce, mix well, set aside for at least 30 minutes
  2. Get 2 Tbsp vegetable oil
  3. Prepare 3 cloves garlic, minced
  4. Take 1/2 onion, thinly sliced
  5. Take 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  6. Get 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  7. Make ready 2 Tbsp sweat soy sauce
  8. Make ready 1 Tbsp oyster sauce
  9. Take 1/2 tsp salt
  10. Get 1/2 tsp sugar
  11. Make ready 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  12. Take 50 ml water
Steps to make Black Pepper Beef:
  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a pan over medium high heat. SautÃ© garlic and onion until fragrant.
  2. Add beef. Cook until it changes color. - Season with sweet soy sauce, oyster sauce, salt, freshly ground black pepper, and sugar. Mix well. Test the taste.
  3. Add red and green bell peppers.
  4. Add water. Keep stirring until the beef is cooked. Remove from the heat.
