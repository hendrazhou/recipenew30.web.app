The ingredients needed to make Paneer Pasanda:
  1. Make ready 200 gm Paneer
  2. Make ready 7-8 chopped cashew nuts
  3. Get 7-8 chopped Raisins
  4. Get 2-3 green chillies (chopped)
  5. Get 1 tsp cumin powder
  6. Prepare To taste Salt
  7. Get 1 tsp red chili powder
  8. Get 1 medium size onion
  9. Make ready 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  10. Make ready 1 medium size Tomato
  11. Make ready Dry spices
  12. Make ready 1 tbsp sugar
  13. Get 2-4 tbsp oil
  14. Get 1 tsp Fenugreek leaves
  15. Make ready 2 tbsp Cornflour
Steps to make Paneer Pasanda:
  1. Take 200 gm paneer and cut into triangle size.
  2. Cut the middle portion but not the whole,try to keep one side like this picture.
  3. Now take chopped cashew nuts, raisins, green chillies, grated paneer,1 tsp cumin powder,1 tsp red chili powder and salt as per taste.Mix together.
  4. Stuffing is ready and put into the paneer.
  5. Make the batter with cornflour and little water.
  6. Dip the stuffing paneer into the batter.
  7. Take a pan, heat the oil and fry it.
  8. Remove it from the pan.
  9. In a pan,fry the chopped onion and make a paste.
  10. In a pan,fry dry spices, chopped tomato, cashew nuts and make a paste.
  12. Take a Kardhai and put dry spices, onion paste and tomato cashew paste and add 1 tbsp sugar.
  13. Cook it for 5 mins and add water.sprinkle some fenugreek leaves.
  14. Paneer Pasanda is ready to serve.
  15. Now put the fried stuffing paneer and cover it for 10 mins.
