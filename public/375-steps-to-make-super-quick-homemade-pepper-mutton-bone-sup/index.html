The ingredients needed to make Pepper Mutton bone sup:
  1. Make ready 1 cup chopped small onion
  2. Make ready 1 pc tomato (big)
  3. Take leaves Coriander
  4. Take leaves Mint
  5. Take 2 pcs green chillies
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  7. Take 3 tbsp gingelly oil
  8. Prepare 1 ‚Äù cinnamon
  9. Prepare 4 pcs clove
  10. Make ready 2 pcs cardamom
  11. Make ready 1/2 tsp Pepper powder
  12. Get 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  13. Make ready 1 Pinch turmeric powder
  14. Prepare 400 gm mutton bone
  15. Prepare Rock salt as required
  16. Prepare Water as required
Steps to make Pepper Mutton bone sup:
  1. In a pressure cooker pour gingelly oil then add cinnamon,clove,cardamom wait till it pop.
  2. Now add chopped onion, salt and chilli saute well then add ginger garlic paste till the raw smell is gone and the colour changed into golden brown.
  3. Add the tomato wait till its become mashy then add coriander leaves, mint leaves, mutton bone, turmeric,cumin and pepper saute till oil break seperately.
  4. Add water and cover the lid and cook for 5-6 whistle then cook for 10mins in a low flame.
  5. Now switch off the flame and the healthy sup is ready to drink üç≤
