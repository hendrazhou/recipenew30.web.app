The ingredients needed to make Paneer potato lollipop:
  1. Get 3-4 medium sized Potatoes
  2. Get 100 gms paneer
  3. Prepare 1 bowl spring onions
  4. Get 1 tsp Ginger garlic paste
  5. Prepare 1 tsp red chilli powder
  6. Make ready 1 tsp Pani Puri masala
  7. Take 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  8. Prepare to taste Salt
  9. Prepare 1 tsp garam masala
  10. Prepare 1 bowl flattened rice flour
  11. Take As needed Ice cream sticks
  12. Take 2 tbsp All-purpose flour
  13. Prepare As needed Oil for frying
