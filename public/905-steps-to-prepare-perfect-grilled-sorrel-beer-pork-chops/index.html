The ingredients needed to make Grilled Sorrel Beer Pork Chops:
  1. Make ready 3 pounds (7) pork chops
  2. Get 1 cup sorrel beer
  3. Get 1/4 cup olive oil
  4. Take 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  5. Prepare 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  6. Get 1 teaspoon ginger powder
  7. Make ready 1 teaspoon cumin
  8. Get 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  9. Take 1 cup honey
  10. Take 1/2 cup sugar
  11. Get 1 teaspoon ground ginger
Steps to make Grilled Sorrel Beer Pork Chops:
  1. Using a blender, Make marinade with sorrel beer, olive oil, ginger powder, garlic powder, cumin and pepper flakes.
  2. Marinate pork in the fridge for 3-12 hours.
  3. Remove pork from fridge while grill is heating up (350-450F) to allow pork to reach ambient temperature.
  4. Sear both sides of pork on direct flame for 5 minutes on each side, then remove to indirect flame to continue cooking. Pork is done when internal temperature is 145F.
  5. Make glaze while pork is on indirect flame.
  6. Glaze pork chops before done while on indirect heat.
