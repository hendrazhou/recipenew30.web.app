The ingredients needed to make Grilled Balsamic Ginger Salmon:
  1. Prepare 1 Salmon Fillet
  2. Prepare 1/4 Cup Balsamic Vinegar
  3. Take 1/3 Can Ginger Ale
  4. Make ready 2 Cloves Garlic
  5. Make ready 3 Tbs Honey
  6. Make ready Ginger
  7. Take Pinch Oregano
  8. Prepare Salt
  9. Prepare Parsley
  10. Get Lemon
Instructions to make Grilled Balsamic Ginger Salmon:
  1. Lightly salt salmon. Allow to sit for 10 minutes. Pat moisture off of salmon with a paper towel. Cut ginger and garlic as pictured below. Add to a pot along with oregano, balsamic vinegar, honey and ginger ale.
  2. Start reducing the balsamic mixture immediately by about half.
  3. Place salmon on the grill, skin side down. Apply balsamic glaze to salmon. Allow to cook until skin becomes crispy. Flip. Apply glaze to opposite side. Cook until internal temp is about 145 F.
  4. Plate and serve. Simple as that.
