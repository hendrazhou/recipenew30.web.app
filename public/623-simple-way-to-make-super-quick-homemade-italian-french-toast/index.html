The ingredients needed to make Italian french toast:
  1. Make ready 4 eggs
  2. Get 8 slice bread

  5. Prepare 1/4 cup brown sugar
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp cinnamon
  7. Prepare 2 tbsp melted butter
