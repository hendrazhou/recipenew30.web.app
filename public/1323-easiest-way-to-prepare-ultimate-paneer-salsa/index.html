Instructions to make Paneer salsa:
  1. Take a pan out some butter or oil and roast cumin seeds
  2. After making brown add grated ginger
  3. Again roast for 1 minute and add tomatoes now add all the spices and green chilli
  4. Stir on low flame for 7 to 8 minutes and by covering and checking after 2 minutes make a masala
  5. In the end add diced Indian paneer
  6. Yummy mouthwatering paneer salsa is ready
