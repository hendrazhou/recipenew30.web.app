The ingredients needed to make Healthy: Coconut Flour Pancake:
  1. Take 2/3 cup coconut flour
  2. Prepare 3 caps of almond milk
  3. Get 2 eggs
  4. Make ready olive oil cooking spray

Instructions to make Healthy: Coconut Flour Pancake:
  1. Add coconut flour, almond milk and egg in a blender and blend the mix together; for the right consistency, you can add more almond milk
  2. Heat up frying pan and spray cooking spray; add a cooking spoon of batter into the pan and fry once its getting brown underneath flip through and remove once its done
  3. Do same for the remaining batter and serve; you can use oat flour or almond flour for the same purpose.
