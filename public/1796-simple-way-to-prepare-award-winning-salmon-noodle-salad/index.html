The ingredients needed to make Salmon Noodle Salad:
  1. Prepare 4 salmon fillets
  2. Take 250 g noodles
  3. Make ready 100 g carrots
  4. Prepare 100 g cucumber
  5. Make ready 100 g peas
  6. Get 50 g red pepper
  7. Get 4 spring onions
  8. Prepare 4 tbsp sweet chilli sauce
  9. Take 1/2 juice lime
  10. Get handful mint
  11. Get 1/2 red chilli
Instructions to make Salmon Noodle Salad:
  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C
  2. Wrap the salmon fillets in foil and place in the oven for 14 minutes
  3. Chop carrots, cucumber, red pepper and mint and mix in with peas
  4. Boil water and place noodles in the water for 4 minutes, then drain
  5. Mix the vegetables in with the noddles and place in a wok
  6. Add sweet chilli sauce, lime, chilli and cook on a high heat for 2 minutes
  7. Plate the noodles and vegetables and top with the salmon and sliced spring onion
