The ingredients needed to make Matar paneer:
  1. Make ready 200 gms Paneer
  2. Make ready 200 gms Peas
  3. Take 1 big Onion
  4. Prepare 2 big size Tomato
  5. Make ready as required Salt, coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chilli powder
  6. Get 2 spoons Oil
  7. Take 1 spoon cumin seeds
  8. Take 1/2 small spoon Garam masala
